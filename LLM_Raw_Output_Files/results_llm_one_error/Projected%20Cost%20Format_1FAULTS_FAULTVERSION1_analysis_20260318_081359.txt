Cell G21 — formula is =COUNT(G19:G20). Error: it counts numeric cells instead of totaling the dollar amounts for "Other Activities", so the Grand Cost Total and the One Year Total (B23) will be wrong.  
Solution: change G21 to =SUM(G19:G20).  
Why: COUNT returns how many numeric cells exist (e.g., 2) rather than the sum of the amounts; SUM adds the monetary values to give the correct total.
