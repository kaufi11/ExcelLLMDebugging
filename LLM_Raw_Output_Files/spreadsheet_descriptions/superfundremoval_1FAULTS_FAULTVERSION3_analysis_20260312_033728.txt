Sheet name and purpose

Layout (columns)
- A: Item / question number / section titles
- B: Question text or sublabels
- C: Ans. (Yes, No, N/A, Small Extent, Large Extent, etc.)
- D: Explanation / narrative
- E: Evidence / Data
- F: Weighting (decimal weights that sum to 1.00 per section)
- G: Weighted Score (calculated from Ans. × Weighting)
