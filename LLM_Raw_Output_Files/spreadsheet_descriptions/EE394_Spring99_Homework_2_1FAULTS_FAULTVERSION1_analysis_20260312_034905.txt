Notes and conventions

- Many angle calculations use ATAN2 with arguments ordered to return degrees via *180/PI().
- The sheet essentially implements standard transmission‑line equations to compute phasors of sending‑end voltage/current and line currents, reflection coefficient and VSWR as functions of distance from the load.
