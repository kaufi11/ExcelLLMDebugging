Error:
In the "Exams" sheet, cell G19 has the formula =SUM(E19:F19) instead of =SUM(D19:F19). This means Exam 1 score (D19) is not included in the total for Jennifer Mittelstadt.

Solution:
Change the formula in G19 from =SUM(E19:F19) to =SUM(D19:F19).

Explanation:
The total for exams should sum all three exam scores (Exam 1, Exam 2, Exam 3) in columns D, E, and F. The current formula excludes Exam 1 for row 19, causing an incorrect total. Correcting the formula ensures all exam scores are included in the total.
