**Error found:**

In Sheet 1 (Quarterly Statement (1)), row 12 (Business machines total), the formula in cell H12 is incorrect:
- H12 formula is `=SUM(H10:H11)` but it should sum H9:H11 like the other columns.

**Explanation:**
- For other quarters, the total of Business machines is calculated by summing rows 9 to 11 (Office imaging products, Computer peripherals, Business information products).
- In column H, the formula mistakenly sums only H10:H11, excluding H9, which causes the total to be incorrect.

---

**Solution:**

Change the formula in cell H12 from:
```
=SUM(H10:H11)
```
to:
```
=SUM(H9:H11)
```

---

**Why this works:**

By including H9 in the sum, the total will correctly add all three product lines for the Business machines category, consistent with other quarters. This fixes the inconsistency and ensures accurate totals.
