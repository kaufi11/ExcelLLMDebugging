Sheet name and purpose
- Sheet2 is a 2002 quarterly cumulative report of fuel sales (liters) by market category and province. The main title is in A1 and each quarter is labeled in row 4 (C4, H4, M4, R4, W4, AI4).

Overall layout and organization
- Column groups: the sheet repeats the same set of product columns for each quarter:
  - Q1 block: C-F = product volumes (Diesel, Illuminating paraffin, LPG, Petrol); G = Q1 TOTAL (SUM(C:F)).
  - Q2 block: H-K = product volumes for Q2; L = Q2 TOTAL (SUM(H:K)); M–P store quarter-to-date per-product sums (M = C+H etc); Q = Q2 CUMULATIVE TOTAL (G + L).
  - Q3 block: R-U = product volumes for Q3; V = Q3 TOTAL (SUM(R:U)); W–Z store running per-product cumulative sums (W = M + R etc); AA = Q3 CUMULATIVE TOTAL (Q + V).
  - Q4 block: AI–AL = product columns for Q4 (Diesel..Petrol). There is a secondary Q4 data block in AE:AH plus AB:AD (codes/category/province repeats); many category totals for Q4 are computed as AE + W, AF + X, etc (e.g., AI6 = AE6 + W6), i.e., Q4 cumulative + prior cumulative.
- Columns AB–AD and AC replicate category/province metadata in the Q4 area (codes in AB, names in AC, provinces in AD).

Rows and grouping
- Rows are grouped by market category. For each category (Construction, General Dealers, Government, Independent LPG Marketers, Local marine fishing, Mining, Public Passenger Bus Transport, General Commercial & Industrial, Retail - garages, Road Freight, Transnet, Agricultural Co‑ops & Farmers, Local Authorities) there are:
  - Per‑province detail rows (Province in column B).
  - A category total row labeled “... Total”.
- Final row 133 = Grand Total (aggregates all categories).

Key formulas and calculation patterns
- Per-quarter product totals: column G, L, V, (and L equivalents) use SUM across that quarter’s product columns (e.g., Gn = SUM(Cn:Fn), Ln = SUM(Hn:Kn), Vn = SUM(Rn:Un)).
- Per-product cumulative columns: M, N, O, P add Q1 + Q2 product volumes (e.g., Mn = Cn + Hn); W, X, Y, Z add cumulative up to Q3 (e.g., Wn = Mn + Rn).
- Quarter cumulative totals: Q = G + L (cumulative end Q2), AA = Q + V (cumulative end Q3).
- Category and grand totals: category total rows sum their province rows using SUM or by summing quarter blocks; Grand Total (row 133) sums across all category totals (e.g., AI133 = AI15+AI25+...+AI132; similarly for AJ, AK, AL).
- Many subtotal/totals are implemented by direct addition of corresponding quarter columns (consistent addition pattern).

Notable data/structure observations
- The sheet is highly regular and formula-driven with repeated patterns per block and category.
- Some anomalies exist: occasional negative values (e.g., J9 = -31,560; E60 = -1,496,201) and a few apparent formula/reference inconsistencies (example: O23 references E24+J23 — likely a typo).
- Q4 is implemented partly via an extra block (AE:AH) plus additions to prior cumulative columns (AI = AE + W etc) rather than the same pattern as earlier quarters.
