Summary — Sheet: "Syracuse, NY"
- Purpose: Market-share table of depositors in the Syracuse, NY market (banks and thrifts). Computes weighted deposits, market-share (%) and a Herfindahl‑Hirschman Index (HHI) contribution per institution and totals.

Layout / contents
- Title area: B1 = "MARKET SHARE TABLE", B2 = "Syracuse, NY *", B4–B5 include scope and date text (Total Bank and 50% Thrift Deposits; (January 30, 2004) and deposit-data footnote).
- Columns:
  - A: Rank/serial (A10:A33).
  - B: Institution name (B10:B33).
  - C: Type (Bank or Thrift) (C10:C33).
  - D: Offices (count) (D10:D33). D36 = SUM(D10:D33).
  - E: Deposits ($000s) (E10:E33). E36 = SUM(E10:E33).
  - F: Weighted Deposits (F10:F33) — formula multiplies E by a weight (1 for banks, 0.5 for thrifts). F36 = SUM(F10:F33) (this is the market denominator).
  - G: Market Share (%) (G10:G33) = F(row) / $F$36 * 100. G36 = SUM(G10:G33) (should total ~100%).
  - H: HHI contribution (H10:H33) = (G(row))^2. H36 = SUM(H10:H33) (sum of squared shares as entered).
- Notes area: B39:B47 contains market definition notes and data-date footnotes (includes counties/townships and dates: deposit data as of June 30, 2002; deposit ownership as of Jan 30, 2004).

Key formulas (examples)
- F10 (bank): =1 * $E10 (applies weight 1 to full deposits).
- F18 (thrift): =0.5 * $E18 (applies half-weight for thrifts).
- G10: =$F10/$F$36*100 (market share as percentage).
- H10: =$G10^2 (squares the percent to form the HHI contribution).
- Totals row (row 36): D36 =SUM($D$10:$D$33), E36 =SUM($E$10:$E$33), F36 =SUM($F$10:$F$33), G36 =SUM($G$10:$G$33), H36 =SUM($H$10:$H$33).

Notable items / possible issues
- F29 uses an unusual multiplier: F29 =891*$E29 — likely a data-entry error (expected 1 or 0.5).
- The HHI is computed by squaring the percent value (e.g., 25%^2 = 625). That yields an HHI on the percent-squared scale (common HHI convention uses market shares as decimals and squares them, yielding a value between 0 and 1, or multiplied by 10,000 if using decimals; this sheet’s H36 will be the percent-squared sum — interpret accordingly).
- Many F formulas are written as "=1 * $E.." which is redundant but functionally fine.

Overall: A single well-structured market-share table listing 24 institutions, using weights to adjust thrift deposits, computing market shares (G column) relative to total weighted deposits (F36) and HHI contributions (H column). Footnotes define the geographic market and data dates.
