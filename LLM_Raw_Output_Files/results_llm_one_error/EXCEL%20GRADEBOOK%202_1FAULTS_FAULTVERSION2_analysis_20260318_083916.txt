Q5 — Error: formula is =(SUM(J5:O5)+MIN(J5:O5))/(COUNT(J5:O5)+1).  
Fix: change to =(SUM(J5:O5)-MIN(J5:O5))/(COUNT(J5:O5)-1).  
Why: All other classwork averages drop the lowest score; Q5’s formula mistakenly adds the minimum and increases the count, giving an incorrect average. The corrected formula sums scores, removes the lowest, then divides by the remaining count.
