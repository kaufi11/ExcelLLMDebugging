1) Seq Production Costs sheet — Other Activities grand total uses COUNT instead of SUM
- Problem cell: G21 = COUNT(G19:G20)  
- Why it’s wrong: COUNT returns the number of numeric cells (1 or 2), not the monetary total. This makes G21 and the One Year Total (B23) wrong.
- Fix: replace with a sum:

- Effect: G21 will show the dollar total for “Other Activities” and B23 = B21 + D21 + G21 will then be correct.

2) Seq Production Costs sheet — hard-coded “1”s for percent totals and inconsistent approach
- Problem cells: C21, E21, H21 are just “1” (hard-coded). That is inconsistent and fragile (and not tolerant of zero totals).
- Better approach / Fix (use formula that avoids #DIV/0):

  - H21 → =IF($G$21=0,0,$G$21/$G$21)
- Explanation: this keeps the “100%” behavior but avoids hard-coding and prevents errors if a category total is zero.
