2) Section weighting checks may spuriously show "ERROR" because of rounding
- Error: IF(SUM(Frange)<>100%,"ERROR","100%") will flag an error if the decimal weights (e.g., 0.1428 / 0.143) do not sum to exactly 1.000000 due to floating/rounding precision.
- Fix: Use a rounded or tolerance comparison, e.g.:
  =IF(ROUND(SUM(F16:F22),4)<>1,"ERROR","100%")
  or
  =IF(ABS(SUM(F16:F22)-1)>0.0001,"ERROR","100%")
- Why: Rounding/tolerance avoids false errors when the displayed decimals don't exactly sum to 1 internally.
