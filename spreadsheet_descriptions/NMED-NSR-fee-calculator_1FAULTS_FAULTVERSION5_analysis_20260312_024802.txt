Overview
- Workbook contains two sheets: "Calculations" (main fee calculator) and "Definitions" (sheet exists but contents not provided).
- Purpose: compute a permit fee based on a points system (points × fee-per-point), with adjustments for small business and special-case “other actions.”

Layout (Calculations sheet)
- Top area: title (A1–A2), permit/site label (A4), Fee $/point label in B5 and fee value in C5 = 315. Total Fee displayed at F5 via FORMULA =IF(C43="N/A",C42,C43).
- Column A: descriptions of permitting actions, instructions and section headings.
- Column B: user inputs (e.g., # units, X flags for presence, small-business mark).
- Column C: calculated points for each action (formulas shown below).
- Columns E–G: list of non‑applicable regulation identifiers/examples (NMAC parts, NSPS, NESHAP references) used for reference.

Key point formulas and rules
- Emission Units (C9): =IF(B9<>"",IF(B9<5,5,IF(B9>15,15,B9)),0)
  - If B9 is entered, points = number of units bounded to a minimum 5 and maximum 15; otherwise 0.
- Fugitive Emissions (C11): =IF(B11<>"",5,0) — 5 points if indicated.
- Non-attainment Area (C12): =IF(B12<>"",75,0) — 75 points if indicated.
- Modeling Review (C13): =IF(B13<>"",15,0) — 15 points if indicated.
- Air Toxics / Levels:
  - Level 1 (C16): =IF(B16<>"",8,0)
  - Level II (C17 not shown as formula) — listed as label only.
- BACT (C18): =IF(B18<>"",60,0)
- Health Assessment (C19): =IF(B19<>"",100,0)
- Applicable Regulations:
  - 20 NMAC 2.X (C22): =IF(B22<>"",(3*B22),0) — 3 points per listed NMAC reg.
  - NSPS (C23): =IF(B23<>"",(5*B23),0) — 5 points per NSPS.
  - NESHAPs (C24): =IF(B24<>"",(5*B24),0) — 5 points per NESHAP.
- Case-by-case MACT (C27): =IF(B27<>"",100,0)
- PSD options:
  - PSD Netting only (C30): =IF(B31="",(IF(B30<>"",20,0)),"") — 20 points if B30 set and B31 blank.
  - PSD review including netting (C31): =IF(B31<>"",75,0)
- Other Actions:
  - General Permit (C34): =7 (flat 7 points)
  - Streamlined (# of Sites) (C35): =IF(B35<>"",(B35*10),0) — 10 points per site
  - TOTAL OTHER ACTIONS (C36): =SUM(C34:C35)
- Total Points (C41): =IF(OR(C34>0,C35>0),C36,SUM(C9:C31))
  - If any "other actions" present, total points = C36; otherwise sum points for individual actions C9:C31.
- Fee calculations:
  - Fee (C42): =C41*C5 (points × $315)
  - Half fee for small business (C43): =IF(B34<>"","N/A",IF(B38<>"",C42/2,"N/A"))
    - Logic: if General Permit flag (B34) present → half-fee is "N/A"; else if small-business box (B38) marked → half fee = C42/2; otherwise "N/A".
  - Final Total Fee (F5): =IF(C43="N/A",C42,C43) — displays half-fee when applicable, otherwise full fee.
