2002 Report!C18 — Error: formula uses =G18*1000000 (should be =G18/1000000).

Solution: edit C18 to =G18/1000000 and press Enter (then recalc). This converts pounds to millions like the other rows.

Why: every other C-row divides the pounds by 1,000,000 to show "Millions of Pounds." Multiplying by 1,000,000 massively inflates C18 and any sums/totals that depend on it, producing wrong totals downstream.
