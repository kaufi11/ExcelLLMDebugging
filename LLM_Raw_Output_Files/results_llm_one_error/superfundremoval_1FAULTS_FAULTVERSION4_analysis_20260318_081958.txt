G21: Formula typo — uses 704 instead of 1.

Problem: G21 currently =IF(C21="yes",(704*F21),IF(C21="no",(0*F21),"")). That multiplies the weight by 704 (≈100.672), blowing up the weighted score and making section totals incorrect.

Fix: Replace with =IF(C21="yes",F21,IF(C21="no",0,"")) or =IF(C21="yes",1*F21,IF(C21="no",0*F21,""))

Explanation: The weighted score should be 1×weight when answer = Yes (or 0×weight for No). Changing 704 to 1 returns the correct weighted contribution and restores correct section totals.
