1) Wrong % variance formula for "Total Funds" (annex f, cell K68)

- Why: This computes % variance = (Revised − Original) / Original for Total Funds and avoids incorrectly showing the Net Assets % for a different total.

- Why: unspent bursaries/hardship funds are cash held (but shown also as a creditor). To reconcile cashflow closing balance to balance sheet cash you must include those funds.
