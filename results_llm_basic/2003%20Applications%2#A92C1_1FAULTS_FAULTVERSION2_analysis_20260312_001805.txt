4) Quick quality check (possible formula-as-text)
- Problem/Risk: Several cells are shown as “FORMULA =E2” etc. If any of these were entered as text (prefixed with an apostrophe or typed literally), they won’t calculate and will be excluded from sums.
- Check/fix: Click any suspicious cell and verify the formula in the formula bar. If you see an apostrophe or the formula is plain text, re-enter it as a real formula (e.g., =E2).
- Why: A formula typed as text looks like a formula but Excel treats it as text and it won’t contribute to totals.
