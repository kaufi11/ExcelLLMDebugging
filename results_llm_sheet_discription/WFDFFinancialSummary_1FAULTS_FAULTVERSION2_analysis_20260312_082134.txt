Summary of errors found (short) and how to fix them

1) Stork - 2002 Expenditures: Receipts / Expenditures references are swapped
- Problem: At the bottom of the Expenditures sheet you have:
  - D59 (labelled "Receipts") = C55 (which is the expenditures total)
  - D60 (labelled "Expenditures") = 'Stork - 2002 Receipts'!C43 (which is the receipts total)
  - D61 = D59 - D60 therefore computes Expenditures - Receipts (wrong sign / wrong numbers).
- Effect: The “Net as of 7/3/03” is reversed and wrong.
- Fix: Swap the two formulas so they match the labels:
  - Set D59 = 'Stork - 2002 Receipts'!C43  (total receipts)
  - Set D60 = C55  (total expenditures)
  - Keep D61 = D59 - D60 (now correct = receipts − expenditures)
- Why this works: D59 will now hold the receipts total and D60 the expenditures total, so the net (D61) will be receipts minus expenditures as intended.
