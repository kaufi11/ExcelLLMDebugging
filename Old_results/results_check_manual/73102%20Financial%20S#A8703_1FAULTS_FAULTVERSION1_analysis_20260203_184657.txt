1) Hard-coded grand totals (big cause of confusion)
- Problem: Cell H285 ("ASSETS =") and H286 ("LIABILITIES/CAPITAL") contain hard-coded numbers (3855164.52). That hard-coded value can differ from the worksheet calculations (D178 and D283), so balance checks can be wrong.
- Fix: Replace H285 with =D178 and H286 with =D283 (or point both to whichever single, authoritative total you use). This makes the displayed totals always come from the calculated totals.
- Why: Hard-coded numbers don't update when underlying data changes. Linking eliminates stale values and shows the true difference.

- Problem: D52 (Unrestricted) = -D273 + D275 + D277 subtracts the permanently restricted fund (D273) and then D54 = D273 adds it back. That cancels out, but it's confusing and risks mistakes.
