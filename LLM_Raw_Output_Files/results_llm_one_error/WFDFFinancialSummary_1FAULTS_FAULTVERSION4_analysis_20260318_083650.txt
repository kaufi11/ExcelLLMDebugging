I15 — formula uses COUNT instead of SUM.

Error: I15 =COUNT(I6:I13) returns a count of entries (a small integer) instead of the total income for 2001, so Net Income (I30), Ending Balance (I33) and all linked Balance Sheet items are wrong.

Fix: change to =SUM(I6:I13).

Why: COUNT counts how many numeric cells exist; SUM adds their values. Using COUNT makes the year’s income far too small and misstates net income and balances.
