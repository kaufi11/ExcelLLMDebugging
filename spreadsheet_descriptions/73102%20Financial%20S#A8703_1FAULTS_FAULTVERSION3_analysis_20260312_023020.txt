Structure and flow
- Header (B1:B3) — title and dates.
- Assets section (A8:A38): current assets (cash, investments, receivables, inventories, prepaid, other current assets), restricted cash, property & equipment (gross), accumulated depreciation, net PPE, other assets, and Total Assets.
  - Each top-line asset cell (e.g., D9 Cash and cash equivalents, D10 Short-term investments, D11 Accounts receivable, etc.) is a formula that aggregates detailed GL lines shown lower on the sheet (e.g., D9 = D83 where D83 is SUM of cash account rows; D11 is a long sum of specific A/R rows).
  - Detailed GL lines for cash, investments, receivables, inventory, prepaid, deposits, suspense accounts, property, accumulated depreciation and other assets are listed in rows ~74–175 (each with an account code in column G/H).
- Liabilities and Net Assets (A42:A59, A51:A59): current liabilities (accounts payable, accrued liabilities, due to Foundation, bid bonds, deferred revenues), total liabilities, then net assets broken down by Unrestricted, Temporarily restricted, Permanently restricted, appropriated/unappropriated, net income, and Total Net Assets.
  - Payables and accruals similarly roll up from detail rows (e.g., D43 = SUM of many D18x rows; D49 = SUM(D42:D47)).
- Capital / Fund balance (A265:A283): capital account detail and totals. Net income (D278) flows into unappropriated surplus and total capital.
- Balancing checks:
  - D178 = Total Assets (computed from detailed subtotals).
  - D283 = Total Liabilities & Capital.
  - H289 = D178; H290 = -D283; H291 = SUM(H289:H290) — intended to verify Assets = Liabilities + Capital (difference should be zero). Also H285/H286 show ASSETS and LIABILITIES/CAPITAL totals and H287 shows their difference.

Key formulas & mechanics
- Most top-level totals are SUM ranges of the detailed GL lines (examples: D83 = SUM(D74:D82) for cash; D96 = SUM(D86:D95) for investments; D114 = SUM(D99:D113) for receivables).
- Net and reconciliation rows reference those subtotals: D18 = SUM(D8:D17) (total current assets), D38 = D18 + D22 + D34 + D36 (total assets), D49 = SUM(D42:D47), D57 = SUM(D51:D56), D59 = D49 + D57, etc.
- Many detail rows contain direct numeric values (positive/negative) and feed the sums; account mapping (G/H) documents GL codes.
