B19 — formula typo: =A19/873 should be =A19/2.

Why it matters: that wrong divisor gives a much smaller f(n) at n=8 and makes the Big-Oh/Big‑Omega boolean checks incorrect for that row (and any summaries that use it).

Fix: edit B19 to =A19/2 (or copy the correct B10–B18 pattern down). After fixing, Excel will recalc the derived columns (D, E, F, G, H) correctly.
