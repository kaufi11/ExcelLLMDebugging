1) Concrete error (wrong function)
- Cell O19 contains:
  =COUNT(D19-M19)
  Value in the file: 1

  Why it's wrong
  - COUNT() counts numeric items passed as arguments. COUNT(D19-M19) evaluates the subtraction first (gives 0) and then COUNT returns 1 (because there is one numeric argument). That is not the intended arithmetic difference. The expected result is D19 - M19 = 0, not 1.

  Fix
  - Replace with either:
    =D19-M19
    or
    =SUM(D19-M19)
  Either will give the correct numeric difference (0). I recommend =D19-M19.
