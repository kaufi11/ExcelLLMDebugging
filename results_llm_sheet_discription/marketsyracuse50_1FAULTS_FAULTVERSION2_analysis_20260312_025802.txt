- F29 is wrong: it reads =891*$E29. That’s a typo — Savannah Bank (C29 = Bank) should be weighted 1, so F29 should be =1*$E29 (or simply =$E29). This single error will massively inflate F36 (total weighted deposits), making every market share (G) and HHI (H) incorrect.
- Possible confusion (not a calculation bug but important): G is calculated as =$Frow/$F$36*100 (so G holds numeric percent values like 12.34), and H is =G^2 (so H is percent-squared on the 0–10,000 scale). That is acceptable if you want HHI in the 0–10,000 convention, but be aware of the unit.

Quick fix
1. Replace F29 with =1*$E29 (or = $E29).
2. Recalculate (Excel will auto-update). G36 should return ≈100 and H36 will be the correct HHI (percent-squared sum).

Safer, robust solution (prevents future typos)

  This automatically applies 0.5 for thrifts and 1 for banks, avoiding manual multipliers.
