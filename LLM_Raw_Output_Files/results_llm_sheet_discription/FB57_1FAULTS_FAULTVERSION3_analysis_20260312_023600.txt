3) Potential division-by-zero or #DIV/0 in per‑recipient sheets
- Issue: per‑recipient formulas divide funding*1000 by recipients. If a recipient cell is blank or zero, this returns #DIV/0 or an incorrect result.
- Why it matters: causes errors and breaks downstream percent‑change formulas.
- Fix: wrap the formula with a safe test or IFERROR. Example pattern:
  =IF('Program'!RecipientCell>0, ('Program'!FundingCell*1000)/'Program'!RecipientCell, "")
  or shorter:
  =IFERROR(('Program'!FundingCell*1000)/'Program'!RecipientCell, "")
- Apply that to all per‑recipient formulas (all three per‑recipient sheets).
