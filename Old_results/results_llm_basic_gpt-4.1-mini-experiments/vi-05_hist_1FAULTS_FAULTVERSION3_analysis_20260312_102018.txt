Error found:
In the "Spring 2003 per credit hour" sheet, cell H14 has the formula =COUNT(H10:H13) instead of =SUM(H10:H13).

Explanation:
- COUNT counts the number of numeric cells, returning 4 (since all 4 cells have numbers).
- SUM adds the values, which is the intended operation for subtotal.

Solution:
Change the formula in cell H14 from =COUNT(H10:H13) to =SUM(H10:H13).

This will correctly calculate the subtotal of State Components for Veterinary courses in Spring 2003.
