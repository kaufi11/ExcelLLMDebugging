Sheet "pricing" — Plan scenario with decision variables and cost sheet
- Top table (A2:K10) lists decision variables per period:

  - Period rows 1–6 show numeric decisions (e.g., D5..D10 ≈ 64.5833 workforce; I5..I10 ≈ 2583.33 production).
- Constraint columns (M:P) are formulas enforcing feasibility:
  - M_t = D_t - D_{t-1} - H_t + L_t  (workforce-change constraint)
  - N_t = 40*D_t + (E_t/4) - I_t    (production capacity: 40 regular hours*workers + overtime/4 minus production)
  - O_t = F_{t-1} - G_{t-1} + I_t + H_t - J_t - F_t + G_t  (inventory balance)
  - P_t = -E_t + 10*D_t             (overtime hours <= 10 hours per worker)
- Aggregate Plan Costs (A14:I20): per-period cost lines referencing Sheet1 parameters:
  - Hiring cost = B19 (300) * #Hired
  - Layoff cost = B20 (500) * #Laid off
  - Regular-time cost = D_t * B22 * 8 * 20  (workforce * regular cost/hour * 8 hours/day * 20 workdays)
  - Overtime cost = E_t * B23
  - Inventory cost = F_t * B17
  - Stockout cost = G_t * B18
  - Subcontract cost = H_t * B24
  - Material cost = I_t * B16
- Totals:
  - Total Cost C22 = SUM(B15:I20)
  - Total Revenue C24 = SUMPRODUCT(J5:J10, K5:K10)  (demand * price)
  - Profit C25 = C24 - C22
