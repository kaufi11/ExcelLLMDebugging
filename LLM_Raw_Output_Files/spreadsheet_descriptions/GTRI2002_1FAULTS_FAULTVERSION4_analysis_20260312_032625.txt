Summary: workbook tracks Toxic Release Inventory (TRI) emissions for Silicon Valley across multiple reports/years with per‑year breakdowns by media (Air, Land, Water), on‑site releases/disposal, and offsite transfers (recycled, energy recovery, other treatment, incineration). Sheets mix raw pound counts and converted "Millions of Pounds" cells; many cells are computed by simple SUMs or by dividing raw pounds by 1,000,000.

Sheet-by-sheet (concise)

- Sheet "2003 Report"
  - Header A3: "TRI - In Millions of Pounds".
  - Columns: A = Year; B = Air (millions); C = Land; D = Water; E = Offsite Release or Disposal; F = Total Releases (F = B+C+D+E).
  - Offsite transfer details in columns J (year) and K–N: K = Recycled, L = Energy Recovery, M = Other Treatment, N = Incineration; O = total offsite transfers (O = K+L+M+N).
  - Years present: 1987–2000. Example values: 1987 total releases F7 = 13.239865; 1999 offsite transfers O19 = 26.282264 (note offsite transfers are reported separately from releases).

- Sheet "2002 Report"
  - Title and notes (A1:A7); primary table converts raw pounds to millions.
  - Columns F,G,H contain raw pounds for Water Pollution Treatment Plants, Air and Land respectively; B,C,D are the same converted to millions via formulas like =F11/1000000, =G11/1000000, =H11/1000000.
  - E = SUM(C:D) (air & land total in millions). R column contains total million lbs (R = B+C+D).
  - Year progression often done with A# = previous year + 1.
  - Additional blocks track disposed/recycled/incinerated offsite (many NA entries for early years), and a JV vs SVEP zip-code breakdown (rows 43–71) where L = SUM(B:K)/1,000,000 and differences N,O compute how much JV data differs from SVEP (N = L - M; O = N/L).
  - Notable formulas: many =value/1000000 and SUM ranges. R24 calculates a percent change: =(R11-R23)/R11.

- Sheet "data"
  - Simple table of incinerated (B), recycled (C) and total recycled+incinerated (E).
  - C formulas generally =E# - B# (recycled = total - incinerated).
  - Years present: 1991–1999, totals in E are populated; 1997–1999 entries appear also in "notes".

- Sheet "notes"
  - Repeats incinerated and recycled totals for 1997–1999 (for quick reference).

- Sheet "1999 Report"
  - Similar structure to "2002 Report": raw pounds in F–H converted to millions in B–D via /1,000,000; years listed 1987 onward.
  - Block for disposed vs recycled/incinerated offsite with F/G raw pounds then B/C converted to millions via /1,000,000.

Key formulas & patterns
- Conversion pounds → millions: =RawPoundsCell / 1,000,000 (repeated across 1999/2002 sheets).
- Year increment: =PreviousYearCell + 1.
- Totals: F (Total Releases) = SUM(B:E) on 2003 Report; Offsite total O = SUM(K:N).
- Recycled (data/notes): C = E - B (recycled = total recycled+incinerated minus incinerated).

Notable values / observations
- Offsite transfers can exceed on‑site releases (e.g., 1999 O19 ≈ 26.28 million vs total releases F19 ≈ 4.256 million on 2003 Report).
- Some cells contain NA (offsite disposal blocks) or raw pound totals (F,G,H) that are consistently converted to million‑pound columns.
- Many consistent SUM and simple arithmetic formulas — no complex lookups or array formulas visible.
