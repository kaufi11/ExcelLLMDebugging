Summary of workbook (concise)

Sheet: "Tution and Fees"
- Purpose/layout: Tuition, fees, room & board by academic year and detailed fee schedule by credit-hour bands. Main annual-year columns run across F:I (F = 2003–2004*, G = 2002–2003, H = 2001–2002, I = 2000–2001).
- Top block (rows 5–12): In-state/out-of-state tuition, required fees, room & board components and comprehensive totals.
  - Key values (2003–2004, column F): In‑State Tuition 1,168; Out‑of‑State Tuition 9,039; Required Fees 1,168; Room & Board on‑campus 4,892; In‑state comprehensive (F11) 7,228.
  - Formulas: F12 = F6+F7+F8 (Out‑of‑State comprehensive); G11 = G5+G8+G7; other year columns use SUM(G6,G8,G7) or similar.
  - Room components: Room (row 9) + Board (row 10) used to compute Room & Board (row 8); e.g., G8 = G9+G10.
  - Note: H8 has "FORMULA =4" which looks anomalous (probably an error).
- Fee schedule (rows 16–30, columns D:K): Breaks required fees and tuition by credit-hour bands for In‑State and Out‑of‑State (2003–2004 in D–G; 2002–2003 in H–K).
  - Tuition per band (In‑State D19:G19): 146 (1–5 hrs), 292 (6–8), 438 (9–11), 584 (12+).
  - Required fees broken down by fee type (health, student activities, athletic, etc.) with numeric entries per band.
  - Totals: Total NC Resident (row 30) uses SUM of D19:D29, etc. Out‑of‑state fees (row 36) mostly SUM(D21:D29) or explicit values, then totals (row 37) sum tuition + fees.
- Boarding fees (rows 39–43): Room and Board by housing type (Traditional, Suites, Apartments) for two years and totals via SUM formulas (e.g., E43 = SUM(E41:E42)).
- Misc: Page number/footer cells (e.g., G64 "Page 38") and additional comparison cells (rows 67–76) showing fall tuition comparisons across years.

Sheet: "Financial Aid"
- Purpose/layout: Need‑based and non‑need based aid statistics (Fall 2002) and summary metrics for cohorts (columns F–H used for First‑time/Full‑time/Part‑time and Undergraduate labels).
- Need‑based aid table (rows 5–17):
  - Key counts (Full‑time column F): # degree‑seeking 664; applied 581; determined to have need 564; received aid 412.
  - Key averages (F14–F17): Avg financial aid package 3,462; Avg need‑based gift 2,381; Avg self‑help award 1,260; Avg need‑based loan 2,379.
  - Avg percent of need met (F13) = 0.81 (81%).
- Non‑need based awards (rows 21–25): counts & averages for recipients who had no financial need; e.g., F22 (# non‑need gift recipients) = 35, avg gift F23 = 2,125.
- Loans & indebtedness (rows 27–31): Borrowing rates and average per‑borrower cumulative indebtedness (2002: 82% borrowed, avg $10,600).
- Aid awarded to In‑State vs Out‑of‑State undergraduates (rows 33–47): Percent receiving and average package by category (first‑time and all degree‑seeking). Example: First‑time in‑state need‑based % = 0.75, avg pkg = 3,210; out‑of‑state 0.68 / 4,211.

Sheet: "Financial Aid 2"
- Purpose/layout: Detailed dollar flows — grants, scholarships, guaranteed loans, other loan/work programs, and specific scholarship programs. Data shown for 2002 and 2000 in most blocks.
- Grants & scholarships (rows 3–13):
  - Pell Grants (B–D): 2002 recipients 1,179; total $5,129,088; avg per recipient $4,381.
  - SEOG and Student Incentive also shown (recipients, totals, average).
  - Athletic & Other Grants: e.g., Athletic grants 2002 recipients 81, total $295,243; Other grants total (F12) $2,334,063.
  - Total grants provided (I12) = $8,336,461 (2002).
- Guaranteed loan programs (rows 15–20): FFELP subsidized/unsubsidized/PLUS with counts and totals for 2002 (e.g., FFELP subsidized recipients 1,684; total $5,557,678).
- Other loan & work programs (rows 22–27): FWS (work study), Perkins, institutional loans, other loans, plus totals:
  - 2002 FWS: recipients 238, total $326,627.
  - Totals (row 26): K26 (Guaranteed Loans) = $8,426,281; L26 (Total Loans) = $9,438,041.
- Scholarships/Loans by program (rotc, NC Teaching Fellows, Nurse programs) with recipients, totals and averages (e.g., ROTC 2002: 9 recipients, $42,048 total, avg $4,672).
- Footer/page markers (G41 "Page 40").

General notes and formulas
- Repeated use of SUM and direct additions to compute composites (e.g., tuition + fees + room/board).
- Many totals are computed per column/year using SUM(range) patterns (e.g., row 30, row 36, row 37, row 43).
- Some anomalies / typos observed: sheet name "Tution and Fees" (missing 'i'), row 13 note has typos, H8 contains "FORMULA =4" which is likely incorrect given pattern; verify formula cells for consistency before use.
- Overall layout: Each sheet uses clear blocks (header row(s) then numeric rows), with years across columns and categories down rows. Key summary totals are provided at the tops and bottoms of blocks for quick reference.
