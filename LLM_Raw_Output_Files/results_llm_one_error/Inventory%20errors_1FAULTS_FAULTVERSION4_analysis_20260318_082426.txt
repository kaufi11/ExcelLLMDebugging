Sheet2!B33 — Error: Retained earnings only = C15 (this year’s Net Income) and does NOT include the prior‑year inventory correction (+17,306).  
Why it matters: you increased ending inventory (Assets) but didn’t increase Equity, so Assets ≠ Liabilities + Equity.  
Fix: adjust retained earnings to include the prior‑year correction. Example formula: =C15+17306 (or better: =C15 + (B11 - 179800) if 179,800 is the originally reported ending inventory).  
Explanation: correcting a prior‑year inventory error increases beginning retained earnings (not current period income). Adding the correction to retained earnings restores the accounting equation and balances the balance sheet.
