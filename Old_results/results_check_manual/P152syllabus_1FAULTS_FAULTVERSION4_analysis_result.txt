I reviewed the workbook formulas (mostly the schedule sheet). Found one definite formula bug, a few inconsistencies that could be either intentional or mistakes, and some minor data/format oddities you may want to clean up.

Summary of problems and recommendations

1) Obvious formula bug (wrong operator)
- Cell schedule!C19 formula: = $C$7 + 7/(A19-1)
  - Problem: uses division (/) instead of multiplication (*). That adds a fractional number of days (7 ÷ (4−1) = 7/3 ≈ 2.333 days) producing a time of day (08:00) rather than a whole-day jump.
  - Result: schedule!C19 shows 2003-01-15T08:00:00 (unexpected).
  - Fix: change to = $C$7 + 7*(A19-1) so the date increments by whole weeks like the other rows.

2) Inconsistent year across sheets
- schedule!D1 = 2003 but the Overview and homework sheets indicate SPRING 2004 (Overview B1, homework C5).
  - Either the schedule dates (the C: dates) are for 2003 intentionally, or the header year is wrong. Confirm and correct the year so all sheets are consistent.

3) Sudden "-1" adjustment starting at week 8 (possible intentional, please confirm)

  and for Thursdays:
    =$C$9 + 7*(A37-1) - 1

5) Blank strings " " present in some date/text cells
