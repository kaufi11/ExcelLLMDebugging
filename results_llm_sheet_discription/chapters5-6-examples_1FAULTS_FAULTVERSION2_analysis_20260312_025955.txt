  e.g. B15 -> =Sheet1!$B$19 * B5, B16 -> =Sheet1!$B$19 * B6, etc.

- Why it’s wrong: 31 is unexplained and inconsistent; revenue should be sum of (demand × unit price).
- Fix (pick one, depending on your model):

    =SUMPRODUCT(J5:J10, K5:K10)
  - If price is a single constant (e.g. 40 per unit), use:

  - Or, better, put the unit price in a cell and reference it (easier to change).
- Effect: revenue (and profit if computed) will reflect the correct unit price.
