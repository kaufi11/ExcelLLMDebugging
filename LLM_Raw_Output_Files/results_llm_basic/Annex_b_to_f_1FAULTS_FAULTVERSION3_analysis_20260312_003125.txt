2) Monthly totals in Cashflow (Original forecast) only use the last month instead of summing months
- Where: Table 3.0 (Operating Cash Flow — ORIGINAL CASH FORECAST)
  - T22 currently =S22 (should be the Total for row 22)
  - T23 currently =S23 (should be the Total for row 23)
- Problem: T22 and T23 only copy July's month (S column). That means the column T "Total" is wrong for those rows and any dependent totals (T24 etc.) are wrong.
- Fix: replace:
  - T22 with =SUM(H22:S22)
  - T23 with =SUM(H23:S23)
  - (T24 can remain =SUM(T22:T23) or you can set T24 = SUM(H22:S23) — both OK once T22/T23 are correct)
- Why: T (Total) column must sum all months H:S, otherwise reconciling totals and the “These cells should be zero” checks will fail.
