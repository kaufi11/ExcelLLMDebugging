How to find and prevent similar errors quickly
- Use Trace Precedents / Trace Dependents to find where an incorrect cell feeds totals.
- Use Go To Special → Constants to spot hard‑coded numbers in ranges that should be formulas.
- Conditional format negative numbers to highlight suspicious negative entries.
- Use IFERROR or N() in cumulative formulas to avoid #VALUE errors when inputs are blank.
