Error: Formula =SUM(C4:C6) returns total indirect costs instead of an hourly indirect cost rate.

Fix: Change to =SUM(C4:C6)/C2

Why: The indirect cost hourly rate must be total indirect costs (office rent + support salaries + utilities) divided by total direct labor hours. The wrong value inflates C12, C14, C16 and C17, producing wildly incorrect job cost and bid amounts.
