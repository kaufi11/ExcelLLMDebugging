Overview
- Two-sheet workbook: "STEP 1 OF 2" (Personal Financial Profile: inputs, assets, giving, savings, debts, subtotal calculations) and "STEP 2 OF 2" (Monthly Financial Plan / Budget built from STEP 1 totals).

STEP 1 OF 2 — structure & key cells
- Header/labels in top rows; main sections stacked vertically: WHAT I EARN, WHAT I OWN, WHAT I GIVE, WHAT I SAVE, WHAT I OWE (debt).
- Earnings: job and other income lines in rows 11–20 (cells such as D11:D20 appear to hold $ amounts). Total labels:
  - E22: FORMULA =SUM(E11:E20) (sums column E entries)
  - G22: FORMULA =SUM(G11:G20) (sums column G entries)
  - M22: FORMULA =SUM(M9:M20) (sums asset values in M9:M20)
- Assets / accounts: Checking, Savings, Money Market, CDs, Stocks, Insurance cash value, Home, Cars, IRAs, Other — listed in I9:I20 with corresponding value column L (L9:L20) and M col for notes.
- Giving (rows 27–35):
  - Row-level giving inputs in G29:G31, I29:I31, K29:K31, M29:M31.
  - Aggregates: G32 =SUM(G29:G31), I32 =SUM(I29:I31), K32 =SUM(K29:K31), M32 =SUM(M29:M31)
  - E33 =SUM(E29:E30); I33 =SUM(G32+I32); M33 =SUM(K32+M32)
  - M35 (TOTAL GIVING) =SUM(E33:M33)
- Savings / Emergencies (rows ~41–45):
  - E43 =SUM(E42) (emergency sum); I43 =SUM(I41:I42)
  - M43 =M42 (plans) and M45 (TOTAL SAVING) =SUM(E43:M43)
- Debt section (rows 50–65):
  - Detailed credit/unsecured debt lines in rows 53–63 (columns E, F, G, I, J, K).
  - Totals: G65 =SUM(G53:G63); M65 =SUM(K53:K63)
  - These feed STEP 2 repaying/expense totals.
- Instructional/footer prompts (proceed to step 2, etc.)

STEP 2 OF 2 — structure & key formulas
- Purpose: build a monthly budget using totals from STEP 1 and percentage guidelines.
- H6 pulls primary earnings: H6 = 'STEP 1 OF 2'!E22
- Major inputs pulled from STEP 1:
  - H8 = 'STEP 1 OF 2'!M35 (total giving)
  - H10 = 'STEP 1 OF 2'!M45 (total saving)
  - H12 = 'STEP 1 OF 2'!M65 (total repaying/debt)
  - Many category subtotal formulas sum rows to H32 (housing), H43 (transportation), H50 (insurance)
- Percent allocations (column F): e.g., F6 =100 (base), F8 =H8/H6, F10 =H10/H6, F12 =H12/H6, etc.
- Category totals and calculations:
  - H32 =SUM(H19:H31) (housing total)
  - H43 =SUM(H35:H42) (transportation total)
  - H50 =SUM(H46:H49) (insurance total)
  - M18 =SUM(M7:M17) (personal spending total)
  - M28 =SUM(M21:M27) (professional services total)
  - M40 =SUM(M31:M39) (entertainment total)
  - M43 (TOTAL EXPENSE) = H32 + H43 + H50 + M18 + M28 + M40
  - M45 (NET INCOME (ENJOYING)) = H16 (enjoying amount)
  - M47 (DIFFERENCE) = M45 - M43 (net enjoying minus total expenses)
- Additional ratio cells: K6 = M18/H6, K20 = M28/H6, K30 = M40/H6, K5 shows percent column label.

User-input areas
- Most inputs are blank entry cells in columns D, F, G, L, M across both sheets (job amounts, account balances, line-item giving/savings/debt amounts, monthly budget line items).
- Several cells are hard-coded (example H14 = 4) or reference possibly non-intuitive ranges (E22 sums E11:E20 while job $ appear in D11:D20) — review if you expect different columns for income.

Notes / highlights
- Step 2 depends heavily on totals from Step 1 (E22, M35, M45, M65, M18). Verify those source ranges match where you enter amounts.
- Many totals are simple SUMs; category percentages are computed as Hxx / H6 (total earnings).
- Output of interest: M43 (total monthly expense), M45 (net available for enjoying), M47 (difference — surplus/shortfall).
