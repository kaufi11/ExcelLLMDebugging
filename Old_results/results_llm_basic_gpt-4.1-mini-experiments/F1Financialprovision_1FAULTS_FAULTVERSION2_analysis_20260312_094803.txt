1. In Sheet B Direct Payments, cell I15 has the formula `=COUNT(I10:I14)` which counts the number of numeric entries, but it should sum the values instead.

Explanation:
- I15 is the "Total" for the "Value of Payments" columns (F to I).
- Other total cells use SUM to add amounts.
- COUNT will give the number of cells with numbers, not the total amount.

Solution:
- Change the formula in I15 from `=COUNT(I10:I14)` to `=SUM(I10:I14)`.

This will correctly sum the total value of payments for all client groups in the 65+ years column.
