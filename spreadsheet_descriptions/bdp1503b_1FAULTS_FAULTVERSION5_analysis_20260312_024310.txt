Formulas and issues to be aware of
- Many “remaining” formulas are written as =SUM(Cx-Ex) (or similar) — SUM is redundant; they could be simply =Cx-Ex.
- One inconsistent reference: G19 is =SUM(D19-E19) but the annual allocation is in C19 (likely a typo; should be =C19-E19).
- Totals sometimes use SUM on a list of summed expressions (e.g. SUM(C10+C24+...)) rather than a proper range; this works but is unconventional.
- Several E (YTD) cells are blank (treated as zero), which affects subtotals/totals; check any missing YTD entries if unexpected.
