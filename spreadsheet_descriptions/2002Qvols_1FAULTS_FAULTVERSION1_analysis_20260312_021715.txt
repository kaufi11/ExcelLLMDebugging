Sheet: Sheet2 — "RSA 2002 QUARTERLY CUMULATIVE FUEL SALES IN LITERS BY MARKET CATEGORY AND PROVINCE"

High‑level layout
- Title in A1; quarter group headings in row 4 (Q1…Q4).
- Column groups arranged by quarter + quarter totals + cumulative totals:
  - Q1: product columns C:F (C = Diesel, D = Illuminating paraffin, E = LPG, F = Petrol), G = Q1 total (SUM(C:F))
  - Q2: H:K, L = Q2 total (SUM(H:K))
  - Q2 cumulative (year‑to‑date after Q2): M:P contain product cumulative values (e.g., M = C+H), Q = cumulative total (G+L)
  - Q3: R:U, V = Q3 total (SUM(R:U))
  - Q3 cumulative: W:Z (product cumulative) and AA = cumulative total (Q+V)
  - Q4: AI:AL (product columns for Oct–Dec) with category subtotals of Q4 shown by AI:AL sums at category level
- Columns AB:AD and AC contain small identifiers/labels (codes and repeated category/province labels).
- Rows 6–131 list market categories and provinces (grouped blocks). Each category block ends with a "Total" row (e.g., row 15 = Construction Total, row 25 = General Dealers Total, etc.).
- Summary blocks end with section totals (e.g., "Construction Total", "General Dealers Total"), and row 133 is the Grand Total across all categories.

Formulas and calculation patterns
- Most totals use SUM across the product columns for the quarter (e.g., Gn = SUM(Cn:Fn); Ln = SUM(Hn:K n); Vn = SUM(Rn:Un)).
- Cumulative product columns (M, N, O, P, W, X, Y, Z) are calculated by adding corresponding quarter product values (e.g., M6 = C6+H6, W6 = M6+R6).
- Cumulative totals are sums of quarter totals (e.g., Qn = Gn+Ln; AAn = Qn+Vn).
- Category totals (e.g., row 15, 25, 35, etc.) are computed by summing the rows in that block (often via SUM over the rows).
- Final Grand Total row (row 133) aggregates all product and total columns across the sheet; AI133..AL133 sum Q4 product totals across categories.

Notable data/formula issues (observations)
- A few cells show negative values (e.g., J9 = -31,560; T9 = -9,600; E60 = -1,496,201) — verify data source/intent.
- Some formulas look incorrect or inconsistent:
  - Row 31, column L31 uses COUNT(H31:K31) rather than SUM — likely an error.
  - Several cumulative formulas reference AG columns (e.g., AK6 = AG6+Y6) but AG columns are mostly blank/not defined in many rows → could produce #REF or incorrect results if AG is empty.
- There are many blank/missing entries for some product cells in certain provinces — expect those to affect sums.

Row 133 aggregates all as Grand Total.
- Each block contains province-level rows followed by a "Total" row for the block. Many blocks also duplicate the category+province in AB:AD for reporting/printing.

Summary
- This is a comprehensive 2002 quarterly fuel‑sales workbook by market category and province. Columns are organized by quarter and cumulative totals; rows are grouped by market segment and provinces with subtotal rows. Main calculation methods are SUM for totals and simple additions for cumulative product columns. The Grand Total row (133) provides the overall totals per product and per quarter. Several formula anomalies and negative/missing data points should be reviewed before final analysis.
