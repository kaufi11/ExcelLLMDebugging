G21 — Error: formula uses COUNT(G19:G20) (returns 0–2) instead of summing costs, so the "Other Activities" grand total is wrong and the One Year Total (B23) is understated.  
Fix: replace with =SUM(G19:G20).  
Why: COUNT returns the number of numeric cells; SUM adds the monetary values. Changing to SUM correctly aggregates the other-activities costs and corrects the overall totals.
