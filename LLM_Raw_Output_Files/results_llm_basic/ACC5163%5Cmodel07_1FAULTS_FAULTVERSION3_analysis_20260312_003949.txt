Found one clear error that will make the consolidation totals and the “proof of debit and credit postings” wrong:

1) Error
- Cell F74 (Total Liabilities & Equity) contains:
  =COUNT(F67:F73)
  COUNT returns the number of numeric cells, not the sum of the amounts.

2) Why it's wrong / effect
- F74 should be the total of the liability & equity amounts in F67:F73. Using COUNT gives a small integer (how many numeric cells), so Total Liab. & Equity is incorrect.
- That error cascades to the proofs (D76/E76 and D134/E134) and any reconciliation or elimination that depends on total liabilities & equity.

3) Fix (short)
- Replace the formula in F74 with:
  =SUM(F67:F73)

- Select F74, edit the formula bar, change COUNT to SUM, press Enter.
