Sheet name: "Syracuse, NY"

Purpose
- Market-share table of depositors in the Syracuse, NY market (Total bank deposits plus 50% of thrift deposits). Deposit dollar amounts are in $000s.

Layout / key ranges
- Header: B1 "MARKET SHARE TABLE"; B2 location; B4 title "Total Bank and 50% Thrift Deposits"; B5 date line.
- Row 9: column headings:
  - B = Institution, C = Type (Bank or Thrift), D = Offices (count), E = Deposits ($000s), F = Weighted Deposits ($000s), G = Market Share (%), H = HHI (squared market share).
- Rows 10–33: 24 institutions listed (A10:A33 show rank numbers; B10:B33 names; C10:C33 types; D10:D33 offices; E10:E33 deposit amounts).
- Row 36: Totals (B36 "TOTALS"); formulas in D36:H36 sum the respective columns.

Formulas / calculations
- F (Weighted Deposits): generally F = 1 * E for Banks and F = 0.5 * E for Thrifts (i.e., thrifts counted at 50%). Most rows use explicit formulas like =1*$E10 or =0.5*$E18.
  - Note: row 12 (KEYCORP) shows "FORMULA =5" — appears to be an anomaly/manual override and should be reviewed.
- G (Market Share %): = $Frow / $F$36 * 100 (market share of weighted deposits relative to total weighted deposits in F36).
- H (HHI contribution): = Grow ^ 2 (squared market-share percent). H36 = SUM(H10:H33) gives the market HHI.
- D36, E36, F36 use SUM over rows 10:33 for offices, deposits, and weighted deposits respectively; G36 sums the percent shares (should be ≈100); H36 sums squared shares.

Units & dates
- Deposits in column E are in thousands of dollars ($000s).
- Deposit data are as of June 30, 2002; deposit ownership (used to assign bank vs thrift) is as of January 30, 2004 (see notes B39–B47).
- Market area definition given in notes (B40–B45): counties and selected towns included.

Overall purpose / output
- Produces institution-level market shares and HHI (Herfindahl-Hirschman Index) for Syracuse market using banks counted fully and thrifts at 50%, with aggregate totals in row 36.
