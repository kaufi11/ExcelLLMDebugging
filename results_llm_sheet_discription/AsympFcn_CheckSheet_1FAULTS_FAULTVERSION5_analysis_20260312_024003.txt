SubstitutionMethod_Checks sheet — errors & fixes
1) Asymptotic expression uses LOG(LOG(n,2),2) which fails for n=1
   - Why: LOG(LOG(1,2),2) = LOG(0,2) is undefined.
   - Fix (pick one):
     - Start your n list at n>=2; OR
     - Guard the formula: e.g.
       - B10: =IF($A10>1, $B$4*$A10*LOG(LOG($A10,2),2), "")
