Summary of main issues
- Q4 Homework!H5 uses COUNT instead of SUM → returns 6 instead of the student's total (propagates into other sheets).
- Q4 Summary F3:F12 (Weighted Score) use the Projects column twice (D) and never use the Homework column (E) → all weighted scores (and resulting percentages) are wrong when D and E differ.
- These two issues propagate to Q4 Summary (G column) and then into Year Summary (G and Final Average), producing incorrect final averages/grades for affected students.

Details

1) Q4 Homework!H5
- Current formula: =COUNT(B5:G5)
- Problem: COUNT returns the number of numeric cells (6), not the sum of the assignment scores. All other students use =SUM(B#:G#).
- Result: H5 = 6 (should be the sum of scores for Evans). This incorrect value is used by Q4 Summary (E6) and then flows into weighted calculations.
- Fix: Change to =SUM(B5:G5)

2) Q4 Summary F3:F12 (Weighted Score)
- Example (row 4 / Brantley): current formula in F4 =B4*0.3+C4*0.1+D4*0.4+D4*0.2
- Pattern: Every F-row uses D#*0.2 as the last term (projects again) instead of E#*0.2 (homework).
- Problem: Homework (E) is not included in the weighted total; Projects (D) are counted twice. If D and E are equal (e.g. the Points Possible row) this may not show up, but for students where D ≠ E the weighted score is incorrect.
- Result: F-values are wrong; G-values (=(F#/300)*100) and Year Summary entries that reference Q4 Summary propagate these errors.
- Correct formula: =B#*0.3 + C#*0.1 + D#*0.4 + E#*0.2
  - e.g. for Brantley (row 4): =B4*0.3 + C4*0.1 + D4*0.4 + E4*0.2
