  Explanation: D114 is the total of all A/R detail rows (SUM(D99:D113)). D12 holds the "due from officers" amount (D100+D101).   Explanation: This ensures D18 only totals the current asset line items shown (Cash, Investments, A/R, Due from officers, Due from Foundation, Inventory, Prepaids, Other current assets) and will not pick up stray cells.
