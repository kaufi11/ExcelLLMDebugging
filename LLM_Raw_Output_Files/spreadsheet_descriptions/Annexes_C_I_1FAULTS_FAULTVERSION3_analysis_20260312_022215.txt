Summary of workbook structure and contents

Overall layout and design
- Template for a 2002/03 Financial Forecast Update, organized into Annex sheets (C, D, E, F, G, H, H(1), I).
- Numeric tables use consistent column structure: H = Original FFR Forecast, I = Mid‑Year Revised Outturn, J = Variance (I−H), K = Variance % (IF H=0 then "N/A" else (J/H)*100), L = Reason for material variances.
- Many input numeric cells are initialized to 0 (placeholders). Common formula patterns: J = I − H, K = IF(H=0,"N/A",(J/H)*100), row totals using SUM. Several B-column cell numbers are auto-incremented by formulas (e.g., B18 = B15+1).
- Central workbook link: college name populated in Declaration - AnnexC!C3 and referenced across sheets (e.g., Annex D A4; annex e A4; annex f A4; annex g A4; Annex h A6; Annex h(1) A6; Annex I A2).

Sheet-by-sheet summary

1) Declaration - AnnexC
- Header: "2003 Financial Forecasts Update".
- Basic college information fields (name, address lines C3, C5:C9, contact C11, telephone C13, email C15).
- Declaration text (A19) and signature area (A26 Signed, B26 text, D26 role, H26 signature field, J26 Date).
- Serves as master for college identification used elsewhere.

2) Commentary - Annex D
- Title and "Financial Commentary" area for material changes.
- Cell A4 links the college name: ='Declaration - AnnexC'!C3.
- Instruction notes and requirement flags ("THIS MUST BE COMPLETED").

3) annex e — Income and Expenditure Account (Consolidated)
- Main I&E statement by line items (Funding Council grants, Education contracts, Tuition fees, Research, Other income, Investment income).
- Totals: Total Income (H15/I15), Total Expenditure (H23/I23), Surplus/(Deficit) (H25/I25) and after gains/losses/tax/minority interest to final surpluses.
- Formula patterns: column J = I−H; column K percentage as described.
- Many rows contain SUM formulas (e.g., H15 = SUM(H9:H14), H23 = SUM(H18:H22)).
- Bursary & Hardship section (H56:H61) with (Over)/Under use calculations.
- Most numeric fields are 0 placeholders.

4) annex f — Consolidated Balance Sheet
- Fixed assets, current assets, creditors (current & long-term), provisions, net assets, deferred capital grants, endowments, reserves, total funds.
- Aggregation formulas: Total Fixed Assets (H13 = SUM(H7:H12)), Net Current Assets (H34 = H22 − H32), Total Assets Less Current Liabilities (H36 = H13+H15+H34), Total Net Assets (H48 = H36 − H43 − H46).
- Consistent J = I−H and K % patterns.
- Links: A1 formula = 'annex e'!A1 to reuse heading; many reconciliation checks later reference annex f (see annex g).
- Most numeric cells initialized to 0.

5) annex g — Operating Cash Flow (Consolidated)
- Two panels: ORIGINAL CASH FORECAST (monthly Aug→Jul) and ACTUAL/REVISED FORECAST (same monthly layout).
- Rows for cash receipts (SFEFC funding, other grants, other cash), cash out (payroll, other payments, interest), net cash inflow/(outflow), balances b/f and c/f.
- Monthly totals across H:S and T total columns use SUM formulas (e.g., T7 = SUM(H7:S7)).
- Balance roll‑forwards link months (e.g., I18 = H19, J18 = I19, etc.).
- Validation/reconciliation cells: "These cells should be zero" comparing financing vs balance (H25 formulas) and T26 = T19 − ('annex f'!H20 + 'annex f'!H21) to reconcile with balance sheet.
- Many numeric cells default to 0.

6) Annex H and Annex H(1)
- Impact of a specific grant (Circular FE/54/02) on I&E and Balance Sheet.
- Fields to allocate total funding and show line‑by‑line impacts (income, expenditure, deferred grants, cash).
- Annex H(1) contains an example completed set: total funding 90,000 (I17), breakdown e.g. Funding Council grants 60,000; Exceptional restructuring costs 40,000; Other operating expenses 20,000; Cash at bank 30,000; Deferred capital grants 30,000 — demonstrates how grant flows into annex e/f items.

7) Annex I — Statement of underlying operating surplus/deficit
- Pulls key numbers to compute underlying operating surplus:
  - H11 = 'annex e'!I25 (Surplus/(Deficit) after Depreciation at valuation before disposal & tax from annex e).
  - H14 = 'annex e'!I19 (Exceptional restructuring costs).
  - H17, H20 are one-off adjustments (0).
  - H27 = SUM(H10:H26) to produce underlying operating surplus/deficit.

Key formulas and validations to note
- Variance and % variance pattern: J = I − H; K = IF(H=0,"N/A",(J/H)*100).
- Aggregations: SUM ranges used for totals across income/expenditure/current assets etc.
- Many cells reference Declaration - AnnexC!C3 for college name; Annex e → annex f links (annex f A1 = 'annex e'!A1).
- Cash flow vs balance sheet reconciliations exist: annex g T26 and T49 use annex f balances (H20/H21 and I20/I21).
- Validation flags: several "THESE CELLS MUST BE ZERO" or "These cells should be zero" rows to catch mismatches.

Overall assessment
- Well-structured standard forecast template with separate annexes for I&E, balance sheet, cashflow, grant impact and commentary.
- Template relies on user inputs in H/I columns (original/revised) and automatic calculations for variances and totals.
- Currently most numeric input cells are 0 (placeholders); an example filled in Annex H(1) demonstrates expected data flow.
