H14: Formula =-C14-F14 is wrong — it makes NCC-04’s Total FTEE negative.  
Fix: change to =C14+F14 (or =+C14+F14).  
Why: Total FTEE should be credit FTEE + non‑credit FTEE; the leading minus sign inverts the value and breaks totals and percent calculations (H27, D14, I14). After fixing, recalc will correct totals and derived percentages.
