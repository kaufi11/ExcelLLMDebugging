2) Weighted Average totals & ending inventory cost (missing / wrong)

3) Other sections (Moving Average perpetual, FIFO/LIFO periodic & perpetual)

- Fix (general rule): For every purchase/sale row:

- Why: consistent row-level formulas are required for correct layer tracking and totals.
