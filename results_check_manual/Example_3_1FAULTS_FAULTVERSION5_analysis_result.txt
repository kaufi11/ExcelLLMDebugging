Summary of problems
- Q4 Homework: H5 uses COUNT instead of SUM, so Evans' total is 6 (the count of numeric cells) instead of the sum of the assignment scores.
- Q4 Summary: The weighted-score formulas in column F (F3..F12) mistakenly use the Projects column (D) twice for both the 0.4 and the 0.2 weights. They never use the Homework column (E). As a result the Homework contribution is ignored and Projects is double-counted.

2) Q4 Summary sheet — weighted score column (F)

- Correct formula (use the appropriate row number):
  - For row 3: =B3*0.3 + C3*0.1 + D3*0.4 + E3*0.2
  - General: =Bn*0.3 + Cn*0.1 + Dn*0.4 + En*0.2
