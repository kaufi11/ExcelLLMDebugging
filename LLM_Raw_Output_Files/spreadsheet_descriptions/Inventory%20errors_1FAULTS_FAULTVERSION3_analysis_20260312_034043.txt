Where the imbalance comes from (concept)
- An incorrect ending inventory in one year affects COGS and net income for that year and the beginning inventory for the next year. If you leave the prior-year income effect in retained earnings (do not adjust retained earnings), the balance sheet can be out of balance. Sheet3 demonstrates the proper treatment: closing the prior-year error into retained earnings restores the accounting equation.
