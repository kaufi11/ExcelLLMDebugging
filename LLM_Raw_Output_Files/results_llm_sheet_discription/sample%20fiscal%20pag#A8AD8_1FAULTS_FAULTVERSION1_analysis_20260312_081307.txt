Summary of the error
- On sheet 1198 the Total Revenues cell for the Projected column (1198!H25) uses =COUNT(H9:H24) instead of =SUM(H9:H24).

Why this is wrong (simple)
- COUNT counts the number of non-empty cells (returns a small integer), it does NOT add up the dollar amounts. That makes 1198's projected revenue total tiny/wrong and therefore breaks the consolidated Summary projected totals, carry‑forwards and ending balances for column H.

How to fix (step‑by‑step)
1. Go to sheet "1198".
2. Select cell H25.
3. Replace the formula =COUNT(H9:H24) with =SUM(H9:H24).
4. Press Enter. Recalculate (F9 / refresh) if needed.
