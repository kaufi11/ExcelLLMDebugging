I reviewed the workbook and found several clear formula/range errors and one text typo. Below are the problems, short explanations, and the simple fixes.

1) Sheet: OP_EXP — Cell L46
- Problem: L46 =SUM(L33:L44) but J46 =SUM(J33:J43). L46 includes row 44 (an extra row) and so the two-year totals use different ranges.
- Why it’s wrong: mismatched ranges cause L-column totals to include an extra item (or a blank) and produces inconsistent totals between years.
- Fix: Change L46 to =SUM(L33:L43)

2) Sheet: Cash Flows — Cell L45

- Why it’s wrong: inconsistent ranges between columns; L45 may double-count or include unintended rows.

3) Sheet: Income — Cell J13
