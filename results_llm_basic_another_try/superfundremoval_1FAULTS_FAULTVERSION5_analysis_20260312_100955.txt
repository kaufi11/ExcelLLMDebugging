**Solutions:**

1. **Fix Weighting Sum Checks:**

     `=IF(ROUND(SUM(F6:F10),4)<>1,"ERROR","100%")`  
   - Use `ROUND` to avoid floating point precision errors.

2. **Fix Formula in G21:**  
   - Change formula to multiply by weighting, not divide:  
     `=IF(C21="yes",(1*F21),IF(C21="no",(0*F21),""))`

---

**Explanation:**

- Weightings are decimal fractions summing to 1 (100%), so the sum check must compare to 1, not 100%. Using 100% (which is 1) as a percentage in the formula causes a mismatch.  
- Weighted scores are calculated by multiplying the answer value (1 for Yes, 0 for No) by the weighting to get the weighted contribution. Dividing by weighting inflates the score incorrectly.
