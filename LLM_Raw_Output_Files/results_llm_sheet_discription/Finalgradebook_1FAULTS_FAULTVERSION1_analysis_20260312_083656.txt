- Problems:

- Current formula example: I3 =(C3*0.125)+(D3*25)+(E3*25)+(F3*25)+(G3*12.5)
  - C (homework) is a 0–100 average (Sheet2 P). D/E/F/G are decimals 0–1 (Sheet3 P and midterm/final/participation).
  - The existing multipliers are chosen to produce points out of 100, but the mix is confusing (0.125 vs 12.5 vs 25).
- Recommended clear/correct formula (produces Final Grade on 0–100 scale; copy down I3:I17):
  - I3 = (C3/100)*12.5 + D3*25 + E3*25 + F3*25 + G3*12.5
  - (Equivalent numeric result to your intent, but explicit that homework is converted from 0–100 to a 12.5‑point contribution.)
- Why: this is clearer/readable and guarantees the final grade sums to 100 max.
