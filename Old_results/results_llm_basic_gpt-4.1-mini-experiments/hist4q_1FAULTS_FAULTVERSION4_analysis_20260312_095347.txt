Error:
- In Sheet 1, cell H12 has the formula `=COUNT(H9:H11)` instead of `=SUM(H9:H11)`. This causes the subtotal for "事務機 計" in column H to show the count of numbers (which is 3) instead of the sum of sales figures.

Solution:
- Change the formula in H12 from `=COUNT(H9:H11)` to `=SUM(H9:H11)`.

Explanation:
- The subtotal row "事務機 計" should sum the sales of the three product categories (rows 9 to 11) for each quarter.
- Using COUNT counts how many numeric entries are in the range, which is incorrect for sales data.
- SUM correctly adds all the sales numbers, giving the accurate subtotal.
