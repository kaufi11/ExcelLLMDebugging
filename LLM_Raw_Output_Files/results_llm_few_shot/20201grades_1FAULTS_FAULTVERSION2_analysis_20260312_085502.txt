3) Text values in numeric ranges (examples: cells with "x", "X", ".", "#")
- Error: cells like BL7="X", BR6="#" or AZ13="x" appear inside numeric ranges that are summed/averaged.
- Why it’s wrong: text in numeric ranges either is ignored by SUM (giving unintentionally low totals) or shows errors for other formulas. It hides missing data and can distort averages or grade buckets.
- Fix:
