1) Sheet E20-9 (the copy named "E20-9 (2)")

- Wrong formula: =SUM(C5:C6)
- Correct formula: =SUM(C4:C6)
- Why: the indirect cost hourly rate must include Office rent (C4) plus Support staff (C5) and Utilities (C6). The current formula omits office rent and understates indirect costs and the bid.
- Effect (rounded):

  - Correct C11 = (350,000+860,000+246,000)/16,000 = 91.00

2) Sheet P20-3B (both copies)
- Error cell: I10
- Wrong formula: =+F14*H10
- Correct formula: =F10*H10
- Why: the manufacturing overhead applied in row 10 should use that row’s direct labor amount (F10), not the total direct labor (F14). Using F14 applies overhead to the whole job incorrectly and inflates overhead.
- Effect (rounded):
  - Wrong I10 = F14*H10 = 910 * (375/250) = 1,365
  - Correct I10 = F10*H10 = 190 * (375/250) = 285
  - Wrong total (I14) = I10 + F14 + D14 = 1,365 + 910 + 714 = 2,989
  - Correct total (I14) = 285 + 910 + 714 = 1,909

Minor note: the leading "+" in formulas (for example =+C3/C2) is harmless — it doesn't change results.
