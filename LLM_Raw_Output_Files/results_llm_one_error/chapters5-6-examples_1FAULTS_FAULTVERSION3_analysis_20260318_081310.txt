Cell: Sheet3!C24

Error: Total Revenue is calculated as =SUM(J5:J10)*31 (multiplying total demand by a fixed 31) — this uses a wrong constant and does not multiply each period's demand by its price.

Fix: Replace the formula with a SUMPRODUCT that multiplies each period's demand by its period price. Example:
=SUMPRODUCT(Sheet3!J5:J10, pricing!K5:K10)
(or if the price column is on Sheet3 use =SUMPRODUCT(J5:J10,K5:K10))

Why: SUMPRODUCT(J_range, Price_range) computes revenue per period (demand × price) and sums them. The current formula produces a meaningless result and makes Profit wrong.
