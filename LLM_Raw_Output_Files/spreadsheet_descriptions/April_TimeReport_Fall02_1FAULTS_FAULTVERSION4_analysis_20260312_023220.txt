Sheet name: Reporting — single sheet with weekly hours by activity and category.

Layout

  - B–M: Weekly hours for Weeks 4–15 (row 1 shows "Week 4"… "Week 15"; row 2 shows the date ranges for each week).
  - N: Row totals (Total hours across Weeks 4–15).
- Rows:

  - Under each category are activity rows (Reading/Preparing, Homework, Class, etc.). Studio I contains multiple sub-roles through row 34.
