1) Seq Production Costs — wrong reference in cost-per-Q20-base
- Problem cell: C11
- Current formula: =B21/C10
- Why it’s wrong: C11 should divide the Q20 grand total (C21) by the Q20 one-year total (C10). Using B21 (shotgun grand total) gives the wrong numerator.

2) Seq Production Costs — other-activities grand total uses COUNT instead of SUM

- Why it’s wrong: COUNT returns the number of numeric cells (usually 2), not the sum of the dollar amounts. That makes G21 incorrect and will break the overall yearly total (B23).
