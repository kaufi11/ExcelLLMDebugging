Faulty cell
- Summary Info!B20

Error type
- Omission / wrong range (mechanical)

What's wrong (short)
- B20 currently sums B12:B18, so it omits row 19 (MPS = 12). That makes the directorate "would like to attend" total 54 instead of 66 and creates a mismatch with the Carnegie totals (E30 = 66) and the narrative.

Fix (exact)
- Replace the formula in B20 with:
  =SUM(B12:B19)

Why this fixes it (short)
- Including B19 adds the 12 MPS responses, producing 66 and aligning the directorate totals with the Carnegie totals and the written summary.

Quick recommendations
- Verify C20 and D20 use the same row range (they do: C12:C19, D12:D19).
- To avoid similar off-by-one mistakes, convert the list to an Excel Table and use structured references or always copy/paste the same range for all three total cells.
