5) General consistency / best practice issues (short notes)
- When you fill formulas right across columns, use relative column references for per‑period cells (e.g. D43, E43) and absolute references for inputs (e.g. $C$58, $C$61). This prevents the “one‑column shift” mistakes above.

- IRR on non‑conventional cashflows: not an "error" in Excel, but be aware IRR may return one of multiple solutions. Use NPV profile or specify a guess in IRR(...) to explore alternative roots.
