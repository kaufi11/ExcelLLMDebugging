Error found
- Cell C19 is =COUNT(C20:C34) but should be summing Studio I hours like the other weekly subtotal cells. COUNT returns how many numeric cells (likely 15), not the sum of hours (should be 10).

Why it's wrong
- COUNT(C20:C34) does not add the hours — it just counts numeric cells. That makes the Week 5 section subtotal (C19) and the weekly grand total (C35) incorrect.

Fix (simple)
1. Select C19.
2. Replace the formula with =SUM(C20:C34)
3. Press Enter and recalc (F9) if needed.

What this does
- SUM(C20:C34) adds all Studio I hours for Week 5, giving the correct subtotal (10) instead of the count (15).
- C35 (Week 5 overall total) will update automatically to the correct value.
