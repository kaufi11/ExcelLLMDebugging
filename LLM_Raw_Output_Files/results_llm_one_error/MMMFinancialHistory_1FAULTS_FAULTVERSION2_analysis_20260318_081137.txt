H14 — Wrong sign in formula. It uses =H12+H13 but should be =H12-H13.  
Why: All other years use "Earnings from Operations = (Operating profit) − (Other operating (income) and expenses)". H13 is negative (‑2110), so +H13 subtracts 2110 instead of adding it back.
