Valuing Ending Inventory!C27 — Error: formula =-I10 (incorrect negative sign).  
Why: This makes "Goods Available for Sale (units)" negative, so D27 (unit cost = E27/C27) becomes negative and flips the signs for ending inventory and COGS throughout the periodic weighted-average calculations.  
Fix: Change C27 to =I10 (remove the minus).  
Result: Unit cost (D27) becomes positive and ending inventory/COGS values will calculate with correct signs.
