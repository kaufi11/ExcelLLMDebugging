Summary of Sheet "A" (JCI Statements of Financial Position)

Purpose / Header
- Title: JUNIOR CHAMBER INTERNATIONAL (JCI), INC. — Statements of Financial Position (July 31, 2002 and 2001).
- Single worksheet (Sheet A) contains a detailed trial-balance / balance-sheet-style layout with account-level detail and rollups.

Layout / Columns
- Column A: line-item descriptions (sections, account names).
- Column C: MTD / account-level detail (sometimes labeled "MTD ACTUAL").
- Column D: 2002 amounts (main current-year column).
- Column E: 2001 amounts (prior year).
- Columns G/H/I/J: accounting codes, code descriptions and ranges for each line (account number mapping).
- Several timestamp / metadata cells in G62–G71 (prepared, extension, company, period).

Major Sections (row blocks)
- Header/title rows (B1:B3).
- Current assets (A8–A18): cash & cash equivalents, short-term investments, receivables, inventories, prepaids, other current assets.
  - Cash: D9/E9 pull from D83/E83 (TOTAL CASH AND EQUIVALENTS).
  - Investments: D10/E10 pull from D96/E96 (TOTAL INVESTMENTS).
  - Accounts receivable: D11/E11 is sum of many A/R detail rows (e.g., D98–D113).
- Restricted cash (A20–A22).
- Property & equipment (A24–A34): line items for land, building, furniture, total property & equipment, less accumulated depreciation, net property & equipment.
- Other assets and totals (A36–A38): Total assets computed as D38 = D18 + D22 + D34 + D36.
- Liabilities (A42–A49 & A258–A262): current liabilities (accounts payable, accrued liabilities, due to foundation), bid bonds (congress & conference), deferred revenues and totals.
  - Accounts payable (D43/E43) are sums of many payable detail rows (D183:D198).
- Net assets / Capital (A51–A57 & A265–A281): Unrestricted, temporarily/permanently restricted, appropriated reserves, unappropriated surplus, and net income.
  - Appropriated totals (D275) and unappropriated (D277) roll into TOTAL CAPITAL (D281 = D275 + D279).
- Final reconciliation (A283 etc.): TOTAL LIABILITIES & CAPITAL (D283 = D262 + D281) and balancing checks.

Key Formulas / Mechanics
- Aggregations use SUM ranges (e.g., D18 = SUM(D8:D17); D96 = SUM(D86:D95); D114 = SUM(D99:D113); D132 = SUM(D117:D131); D170 = SUM(D165:D169)).
- Many roll-ups reference specific detail rows (e.g., Accounts receivable D11 = D98+D99+...+D113).
- Bid bond totals: D221 (congress) and D253 (conference); D255 = D221 + D253.
- Final assets vs liabilities reconciliation:
  - D178 = D83 + D96 + D114 + D132 + D139 + D144 + D152 + D162 + D170 + D175 (total assets components).
  - D283 = D262 + D281 (total liabilities + capital).

  - H289 = D178; H290 = -D283; H291 = SUM(H289:H290) (another reconciliation to zero).
