Main problems (what’s wrong and why)
1) Stray “+1” (artificially inflates totals and percent shares)
   - Sheet “Revenues by Source” C11 = 141577402+1 — the “+1” is accidental. It increases the General State Financial Aid dollar value by 1 and thus skews totals and percent calculations.
   - Sheet “General State Financial Aid” B21 = 3684419+1 — same problem for SWCC.

   Why: adding +1 alters totals and percent-of-total results. Remove the +1 so SUM/values reflect true data.

2) Negative sign typo producing a negative total FTEE for one college
   - Sheet “FTEE Calculation” H14 currently = -C14 - F14.
   - This returns a negative Total FTEE for NCC (row 14) and makes the % of total (I14 and any formula dividing by H27) incorrect.

   Why: Total FTEE should be the sum of credit FTEE (C14) and non-credit FTEE (F14). The leading minus is a typo; it produces a wrong sign and breaks all %s that use that row.

3) A few formulas use explicit additions instead of clean SUM ranges (not strictly an error but risky)
   - Example: “Revenues by Source” C9 = 9432730+111410104. If those are two components it’s OK, but better to put the components in separate cells and use SUM, or at least verify the numbers are intended. (This is lower priority.)
