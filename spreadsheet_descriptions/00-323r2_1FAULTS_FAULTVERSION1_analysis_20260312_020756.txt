Important formula patterns
- J/K/L per-item values are typically =C?/2 or =C? (skew). Many are implemented as fixed formulas like J9 =$C9/2, etc.
- Non-compensatable total: B25 = SUM(B9:B24), C25 = SUM(C9:C24).
- Compensatable total: B35 = SUM(B27:B34), C35 = SUM(C27:C34).
- Compensation total: C38 = SUM(C36:C37).
- Total error inputs: B39 = B25 + B35; C39 = C25 + C35.
- Post-compensation error: B40 = B39 - B38; C40 = C39 - C38.
- Minimum data valid window (data time - net error): B41 = B5 - B40; C41 = C5 - C40.
- Device-level calculations in P/Q use combinations of C- and L-/J-column halves to compute transmit and receive setup/hold budgets (examples: P12 = C5 - C9 - C12 - C15 - C21; P13/P14 compute transmit hold/setup after subtracting L-column terms).
