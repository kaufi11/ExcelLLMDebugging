I reviewed the workbook and found several formula problems that will give wrong totals and cascade through the financial statements. Short summary (sheet!cell — problem — fix — why):

1) OP_EXP!L46
- Problem: =SUM(L33:L44) (includes an extra row)
- Fix: =SUM(L33:L43)
- Why: The L44 row is the total/label row and should not be summed. Including it double-counts or pulls an unintended value into “TOTAL OPERATING AND MAINTENANCE.”

2) Bal Sht!J36 and Bal Sht!L36 (TOTAL ASSETS)
- Problem: J36 =SUM(J23:J35) and L36 =SUM(L23:L35) — wrong source range
- Fix: J36 = J17 + J34 ; L36 = L17 + L34
- Why: Total Assets should be Net Investment in Real Estate (cell J17/L17) plus Total Other Assets (J34/L34). The current range is incorrect and will produce a wrong total.

3) Income!L14 (TOTAL RENTAL INCOME — 2001 column)
- Problem: entered as =1 (hard-coded)
- Fix: =L10 + L13
- Why: It must sum Net Residential Rent (L10) and Net Commercial Rent (L13). A hard-coded “1” breaks all downstream totals (J20/L20, J32/L32, etc.).

4) Income!L62 (NET INCOME)
- Problem: =L57 - L60 (wrong sign for cumulative effect)
- Fix: =L57 + L60
- Why: Net income should add the cumulative effect in L60 (same sign as the J column). The minus produces an incorrect final net income.

5) Cash Flows!L45 (NET CASH FROM INVESTING — 2001)
- Problem: =SUM(L37:L44) (extends past the intended rows)
- Fix: =SUM(L37:L42)
- Why: J45 sums J37:J42. L45 must match that range. The larger range pulls in extra rows (totals/labels) and skews the investing cash flow and thereby Net Change in Cash (L56) and ending cash (L58).

6) Stkhold.Equity!G4 (label)
- Problem: Text says “YEARS ENDED DECEMBER 31, 2001 AND 2001”
- Fix: change to “YEARS ENDED DECEMBER 31, 2002 AND 2001”
- Why: Label error — not a formula but will confuse users.

Notes and impact
- Several of these are “range off-by-one” errors and one is a manual overwrite (L14). They cause cascading misstatements: totals on Schedule I, Total Assets, Total Income, Net Income, Cash Flow totals and any statements that link to them.
- After you fix the formulas above, recalculate and then trace dependent cells (use Trace Dependents) to confirm downstream totals correct.
