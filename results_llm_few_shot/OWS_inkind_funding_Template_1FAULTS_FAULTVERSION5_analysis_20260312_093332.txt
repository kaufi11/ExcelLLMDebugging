Faulty cell (main error)
- Sheet: In-kind contribution template — Cell F5
- Wrong formula: =E5*E5
- Why it’s wrong: It multiplies Hours (E5 = 100) by itself instead of Hourly Rate (D5 = 50) × Hours (E5). That produces 10,000 instead of 5,000.
- Fix: change F5 to =D5*E5

Why you must fix it
- F5 feeds the subtotal totals: F16 (Total Labor), G16, G29, G41 and the Grand Total F43. With the wrong formula those totals are overstated by 5,000 (100*100 − 50*100 = 5,000).

Quick checklist (recommended)
- Correct F5 to =D5*E5.
- Scan other rows for similar typos (e.g., accidental repeated references like E*E).
- Optionally convert the ranges to an Excel Table so row formulas auto-fill consistently and reduce manual errors.

That will restore correct line-item and grand totals.
