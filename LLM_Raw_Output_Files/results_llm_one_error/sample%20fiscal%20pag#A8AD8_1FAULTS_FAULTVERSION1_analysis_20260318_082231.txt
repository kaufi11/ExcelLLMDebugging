Cell 1198!H25 — Error: formula uses COUNT(H9:H24) instead of SUM(H9:H24).  
Why it matters: COUNT returns the number of numeric cells (not their total), so the fund's "Total Revenues" (Projected column) is wrong and this error cascades into the Summary totals.  
Fix: replace with =SUM(H9:H24). This will correctly sum revenue lines and restore correct totals throughout the workbook.
