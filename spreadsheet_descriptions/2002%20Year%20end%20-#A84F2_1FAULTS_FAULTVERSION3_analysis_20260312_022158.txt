Formulas & mechanics (common patterns)
- Heavy use of SUM ranges (e.g., C13, C17, C54, C61, C78, C92).
- Many cross-references within the sheet (e.g., accumulated funds C29 references C330; surplus C327 references C102).
- Cash-flow and five-year review pull prior-year balances via direct links (e.g., C145 = +E147).
- Negative values used to represent outflows (e.g., purchases, direct costs).
- Cross-sheet links: "5 Year review" references many values from 'Financial Statements' (e.g., 'Financial Statements'!E54, !E61, !C100, !C117, !C129, !C136).

5 Year review sheet (summary & trends)
