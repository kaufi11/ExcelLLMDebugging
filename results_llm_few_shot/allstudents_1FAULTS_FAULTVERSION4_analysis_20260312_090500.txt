Faulty cell: F44 (Grade 8, Spring 1999)

Error type: Wrong cell reference (typo).

Explanation: F44 is =E44/D45 so it divides Grade 8 passing (E44) by the Grade 10 tested count (D45) instead of Grade 8 tested (D44). That produces an incorrect percent for Grade 8.

Fix (simple):
- Replace F44 with =E44/D44

Better (safe) formula to avoid divide-by-zero and show percent formatting:
- =IF(D44=0,"",E44/D44)
or
- =IFERROR(E44/D44,"")

Prevention tip:
- Enter the correct formula for the first row (e.g. F10 = E10/D10) and fill/copy it down the column so row references stay consistent and typos like this are avoided.
