Faulty cell
- F12

What’s wrong (short)
- F12 contains the value/formula "5" instead of the weighted deposit for KEYCORP. KEYCORP is a bank and should be counted at 100% of E12 (not 5). That makes F36 (total weighted deposits) too small and therefore every market share (G) and HHI (H) wrong.

Fix (one-line)
- Replace F12 with =E12  (or =1*$E12)

Why this fixes it (short)
- F is supposed to be weighted deposits: banks = 100% * E, thrifts = 50% * E. Using =E12 restores KEYCORP’s weighted deposits so F36, G (F/F36*100) and H (square of G) recalc correctly.

Suggested improvement (to avoid similar errors)
- Put a single copy-down formula in F10:F33 to apply the weighting automatically:
  =IF($C10="Thrift",0.5*$E10,1*$E10)
  then copy down. This prevents manual entry mistakes.

Quick checks after fix
- G36 should equal ~100 (sum of market share %).
- H36 will then be the correct HHI (sum of squared shares).
