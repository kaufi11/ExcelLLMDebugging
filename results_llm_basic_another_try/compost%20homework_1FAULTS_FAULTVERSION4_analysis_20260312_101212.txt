Error found:

In the "Bulk compost" sheet, cell C19 has a different formula from the rest:

- C19 formula is `=A19+(B19/24)+1.48` (plus 1.48)
- All other C cells use `=A+(B/24)-1.48` (minus 1.48)

This inconsistency will cause incorrect "Days from" values for row 19.

Solution:

Change the formula in C19 to be consistent with the others:

`=A19+(B19/24)-1.48`

Explanation:

The formula calculates a decimal day by adding the day number plus the fraction of the hour (hour/24), then subtracts 1.48 to adjust the starting point. The plus sign in C19 breaks this logic and causes an incorrect date/time calculation. Correcting it to minus 1.48 aligns it with the rest of the data.
