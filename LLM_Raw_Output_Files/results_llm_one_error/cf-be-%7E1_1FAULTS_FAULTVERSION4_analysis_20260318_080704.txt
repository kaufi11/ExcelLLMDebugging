Cell: Year 1!E55

Error: contains a hard-coded value "3" instead of the end‑of‑month cash formula used for all other months.

Why it’s important: E55 should be the month‑3 "CASH POSITION END OF MONTH". Because it's hard‑coded, all downstream links that carry forward cash (next months, Year 2 starting balances and variance sheets) are wrong.

Fix: replace the 3 with the correct formula used in other months — e.g.
=E15-E54
(or consistent style =SUM(E15-E54))

Explanation: E15 is "TOTAL CASH AVAILABLE" for month 3 and E54 is "TOTAL CASH PAID OUT" for month 3. The end‑of‑month cash must be the difference of those two; using the formula restores correct carry‑forwards and linked sheet calculations.
