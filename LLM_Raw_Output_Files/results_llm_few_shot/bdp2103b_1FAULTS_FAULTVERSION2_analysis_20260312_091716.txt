     - they can hide mistakes when arguments include text.

     - Use =D12-M12 (not =SUM(D12-M12))

   - O95 is the only obvious hard-code. Scan for any other numbers entered where a formula should be (especially in columns O and M). Hard-coding breaks live updates.

Why these fixes matter (short)
- Using direct arithmetic (=D95-M95) and proper SUM ranges makes the sheet easier to audit, prevents wrong results when values change, and avoids accidental static numbers (hard-coding).
