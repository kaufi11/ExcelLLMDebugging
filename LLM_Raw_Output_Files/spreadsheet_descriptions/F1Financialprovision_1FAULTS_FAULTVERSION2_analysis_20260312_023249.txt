Summary of workbook structure and contents

Sheets
- Contact details
  - Simple contact form fields:
    - B2: Name of Local Authority
    - B3: Contact name
    - B4: Telephone number
  - B9: Instruction to e-mail completed form to SWStat@scotland.gsi.gov.uk

- A: Financial Provision
  - Purpose: Records financial assistance (cash or vouchers) for period 1 Apr 2002 – 31 Mar 2003.
  - Section 1: Payments under Section 12 (Social Work (Scotland) Act 1968)
    - Table 1 layout:
      - Column headings: B = Number of Payments, C = Grant/Loan amount, D = Recovered amount
      - Rows A8–A13 (categories): Energy, Rent, Travel, Food, Asylum Seekers, Other
      - A14 = TOTAL with formulas:
        - B14 = SUM(B8:B13)
        - C14 = SUM(C8:C13)
        - D14 = SUM(D8:D13)
  - Section 2: Payments under Sections 22 and 25 (Children (Scotland) Act 1995)
    - Table 2 layout:
      - Columns: B = Number of Payments (split B = Section 22, C = Section 25), D = Grant/Loan amount, E = Recovered amount
      - Rows A22–A28 (categories): Energy, Rent, Travel, Food, Day Care for Children in need, Asylum Seekers, Other
      - A29 = TOTAL with formulas:
        - B29 = SUM(B22:B28)
        - C29 = SUM(C22:C28)
        - D29 = SUM(D22:D28)
        - E29 = SUM(E22:E28)

- B: Direct Payments
  - Purpose: Records direct payments under Section 12B (Social Work (Scotland) Act 1968) for same period.
  - Layout:
    - Two blocks per client group: Number of clients (columns B–E) and Value of payments (columns F–I)
    - Age-group columns: 16–17, 18–64, 65+, plus row totals (E and I)
    - Client groups listed in rows A10–A14: People with Physical Disabilities; Learning Disabilities; Mental Health Problems; Disabled Parents; Other (specify)
  - Row and column totals with formulas:
    - Row totals per client group:
      - E10:E14 = SUM(B10:D10) etc.
      - I10:I14 = SUM(F10:H10) etc.
    - Column totals (row 15):
      - B15 = SUM(B10:B14)
      - C15 = SUM(C10:C14)
      - D15 = SUM(D10:D14)
      - E15 = SUM(E10:E14)
      - F15 = SUM(F10:F14)
      - G15 = SUM(G10:G14)
      - H15 = SUM(H10:H14)
      - I15 is currently FORMULA = COUNT(I10:I14) (note: this counts non-empty cells; likely intended to be SUM(I10:I14))
  - Notes area: guidance on disabled parents and space to specify “Other” reasons

Key formulas / checks
- Totals in A: Financial Provision use straightforward SUM ranges for category totals (B14,C14,D14 and B29,C29,D29,E29).
- In B: Direct Payments all row/column totals use SUM except I15 which uses COUNT(I10:I14) — likely an error if the intent is to total payment values.
- Timeframe and legal basis are repeated in headings: 1 Apr 2002 – 31 Mar 2003; Sections 12, 12B, 22 and 25 referenced.

Overall layout
- Each sheet has a clear header describing legal basis and reporting period.
- A: two vertically stacked tables (Section 12; Sections 22/25) with identical category rows and SUM totals.
- B: matrix of client groups × age bands with separate blocks for counts and monetary values, with row and column totals.
