Error:
In cell A22, the formula is =A21-1, which breaks the sequential numbering of the IDs. All other rows use =previous cell + 1, but this one subtracts 1, causing the ID to go backward from 21 to 20 instead of continuing to 22.

Solution:
Change the formula in A22 from =A21-1 to =A21+1.

Explanation:
The numbering in column A is meant to be sequential and increasing by 1 for each row. Using =A21-1 in A22 causes the sequence to go backward, which is inconsistent and likely an error. Changing it to =A21+1 restores the proper incremental sequence.
