Error:
In Sheet 1, row 12 (Business machines total), the formula in cell H12 is incorrect:
- H12 formula is =SUM(H10:H11) but it should sum H9:H11 like other months.
This causes the total for Business machines in the 2nd quarter of 2001 to be wrong because it excludes H9 (Office imaging products).

Solution:
Change the formula in H12 from:
=SUM(H10:H11)
to:
=SUM(H9:H11)

Explanation:
The total for Business machines should be the sum of Office imaging products (row 9), Computer peripherals (row 10), and Business information products (row 11). All other columns use =SUM(row9:row11), but H12 mistakenly excludes row 9. Fixing this formula will correctly calculate the total for that quarter.
