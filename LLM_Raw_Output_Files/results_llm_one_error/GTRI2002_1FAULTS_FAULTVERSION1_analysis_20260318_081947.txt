Cell: 2002 Report!C21

Error: formula is =H21/1000000 (uses Land) but should use Air (G21).  
Fix: change formula to =G21/1000000.

Why: C21 is supposed to show the Air value (G21) in millions of pounds. Using H21 duplicates the Land value in both C21 and D21, misreporting the Air column and breaking downstream totals. Correcting to G21/1000000 restores the proper air value and fixes the totals.
