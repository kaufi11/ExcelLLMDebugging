5) Sheet: FTEE Calculation
- Title/period: "FTEE Calculation by Iowa Community College" — FY1999–2000.
- Purpose: convert credit hours and non-credit contact hours to FTEE and compute college and system totals/percentages.
- Key columns and formulas:
  - Credit hours in B; Credit FTEE C = B / 24 (C11 = B11/24, etc.).
  - Non-credit contact hours in E; Non-credit FTEE F = E / 600.
  - Total FTEE H = C + F (Hn = Cn + Fn).
  - Percent of total I = Hn / $H$27.
  - Totals row (row 27): B27 = SUM(B11:B26), C27 = SUM(C11:C26), F27 = SUM(F11:F26), H27 = SUM(H11:H26); C27/H27 and F27/H27 produce system-level percents.
