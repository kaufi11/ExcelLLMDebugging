Cell with the biggest error: 1198!H25

Error: formula uses =COUNT(H9:H24) instead of =SUM(...). COUNT returns the number of nonblank numeric cells (a count), not the total revenue amount, so the Total Revenues (and all dependent totals/ending balances) are wrong.

Fix: replace formula in 1198!H25 with =SUM(H9:H24). Then recalc or press F9 to update dependent totals (C25, D37, D72, ending balances on Summary).
