Cell: Year 1!M6

Error: contains the hard-coded value "5" instead of a formula. This breaks the chain where each month's "Cash on Hand (beginning)" should reference the previous month's "Cash position end" (row55), causing incorrect totals downstream (months 11–12 and Year 2 links).

Solution: replace M6 with the correct formula linking to the prior month end:
=SUM(L55)

Why this fixes it: every month’s beginning cash must equal the previous month’s ending cash. Using =SUM(L55) restores the monthly chain so TOTAL CASH AVAILABLE, CASH POSITION (row55) and any sheets referencing Year 1!N55 will calculate correctly.
