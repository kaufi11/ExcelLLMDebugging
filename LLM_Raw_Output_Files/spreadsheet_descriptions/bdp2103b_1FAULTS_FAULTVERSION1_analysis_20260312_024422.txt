Overview
- Workbook: Sheet1 contains the financial projection; Sheets2–16 are present but contain no data in the supplied extract.
- Purpose: 2003/04 financial projection for “Scotland Against Drugs” / NHS Health Scotland (prepared 13 Oct 2003).

Sheet1 – layout and structure
- Header: A1–A4 show title and date.
- Column roles:
  - A: Expenditure descriptions and section headings.
  - C: “Annual allocation” (budgeted amounts).
  - D: “Revised annual” (revised budgets).
  - F–L: Monthly forecasts/actuals for the 6-month/period (F = period/6 month and G–L = Oct–Mar).
  - M: “period / 12 month” — per-row formula summing F:L (usually Mx = SUM(Fx:Lx)).
  - O: “over(-)/underspend / year end” — typically O = D − M (some cells use SUM(Dx−Mx) notation).

Sections and key rows
- Campaign (top of sheet): Staffing, Administration (many sub-lines), Challenge Fund administration, Public Relations, School Programme, Community Programme, Business Programme.
  - Many rows compute monthly entries and roll up to M via SUM(F:L). Subtotals use SUM over the relevant row ranges (e.g., administration subtotal = SUM(C15:C25), and monthly subtotals = SUM(F15:F25), etc.).
- Totals:
  - C72 Total campaign expenditure = sum of major category totals (C12 + C26 + C31 + C38 + C56 + C62 + C70) → computed total = 1,000 (annual allocation).
- Scottish Drugs Challenge Fund (separate block):
  - C76 Gross expenditure = 508 (annual); monthly breakdown in F–L; M76 = SUM(F76:L76).
  - C78 Income from donations = -8.
  - C80 Net expenditure = C76 + C78 → 500 (annual).
- Education Sector Initiative (ESI):
  - Detailed rows C93:C98 with C99 Total ESI expenditure = SUM(C93:C98) → 254 (annual).
- Primary School Initiative (PSI):
  - Rows C103:C106 with C107 Total PSI expenditure = SUM(C103:C106) → 173 (annual).

Formulas and mechanics
- Repeated formula patterns:
  - Row totals: Mx = SUM(Fx:Lx)
  - Subtotals: Cx/Dx = SUM(range) for the annual/revised amounts; monthly subtotals = SUM over same rows in F–L.
  - Underspend/year-end: Ox = D x − M x (often expressed as SUM(Dx−Mx) in the source).
  - Category totals roll up by summing subtotals (e.g., C72 sums C12, C26, C31, etc.).
- Minor inconsistencies/spots to check:
  - O19 uses COUNT(D19−M19) which looks incorrect (should be D19−M19 or SUM).
  - Some rows have blank months (no entries) — M will reflect only the filled months.
  - A donations note (A113–A114) states an additional £20,936.30 of donations/interest is held externally and not included in the prior figures.

Key numeric summaries (annual column C)

- Scottish Drugs Challenge Fund gross (C76): 508; donations (C78): -8; net (C80): 500
- Education Sector Initiative total (C99): 254

Overall notes
- The sheet is a classic budget/forecast layout: planned (C), revised (D), monthly actuals/forecasts (F–L), period totals (M), and variance/underspend (O). Most totals are calculated with SUM formulas and category roll-ups.
