Faulty cell
- D6

Error type
- Wrong reference / mechanical typo

What’s wrong (short)
- D6 is "=607+D8" instead of adding the two deficient sub‑totals in that column. The constant 607 should be the value from D7. This makes the D6 total (and any totals that use it, e.g. D12) incorrect.

Fix (formula)
- Replace with =D7+D8  (or =SUM(D7:D8))

Why this fixes it (short)
- D6 is "Urban deficient bridges, total" and should equal the sum of the urban structurally (D7) and urban functionally (D8) counts. Using D7+D8 restores the correct aggregation and fixes downstream totals that depend on D6.

Optional recommendation
- Several totals in the sheet are hard‑entered numbers (e.g. N3, N6, N9, N12, N13, N14). For consistency and to avoid future errors, convert those to formulas referencing source cells.
