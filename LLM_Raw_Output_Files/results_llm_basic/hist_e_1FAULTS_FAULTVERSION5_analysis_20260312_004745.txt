I found two problems in the workbook. Short summary, cause and simple fixes below.

1) Wrong sign in Operating profit (1997)
- Error: Sheet 1, cell H9 (Operating profit for 1997) is =H6+H8. It adds S.G.&A instead of subtracting it.
- Impact: Income before income taxes (H16), Income before cumulative effect, Net income (H22) and all dependent % rows for 1997 are wrong.
- Fix: Change H9 to =H6-H8 (i.e. Gross profit minus S.G.&A). After that, recalc (Excel will update) and the dependent rows will correct themselves.

2) Inconsistent treatment of "Corporate and Eliminations" → mismatched consolidated Net Sales across sheets
- Symptom: Consolidated Net Sales on the Income Statement (Sheet 1 row 4: C4:N4) match the "Total" in Sheet 4 (Sales by Product, row 11) but do NOT match the "Consolidated" in Sheet 5 (segment table, row 33). Sheet 5’s consolidated is SUM(segment rows including the Corporate & Eliminations negative row), so its consolidated figures are different from Sheet 1 / Sheet 4 totals.

This is a consistency / modelling error (either the corporate eliminations were double‑counted/omitted or the wrong aggregation method is used).
- How to fix (pick one consistent approach):
