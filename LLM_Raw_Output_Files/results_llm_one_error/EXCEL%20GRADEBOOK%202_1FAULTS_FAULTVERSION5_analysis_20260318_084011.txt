Cell Q5 — Wrong formula.
Current: =(COUNT(J5:O5)-MIN(J5:O5))/(COUNT(J5:O5)-1)
Fix: =(SUM(J5:O5)-MIN(J5:O5))/(COUNT(J5:O5)-1)

Explanation: The numerator used COUNT (number of scores) instead of SUM (total points), so the "drop lowest and average" calculation returns a tiny/incorrect value. Replacing COUNT with SUM makes it subtract the lowest score from the total then divide by (n‑1) to give the correct average.
