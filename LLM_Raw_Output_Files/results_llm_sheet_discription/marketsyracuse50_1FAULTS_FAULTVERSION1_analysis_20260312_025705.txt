Findings (short)

2. Potential inconsistency (risk of wrong HHI): Column G multiplies the share by 100 in the formula (= $Fxx / $F$36 * 100) while the sheet header shows (%) and column H squares G. If G is formatted as Percentage (Excel stores 0.2 but displays 20%), the *100 will produce the wrong underlying value and H will be wrong. The sheet will be correct only if G is stored/displayed as a plain number (e.g. 20 for 20%). The current layout is ambiguous and fragile.
