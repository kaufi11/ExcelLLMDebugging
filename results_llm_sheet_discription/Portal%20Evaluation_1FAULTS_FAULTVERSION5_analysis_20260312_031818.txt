Short answer — main errors found
1. Typo in I21: formula is =H21*116 but should be =H21*C21.  
2. Several weighted-score (WS) cells in columns I, L, O, R are hard-coded numbers instead of formulas (inconsistent). That makes totals wrong if any score or weight changes.  
3. Summary table (A45 onward) doesn’t clearly pull all four vendor totals from the totals row (I44, L44, O44, R44) — appears incomplete/incorrect.

Why these are problems
- The I21 typo multiplies by 116 (wrong constant) so Oracle’s WS and Oracle total are incorrect.  
- Hard-coded WS values break the automatic calculation model: changing a score or weight will not update the WS or totals.  
- If the summary cells don’t reference I44/L44/O44/R44, the reported overall scores can be out-of-sync with the detailed sheet.

How to fix it (easy steps)
Manual fix (quick)
1. Fix the typo: in cell I21 change formula to:
   =H21*C21
2. For each vendor block, in row 8 enter the correct formula and then fill down to row 43:
   - Oracle WS (column I): in I8 enter =H8*C8 and fill down to I43.
   - uPortal WS (column L): in L8 enter =K8*C8 and fill down to L43.
   - CleverPath WS (column O): in O8 enter =N8*C8 and fill down to O43.
   - SharePoint WS (column R): in R8 enter =Q8*C8 and fill down to R43.
3. Ensure totals row formulas are correct:
   - I44 = SUM(I8:I43)
   - L44 = SUM(L8:L43)
   - O44 = SUM(O8:O43)
   - R44 = SUM(R8:R43)
4. Update the summary table to reference those totals (example):
   - Oracle total cell = I44
   - uPortal total cell = L44
   - CleverPath total cell = O44
   - SharePoint total cell = R44

Automatic fix (one-click VBA)
Paste this small macro and run it (fixes all WS formulas and totals):

Sub FixWeightedScores()
  Dim r As Long
  For r = 8 To 43
    Cells(r, "I").Formula = "=H" & r & "*C" & r
    Cells(r, "L").Formula = "=K" & r & "*C" & r
    Cells(r, "O").Formula = "=N" & r & "*C" & r
    Cells(r, "R").Formula = "=Q" & r & "*C" & r
  Next r
  Range("I44").Formula = "=SUM(I8:I43)"
  Range("L44").Formula = "=SUM(L8:L43)"
  Range("O44").Formula = "=SUM(O8:O43)"
  Range("R44").Formula = "=SUM(R8:R43)"
End Sub

- After fixing, press Ctrl+` to inspect formulas or use a helper column: =IF(I8=H8*C8,"OK","WRONG") to confirm all rows.  
- Make sure score cells (H,K,N,Q) are numeric, not text. If you see totals still off, recalc (F9) and check for any leftover hard-coded values.
