1) Mechanical typo (wrong function)

- Problem: Formula uses COUNT(B20:B23) which returns the number of numeric cells (4) instead of their sum. This makes Total Assets far too small and breaks the balance sheet.

- Why: SUM adds the asset amounts; COUNT only counts how many cells contain numbers.

- Cause: Changing beginning/ending inventory changes COGS and Net Income, but the prior-period inventory mistake wasn’t closed to retained earnings on the balance sheet.
