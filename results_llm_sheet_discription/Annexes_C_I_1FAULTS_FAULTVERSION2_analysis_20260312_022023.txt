2) Incorrect variance % formula for Total Funds in annex f (cell K68)

- Impact: if H68 (Original Total Funds) and H48 (Original Total Net Assets) differ for any reason, K68 will be wrong. It’s fragile and inconsistent with every other % cell.

- Why it works: this computes the actual variance % for Total Funds (J68 / H68) and matches the defensive divide‑by‑zero check used everywhere else.
