Error:
- In Sheet 1, cell H12 has the formula `=COUNT(H9:H11)` instead of `=SUM(H9:H11)`. This causes the total for "Business machines total" in the 2nd quarter of 2001 to be incorrect because COUNT counts the number of numeric entries (which is 3), not their sum.

Solution:
- Change the formula in cell H12 from `=COUNT(H9:H11)` to `=SUM(H9:H11)`.

Explanation:
- The row "Business machines total" sums the sales of three product categories (Office imaging products, Computer peripherals, Business information products). Using SUM adds their values correctly, while COUNT only counts how many cells contain numbers, leading to a wrong total. Replacing COUNT with SUM fixes the total calculation.
