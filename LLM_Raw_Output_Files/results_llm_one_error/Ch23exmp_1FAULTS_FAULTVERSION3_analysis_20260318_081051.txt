Cell: Formulas!C24 — Error: formula uses =COUNT(B21:B24) instead of =SUM(B21:B24).

Why it’s wrong: COUNT returns the number of numeric cells (4), not the total of the expense amounts, which makes operating income, tax and net income incorrect.

Fix: Change the formula in C24 to =SUM(B21:B24). This will correctly add the expense lines and restore correct downstream calculations.
