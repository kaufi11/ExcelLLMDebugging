Error:
- In cell E60 (All Grades for Spring 1998), the formula sums E53:E59. It should sum E53:E58 to match the tested range D53:D58.
- This mismatch causes the total passing count to be incorrect for Spring 1998, which also affects the percent passing in F60.

Solution:
- Change the formula in E60 from `=SUM(E53:E59)` to `=SUM(E53:E58)`.

Explanation:
- The tested numbers for Spring 1998 are in D53:D58 (Grades 3 to 8 plus Grade 10 is in D59 but not included in the sum).
- To correctly calculate total passing, sum the passing numbers for the same grades (E53:E58).
- Summing E53:E59 includes an extra row (Grade 10) not included in tested sum, causing inconsistency.
- Correcting this ensures the total passing and percent passing are accurate and consistent.
