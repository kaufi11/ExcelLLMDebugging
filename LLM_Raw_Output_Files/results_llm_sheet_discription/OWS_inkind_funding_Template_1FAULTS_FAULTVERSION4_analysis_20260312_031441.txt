Findings
- Error: On the "In-kind contribution template" sheet cell F5 contains =E5*E5 (100*100) instead of hourly-rate × hours.
- Impact: F5 shows 10,000 instead of the correct 5,000. That overstates labor total and cascades through running totals:
  - Current Grand Total (with bad F5) = 88,600
  - Correct Grand Total (after fix) = 83,600
  - Labor total (F16) drops from 14,500 → 9,500 (difference = 5,000)

Why it’s wrong
- E5 is Projected Labor Hours (100). The formula multiplies hours by hours instead of Hourly Rate (D5) × Hours (E5).

Fix (quick steps)
1. Go to sheet "In-kind contribution template".
2. Select cell F5.
3. Replace the formula =E5*E5 with =D5*E5
4. Press Enter. Verify F5 becomes 5,000 and totals (F16, G16, G29, G41, F43) update.

Explanation of fix
- Changing to =D5*E5 computes Hourly Rate × Projected Hours, consistent with the other labor rows (and with the "Cost sharing template").
- The change corrects the per-task amount and automatically corrects all dependent totals (section sums and running totals).

Quick recommendation to avoid this type of error
- Use consistent formulas filled down (enter =D5*E5 in F5 then drag/fill down) so every row uses the same pattern.
- Consider conditional checks (e.g., a simple audit column that flags F5 <> D5*E5) or Data Validation to catch accidental formula changes.
