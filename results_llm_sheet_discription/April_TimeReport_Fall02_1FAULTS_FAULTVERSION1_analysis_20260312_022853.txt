Findings (short)
- Error: Cell N24 (Client Meetings total) has subtraction: N24 = B24 - C24 - D24 - ... - M24. This produces -4 instead of +4 (should be sum of weekly hours).  
- Risk/poor practice: Column N mostly uses long manual addition (B4+C4+...+M4). Manual formulas are error-prone (N24 is an example).

Why it's wrong
- Totals should add weekly hours. Using minus signs produces wrong (negative) totals and makes grand totals incorrect.
- Manual long formulas are easy to mistype and hard to maintain.

Fix (quick)
1. Correct N24:
   - Replace with: =SUM(B24:M24)

2. Make column N robust (recommended):
   - In N4 enter: =SUM(B4:M4)
   - Fill / copy N4 down through N34 (this replaces all manual long-add formulas and prevents similar typos).

3. Make grand total clear (optional improvement):
   - Change N35 to: =SUM(N4:N34)  (this sums all activity row totals)
   - Or keep N35 = N3+N7+N11+N15+N19 if you prefer category subtotals — both work, but SUM(N4:N34) is simpler and avoids missing rows.

Why this solution
- SUM(Bx:Mx) automatically adds the full week range and is less likely to contain sign or omission mistakes.  
- Fixing N24 restores the correct positive total and brings down-stream totals (category and grand totals) back into alignment.

Extra small note
- Cosmetic: B1–M1 labels include "Week9" without a space; not an error for calculations but you may want to make it "Week 9" for consistency.
