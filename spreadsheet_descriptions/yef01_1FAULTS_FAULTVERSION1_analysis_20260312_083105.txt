Summary of workbook structure and contents

Overall layout/common conventions
- Each table starts at row 2 (A2 = Table #) with titles in rows 3–5. Headers typically at row 7–8. Data begins at row 10, with a totals row (mid-teens or row 20/28). Footnotes and source lines follow.
- Percent columns compute each line’s share of a sheet-level total (absolute references to the total cell). Totals use SUM. Several sheets include ROUND and small manual adjustments (±0.01, ±0.000001, −0.0001) to force rounding/totals.

Sheet: Revenues by Source
- Purpose: Shows revenue sources (dollars and percent of total) for FY 2000–2001.
- Key columns: C = dollar amounts; B = percent of total via formulas like =C10/$C$16.
- Important formulas/values:
  - Tuition & Fees C10 = 9,595,817 + 116,896,967 → 126,492,784; B10 = C10 / $C$16
  - General State Financial Aid C12 = 147,577,404 − 1 → 147,577,403
  - Other Income C14 = 579,467 + 2,102,504 + 3,320,377 + 14,252,767 → 20,255,115
  - Local C11 = 18,974,313; Federal C13 = 11,019,523
  - Total Revenues C16 = SUM(C10:C15) → 324,319,138 (sum of listed items)
- Notes: “Other Income” components and rounding footnote present.

Sheet: Expenditures by Function
- Purpose: Breaks unrestricted operating expenditures by function (dollars and percent).
- Key columns: C = dollar expenditures; B = percent (each = Cx / $C$20).
- Values:
  - Row values: Arts & Sciences 68,296,648; Vocational/Technical 81,061,843; Adult Education 36,221,594; … General Institutional 38,233,292.
  - Total Expenditures C20 = SUM(C10:C19) → 320,921,214.
- Notes: Rounding footnote and source line included.

Sheet: Expenditures by Category
- Purpose: Expense categories (Salaries, Services, Materials, etc.) with percent of total.
- Key formulas:
  - B% uses ROUND((Cxx/$C$16),4) (four decimal places), with slight manual adjustments on some lines.
- Values:
  - Salaries C10 = 240,013,505 (largest);
  - Services C11 = 43,768,807; Materials/Travel C12 = 18,503,493; Current Expenses C13 = 11,355,949; Capital Outlay C14 = 7,279,460.
  - Total C16 = SUM(C10:C15) → 320,921,214 (matches Expenditures by Function total).
- Note: Rounding/adjustment footnote.

Sheet: General State Financial Aid
- Purpose: Lists FY2001 general state financial aid by college and each college’s share of total.
- Key columns:
  - B = aid by college (dollar values).
  - C = share of total = Bx / $B$26 (B26 = total).
  - D = % of Total FTEE (provided as decimals in the sheet).
- Values:
  - Individual college examples: NICC 7,082,328; KCC 20,391,658; DMACC 21,642,884; IHCC B23 = 12,027,969.
  - Total B26 = SUM(B10:B24) → 147,577,403 (this equals the General State Financial Aid figure in Revenues sheet).
- Notes: “May not total due to rounding” footnote and citation of data sources.

Sheet: FTEE Calculation
- Purpose: Computes Full‑Time Equivalent Enrollment (FTEE) by college from credit and non‑credit activity, and each college’s share of total FTEE.
- Key calculations:
  - Credit FTEE = Credit hours / 24 (Cx = Bx/24).
  - Non‑credit FTEE = Non‑credit contact hours / 600 (Fx = Ex/600).
  - Total FTEE (H) = Credit FTEE + Non‑credit FTEE (some rows include +0.01 small adjustments).
  - Percent of total (I) = Hx / $H$28 (H28 is total FTEE, with small adjustments applied in the totals row).
- Structure: Columns for credit hours, credit FTEE, credit % of college, non‑credit contact hours, non‑credit FTEE, non‑credit % of college, total FTEE, and % of total FTEE. Totals row sums hours, FTEE and percentages with small manual tweaks to force sums.
- Notes: Footnote: percentages may not equal 100% due to rounding.

Sheet: FTEE Graph
- Purpose: Chart data for percentage of total FTEE by college (used to build Figure 25).
- Key values: D10:D24 list the % of total FTEE decimals for each college (e.g., 0.055031; 0.049043; 0.036029; … 0.039923).
- Source and caption lines present.

Other common items and data provenance
- Multiple sheets include asterisks and notes saying figures are rounded to nearest dollar and may differ due to rounding.
- Source cited repeatedly: Iowa Department of Education, Annual Report FY2001, Unrestricted General Fund AS‑15E, Fund 1; MIS files for FTEE and enrollment data.
