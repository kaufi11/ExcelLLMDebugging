### Sheet: E20-9 vs E20-9 (2)

**Error:**
- In E20-9 (2), cell C11 formula is `=SUM(C5:C6)/C2` which sums only Support staff salaries and Utilities, excluding Office rent (C4).
- In E20-9, cell C11 formula is `=SUM(C4:C6)/C2` which correctly sums Office rent, Support staff salaries, and Utilities.

**Explanation:**
Indirect cost hourly rate should include all indirect costs (Office rent, Support staff salaries, Utilities). Missing Office rent underestimates the indirect cost rate.

**Solution:**
Use `=SUM(C4:C6)/C2` in C11 to include all indirect costs.

**Solution:**
Change I10 formula to `=F10*H10` to correctly allocate overhead for that date.
