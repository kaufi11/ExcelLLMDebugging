Faulty cell
- F74

Error type
- Mechanical / wrong function

Explanation (short)
- F74 currently uses COUNT(F67:F73). COUNT returns how many numeric cells there are, not their total. Total Liabilities & Equity should add the amounts, so COUNT produces a wrong (usually much smaller) result and breaks the balancing/proof formulas.

Fix (one-line)
- Replace the formula in F74 with: =SUM(F67:F73)

Why this fixes it (short)
- SUM adds the values in F67:F73 (the component liability & equity lines), giving the correct Total Liab. & Equity. That restores correct totals used by the proof formulas (D76/E76, D134/E134, etc.).

Quick check after fix
- Verify D76 = E76 and D134 = E134 (proofs balance). If they still don’t balance, look for non-numeric text in the summed ranges or additional wrong functions.
