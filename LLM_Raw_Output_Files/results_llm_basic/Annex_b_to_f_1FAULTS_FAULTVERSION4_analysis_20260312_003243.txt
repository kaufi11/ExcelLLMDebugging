Why these fixes work
- T22/T23/T45/T46: those cells should be the sum across the 12 monthly columns (H:S). Using S22/S23 only returned the single-month (July) value and made year totals and reconciliations incorrect. SUM(H22:S22) aggregates all months as intended.
- K69: every “Variance as % of original forecast” cell follows the pattern IF(Hx=0,"N/A",(Jx/Hx)*100). K69 was incorrectly referencing another cell; replacing it restores the intended percent calculation.
- B22: the +256 was a clear typo in the auto-numbering pattern (others are +1). Changing it to +1 restores consistent numbering.
