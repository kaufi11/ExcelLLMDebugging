Spreadsheet overview — two worksheets: "STEP 1 OF 2" (data collection) and "STEP 2 OF 2" (monthly budget driven by Step 1 totals).

1) STEP 1 OF 2 — Personal Financial Profile (layout)
- Organized in vertical sections across columns C–M and I–M:
  - "WHAT I EARN" (rows 9–20): rows for JOB #1, JOB #2, OTHER incomes. Key total: E22 = SUM(E11:E20) (gross income column).
  - "TAX WITHHELD" (G9 and G11:G20): G22 = SUM(G11:G20) (total tax withheld).
  - "WHAT I OWN" (I9:I20 with values in L9:L20 and descriptions in M9:M20): assets such as checking, savings, money market, CDs, stocks, home, cars, retirement. Asset total: M22 = SUM(M9:M20).
  - "TOTALS" labels: C22 TOTAL (E22, G22, M22 shown as above).
- Giving section (rows 25–35):
  - Line items for Tithes, Offerings, Other (rows 29–31) with row sums by column: G32 = SUM(G29:G31), I32 = SUM(I29:I31), K32 = SUM(K29:K31), M32 = SUM(M29:M31).
  - E33 = SUM(E29:E30) and I33 = SUM(G32+I32) (I33 aggregates giving columns), M33 = SUM(K32+M32).
  - M35 (TOTAL GIVING) = SUM(E33:M33) — overall giving total.
- Saving / Future needs (rows 38–45):
  - Emergency and plans fields; E43 = SUM(E42) (emergencies), I43 = SUM(I41:I42), M43 = =M42.
  - Total saving: M45 = SUM(E43:M43).
- Debt section (rows 48–65):
  - Credit/unsecured/excessive lifestyle debt table with many row entries (E53:E63, G53:G63, I53:I63, K53:K63).
  - Debt totals: G65 = SUM(G53:G63). K65 labeled TOTAL MONTHLY; M65 set to 6 (static).
- Navigation note at G67 prompting to proceed to STEP 2.

2) STEP 2 OF 2 — Monthly Financial Plan (budget)
- Top: H6 pulls total gross income from Step 1: H6 = 'STEP 1 OF 2'!E22. F6 shows 100% (base).
- Major budget categories laid out with recommended % ranges in labels:
  - Giving: H8 = 'STEP 1 OF 2'!M35; F8 = H8/H6
  - Saving: H10 = 'STEP 1 OF 2'!M45; F10 = H10/H6
  - Repaying (debt): H12 = 'STEP 1 OF 2'!M65; F12 = H12/H6
  - Tax withheld/payments: H14 = 'STEP 1 OF 2'!G22; F14 = H14/H6
  - Enjoying (net discretionary): H16 = H6 - H8 - H10 - H12 - H14; F16 = H16/H6
- Housing, Professional services, Entertainment, Transportation:
  - H32 = SUM(H19:H31) (housing total)
  - M28 = SUM(M21:M27) (professional services total); K20 = M28/H6
  - M40 = SUM(M31:M39) (entertainment total); K30 = M40/H6
  - H43 = SUM(H35:H42) (transportation total); F34 = H43/H6
- Overall expense and net:
  - H50 = SUM(H46:H49) (insurance total); F45 = H50/H6
  - M43 (TOTAL EXPENSE) = H32 + H43 + H50 + M18 + M28 + M40 (sums housing + transport + insurance + personal totals + prof services + entertainment).
  - M45 (NET INCOME (ENJOYING)) = H16; M47 (DIFFERENCE) = M45 - M43.
- Cross-sheet links: Step2 depends on Step1 totals at:
  - 'STEP 1 OF 2'!E22 → H6 (income)
  - 'STEP 1 OF 2'!G22 → H14 (tax)
  - 'STEP 1 OF 2'!M35 → H8 (giving)
  - 'STEP 1 OF 2'!M45 → H10 (saving)
  - 'STEP 1 OF 2'!M65 → H12 (repaying target)

Key formulas / noteworthy cells (quick list)
- E22 = SUM(E11:E20) (gross income)
- G22 = SUM(G11:G20) (tax withheld)
- M22 = SUM(M9:M20) (assets total)
- M35 = SUM(E33:M33) (total giving)
- M45 = SUM(E43:M43) (total saving)
- G65 = SUM(G53:G63) (debt total)
- H6 = 'STEP 1 OF 2'!E22 (Step2 income input)
- H8/H10/H12/H14 link to Step1 totals as above
- H16 = H6 - H8 - H10 - H12 - H14 (discretionary/enjoying)
- M43 = H32+H43+H50+M18+M28+M40 (total expense)
- M47 = M45 - M43 (surplus/(shortfall))

Overall layout
- Step 1: data-entry form style: left columns for income, middle for taxes, right for assets and column of amounts; separate giving, saving and debt tables with prebuilt SUM cells.
- Step 2: budget worksheet using Step 1 totals to compute category dollar amounts and percentages versus total income; many category lines left for user-entered monthly amounts (column M) with automatic subtotals and comparisons.
