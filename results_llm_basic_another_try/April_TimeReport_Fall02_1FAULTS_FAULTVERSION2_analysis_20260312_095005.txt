Error:
- In cell H19 (Studio I total for Week 10), the formula is =COUNT(H20:H34) instead of =SUM(H20:H34). This causes it to count non-empty cells rather than summing their values, resulting in an incorrect total.

Solution:
- Change the formula in H19 from =COUNT(H20:H34) to =SUM(H20:H34).

Explanation:
- COUNT counts the number of cells with data, ignoring the actual numeric values.
- SUM adds all numeric values in the range.
- Since all other totals use SUM, H19 should also use SUM to correctly total the hours for Week 10.
