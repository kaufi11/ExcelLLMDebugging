Findings — main error
- Cell 'Your Company'!C22 contains the value/formula "7" instead of the intended cash-flow formula. Because C22 is included in the total (C41 = SUM(C22:C39)), the workbook's Total Possible INzone Savings is wrong.

Why it's wrong
- C22 should be "Cash Flow / Inventory Cost of Money" = (Annual imports ÷ Inventory turns) × Average duty (parts) × Interest rate. The Sample and Text Only sheets use that formula; 'Your Company' does not.

Fix (exact formula to paste into C22)
- Replace the contents of C22 with:
  =(C13/C14)*C15*C17
  (or equivalently = (C13/C14) * C15 * C17 )
