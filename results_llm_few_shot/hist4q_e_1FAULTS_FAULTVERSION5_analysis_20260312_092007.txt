Error type

What’s wrong (short)

Fix (one step)

Tip to avoid this
- When filling similar formulas across many columns, fill/copy the correct cell (instead of retyping ranges) or use consistent relative ranges (e.g., select C9:E11 before SUM) to prevent single-column typos.
