Faulty cell: C19

Error type: Wrong function (mechanical/formula error)

What’s wrong: C19 uses =COUNT(C20:C34). COUNT returns how many numeric cells are in that range (a number of cells), not the sum of hours. That makes the Studio I column-C total — and the overall column total (C35) — incorrect.

Fix (repair formula): replace with =SUM(C20:C34)

Why this fixes it: SUM adds the hours in C20:C34 (what you want). After changing C19 to SUM, the Studio I total and the grand total in C35 will show correct hour totals.  

Tip: Scan for any other COUNT used where you expect totals; use SUM consistently for summing hours.
