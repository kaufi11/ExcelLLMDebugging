1) Sample Company — Cash Flow formula (big numeric error)
- Error: Cell Sample Company C18 is =C9/C10/C11/C13. That divides by the duty rate and interest rate instead of multiplying by them.
- Why it’s wrong: /C11/C13 divides the running result by 0.1 and 0.12, producing a huge, incorrect number.
- Fix: Replace with a multiplication formula:
  - New formula for Sample Company C18: =C9/C10*C11*C13
- Result: (Annual imports / turns) * duty rate * interest rate — yields the correct cash-flow/interest cost (e.g. 150,000 with the sample inputs).
