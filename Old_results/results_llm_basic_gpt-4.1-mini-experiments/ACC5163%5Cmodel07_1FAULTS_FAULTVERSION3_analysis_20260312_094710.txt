**Error(s) Identified:**

1. **Incorrect formula in cell F74:**  
   - Cell F74 (Total Liabilities & Equity) has the formula `=COUNT(F67:F73)`.  
   - This is incorrect because it counts the number of numeric entries instead of summing the values.  
   - It should sum the values to get the total liabilities and equity.

2. **Potential inconsistency in totals proof cells (D76 and E76):**  
   - D76 formula: `=(D65+D74)`  
   - E76 formula: `=(E65+E74)`  
   - These add asset totals and liabilities & equity totals, which should balance (debits = credits).  
   - If F74 is wrong, these totals will be off.

---

**Solution:**

- Change the formula in **F74** from:  
  `=COUNT(F67:F73)`  
  to:  
  `=SUM(F67:F73)`

---

**Explanation:**

- The **COUNT** function counts how many numeric cells are in the range, not the sum of their values.  
- For financial totals, you need to add the amounts, so **SUM** is the correct function.  
- Fixing this will ensure that total liabilities and equity are correctly calculated and balance with total assets.  
- This correction will also make the proof of debit and credit postings (D76 and E76) accurate.
