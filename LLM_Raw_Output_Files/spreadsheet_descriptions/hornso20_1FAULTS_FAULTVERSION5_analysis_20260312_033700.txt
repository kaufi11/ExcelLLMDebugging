1) E20-9
- Layout: inputs in C2:C6 (direct labor hours/cost and overhead components) and C8 (estimated job hours). Calculations in C10:C17.
- Key inputs: Direct labor hours = 16,000; Direct labor cost = 2,240,000; Office rent = 350,000; Support staff salaries = 860,000; Utilities = 246,000; Estimated job hours = 220.
- Formulas:
  - C10 = C3 / C2 → Hourly direct labor rate = 2,240,000 / 16,000 = 140.00
  - C11 = SUM(C4:C6) / C2 → Indirect cost hourly rate = (350,000+860,000+246,000)/16,000 = 91.00
  - C12 = C11 / C10 → Allocation rate ≈ 0.65
  - C14 = (C10 + C11) * C8 → Predicted job cost = (140 + 91) * 220 = 50,820
  - C16 = C14 * 20% = 10,164
  - C17 = C14 + C16 = 60,984 (Total bid)
