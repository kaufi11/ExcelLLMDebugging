1) Incorrect formula (actual bug)
- Cell: X5
- Current formula: =(COUNT(R5:V5)-MIN(R5:V5))/(COUNT(R5:V5)-1)
- Problem: it uses COUNT(...) where SUM(...) is intended, so it computes (count - min)/(count-1) instead of (sum - min)/(count-1). That yields -17.5 and causes the student's Final Grade (AE5) to be wrong.

- Effect of correcting: X5 becomes 95.25 (instead of -17.5) and AE5 recalculates to 87.7225 (instead of 59.535).
