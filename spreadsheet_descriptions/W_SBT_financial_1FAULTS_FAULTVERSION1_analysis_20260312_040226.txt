Summary of workbook structure and key logic

Overall layout / conventions
- Time axis: monthly columns for 36 months. Months 1–12 are in D:O (Year‑1), Year totals in P; months 13–24 in R:AC (Year‑2), Year total in AD; months 25–36 in AF:AQ (Year‑3), Year total in AR. (Column Q and column AS+ are unused for month data.)
- Month-to-month propagation: most row formulas copy the prior month (e.g. E = D, F = E …) so a value entered in the first month flows forward unless changed.
- Year totals: computed with SUM across monthly ranges (e.g. P = SUM(D:O), AD = SUM(R:AC), AR = SUM(AF:AQ)).
- Many category totals are monthly sums across a block of rows (SUM(rowX:rowY) for that column) and annual totals are SUM of monthly totals.

Sheets

1) Instructions
- Title/metadata: Telecentre name (Cash Flows!C2) and model title.
- Purpose: instruction / heading sheet (no detailed formulas presented here).

2) Cash Flows (main model)
- Purpose: builds financing, operating inflows, asset purchases and operating outflows, then computes Net Cash Flow, Cumulative Net Cash, Reserve for Capital Replacement and Unallocated Net Cash.
- Major sections (by row):
  - Financing Cash Flows (rows 9–14): Equity, Loans, Startup grants, Sustaining grants, Sale of assets, etc. Monthly values are entered and year totals computed (P9 = SUM(D9:O9)); row 15 is Total Financing Cash Inflows computed per month as SUM of rows 9:14 (D15 = SUM(D9:D14)) and P15 = SUM(D15:O15).
  - Operating Cash Inflows / Revenues (rows 18–37): many revenue lines (membership, internet subscriptions, telephone, fax, photocopying, training, product sales, etc.). Default first-months are zero; each subsequent month cell references previous (E18 = D18, etc.). Row 38 is Total Operating Revenues per month (D38 = SUM(D18:D37)), year totals similarly in P38/AD38/AR38.
  - Purchase of Assets (rows 42–52): listing asset types (computers, furniture, leasehold improvements, etc.). Totals per month in row 53 (D53 = SUM(D42:D52)), year totals in P53/AD53/AR53.
  - Operating Costs (rows 56–82): personnel (linked from Personnel sheet), vehicle costs, rent, utilities, maintenance, insurance, advertising, goods for resale, loan repayments/interest, taxes etc. Personnel row links: D56 = Personnel!D20 etc. Row 83 totals operating costs (D83 = SUM(D56:D82)).
  - Net Cash Flow (row 85): per month formula = Financing (row15) + Operating revenues (row38) − Asset purchases (row53) − Operating costs (row83). Example: D85 = D15 + D38 − D53 − D83. Year totals in P85/AD85/AR85.
  - Cumulative Net Cash Flow (row 87): running total of Net Cash Flow (D87 = D85, E87 = D87 + E85, …). Year boundaries: P87 = O87 (derived via formula chain) and AD/AR as shown.
  - Reserve for Capital Replacement (row 89): monthly/annual reserve (defaults mostly 0); P89 = SUM(D89:O89).
  - Unallocated Net Cash (row 91): cumulative net less reserves. D91 = D87 − D89, then running additions/subtractions per month. Year totals follow same pattern and P91 = P87 − P89.
- Notable cell/value: many initial monthly inputs are 0 by default; formulas propagate values forward. Cash Flows!C2 contains Telecentre name used elsewhere.

3) Personnel
- Purpose: builds detailed personnel salary schedule, overhead percentage and computes total personnel costs which feed into Cash Flows.
- Structure:
  - Salary rows for positions (Telecentre Manager, Assistant, Marketing Co‑ordinator, Trainers, Technicians etc.). D7:D13 initial months default 0; rows copy forward (E7 = D7 …). One explicit nonzero: Marketing Co‑ordinator E9 = 461 (so month 2 contains 461 and propagates forward).
  - Totals: Total Salaries row 15 sums salary rows per month (D15 = SUM(D7:D14)), year totals in P15 etc.
  - Overhead percentage row 17 (D17 default 0); Overhead costs row 18 multiply salaries by overhead percentage (D18 = D15 * D17).
  - Total Personnel Costs row 20 = Salaries + Overhead (D20 = D15 + D18). These totals are linked into Cash Flows: Cash Flows!D56 = Personnel!D20 etc and similarly for each month/year.
- Purpose in model: Personnel!D20 drives operating cost row Cash Flows!D56 so changes here update operating costs and downstream cash flow results.

4) In‑Kind Contributions
- Purpose: records donated asset contributions and contributions that displace operating costs, aggregated monthly and annually.
- Structure:
  - Contribution of Assets (rows 9–18): same asset categories as Cash Flows; monthly values and P/AD/AR year totals (P9 = SUM(D9:O9) etc).
  - Total Asset Contributions (row 20) sums rows 9:19 per month (D20 = SUM(D9:D19)). Year totals: P20 / AD20 / AR20.
  - Contributions Displacing Operating Costs (rows 23–46): lists items that replace operating costs (personnel, rent, electricity etc). Row 47 totals these per month (D47 = SUM(D23:D46)) and year totals P47/AD47/AR47.
  - Many rows default 0 and use the same month-to-month copy pattern; some rows reference Personnel sheet (row 23 references Personnel data).
- Links: Cash Flows!C2 is used for name; Financial Summary pulls In‑Kind totals from this sheet.

5) Financial Summary
- Purpose: high‑level 3‑year summary drawing key year totals from Cash Flows and In‑Kind sheets.
- Items / links:
  - In‑Kind Contributions: Contribution of Assets and Contributions displacing operating costs pulled from In‑Kind P20/AD20/AR20 and P47/AD47/AR47. Total In‑Kind row sums those two items.
  - Cash Flows section: Total Financing Cash Flows, Revenue from Operations pulled from Cash Flows P15/AD15/AR15 and P38/AD38/AR38. Total Cash Inflows = financing + revenue. Purchase of Assets and Operating Expenses pulled from Cash Flows P53/P83 etc. Total Cash Outflows = sum of those. Net Cash Flow = Total Cash Inflows − Total Cash Outflows. Reserve and Unallocated Cash at year end pull Cash Flows P89/P87 etc.
- All high‑level year figures are cell references to the detailed sheets; no independent calculations beyond simple sums/differences.

Key formulas / mechanics (recap)
- Monthly roll‑forward: each month's cell typically equals previous month (E = D, F = E, …). This creates a propagated time series from the first-month inputs.
- Year totals: P = SUM(D:O), AD = SUM(R:AC), AR = SUM(AF:AQ).
- Category totals: per month SUM across category rows (e.g. D15 = SUM(D9:D14), D38 = SUM(D18:D37), D53 = SUM(D42:D52), D83 = SUM(D56:D82)).
- Net Cash Flow (monthly): Financing + Operating revenues − Asset purchases − Operating costs.
- Personnel totals: Salaries SUM + Overhead (Salaries * Overhead %) -> Personnel!D20 feeds Cash Flows row for personnel costs.

Notes & quick checks
- Many inputs default to 0; to populate a projection enter the intended value in the initial month(s) (usually D cells) or the first non‑zero month and the forward‑copy formulas will propagate.
- To change overhead %, update Personnel!D17 and the overhead and total personnel rows will update and flow into Cash Flows.
- The Financial Summary provides a top‑level check: its year cells are direct links to the detailed sheets (useful for presentation and verification).
