4) General State Financial Aid
- Title/period: "General State Financial Aid by Iowa Community College" — FY1999–2000.

5) FTEE Calculation
- Title/period: "FTEE Calculation by Iowa Community College" — FY1999–2000.
- Layout: multi-column detailed conversion: credit hours (B), credit FTEE (C = B/24), percent of college credit vs total (D = C / H-row), non-credit contact hours (E), non-credit FTEE (F = E/600), non-credit percent of college (G = F / H-row), total FTEE per college (H = C + F), percent of total (I = H / $H$27). Each college has a row; totals on row 27.
- Totals and adjustments: B27 = SUM(B11:B26); C27 = SUM(C11:C26); E27 = SUM(E11:E26) + 1 (there is a +1 adjustment); H27 = SUM(H11:H26); I27 = SUM(I11:I26).
- Notes: some hardcoded expressions (e.g., F14 = 829/600) and asterisks about rounding and FY97–98 FTEE usage.
- Purpose: converts credit and non-credit activity into FTEE and computes each college’s share of system FTEE.

6) FTEE Graph
- Title/period: chart/figure (Figure 25) tied to FTEE Calculation sheet; contains source note.

Key formulas and patterns
- Percent formulas uniformly compute category percent = category dollars / total dollars (e.g., =C9/$C$15 or =C9/$C$19).
- Totals use SUM across the relevant data rows.
- FTEE conversions: credit FTEE = credit hours / 24; non-credit FTEE = contact hours / 600; total college FTEE = credit FTEE + non-credit FTEE; percent of total = college total FTEE / grand total FTEE (H27).
- A few cells contain inline arithmetic or ad-hoc +1 adjustments (C9, C11, B21, E27), and one formula is hard-coded (F14 = 829/600) instead of referencing a cell.
- Rounding notes and source citations appear on each sheet.
