Summary of errors and how to fix them (short & simple)

1) Missing / mis‑placed journal entry for collections (transaction e) — entries e and f are swapped
- Problem: Your sheet shows the $1,700 sales allowance where "e" (collections) should be. The cash collections of $28,000 were not recorded; the sales allowance entry is labeled under the wrong transaction letter.
- Fix: Create two separate journal entries and label them correctly:
  - For e (Collections): Debit Cash $28,000; Credit Accounts Receivable $28,000.
    - Example cells: Debit column = 28000, Credit column (A/R) = 28000.
  - For f (Sales allowance): Debit Sales Returns & Allowances $1,700; Credit Accounts Receivable $1,700.
- Why: Collections reduce A/R and increase Cash. Sales allowance reduces revenue (SRA) and reduces A/R. Separating them keeps the ledger and Net Sales correct.

2) Part B — wrong formula/value in E91 (extended cost for 12/24 purchase)
- Problem: Cell E91 shows "=2" or otherwise incorrect value instead of units*unit price for the 2,000 @ $8.25 purchase.
- Fix: Change E91 to =C91*D91 (which will give 2000 * 8.25 = 16,500).
- Why: All extended cost rows must be units × unit price to compute correct GAFS.

3) Part B — weighted‑average calculations inconsistent (EI and COGS wrong)
- Problem: The sheet shows Ending Inventory or COGS values that do not match the weighted average per‑unit price (example: COGS shown 60,735 but correct is 60,375).
- Fix (clear, robust formulas):
  - Compute totals: Units total (C92) = SUM(C88:C91). Cost total (E92) = SUM(E88:E91).
  - Per‑unit weighted average (use an empty helper cell, e.g. D108): =E92/C92 → gives 8.05.
  - Ending inventory (EI) for weighted average (use cell for EI, e.g. J110 or C83): =D108 * C93 (or =D108*2500) → 20,125.
  - Weighted COGS: =E92 - (D108 * C93) → 60,375.
- Why: Weighted-average per unit must be calculated from total cost / total units, then multiplied by EI units. Hard‑coded or incorrect numbers caused the mismatch.

4) Cosmetic / minor issues
- Some stray helper cells (K-column values like K48, L91) are unused or confusing. Remove or relocate them to a “work” area.
- Some date labels show 2002 instead of 2004 — cosmetic only; not affecting calculations.
