High-level overview
- Purpose: National Food Industry export database (values in $m) covering financial years 1999-00 to 2001-02, plus 6‑month periods to Dec 2001 and Dec 2002. Source: National Food Industry Strategy Ltd, compiled from ABS data. Notes: ABS does not publish barley/rice by some breakdowns; wheat by country excluded.

Sheets and contents (concise)
1. Main Menu
  - Navigation/cover page with titles, update date (March 2003) and links/headings to the main reports and charts.

2. Summary
  - One‑page headline extract. Pulls key metrics from other sheets via direct links (e.g. D4 ='By Sector'!I14). Presents sector, processing‑level and major‑destination summary figures and % changes. Includes source citation.

3. By Sector
  - Detailed sector-level table: columns for 1999‑00, 2000‑01, 2001‑02 (C:E), 6 months to Dec 01 (G), 6 months to Dec 02 (H) and % change (I).
  - Key formulas: I-column uses percent change =(H?-G?)/G?. Totals row (B14/C14/E14 etc.) and sector totals available.
  - Key totals: Total All Sectors — 1999‑00: 18,855,268,979; 2001‑02: 24,639,069,381; 6 months to Dec 02 (H14): 11,437,615,107.

4. By Level of Processing
  - Same layout as By Sector but broken into Unprocessed / Simply processed / Highly processed by sector.
  - Percent changes in J-column use =(I?-H?)/H?.
  - Aggregate totals (e.g. Total All Food H44 = sum of H values by product groups).

5. Sector by Country
  - Country‑by‑sector matrices. For each country, values for the three years and 6‑month periods, plus % change.
  - Many cells are calculated as SUM across sector rows (e.g. C6 = SUM(C37,C67,...), G/H columns likewise). There are regional totals (Total Asia) and Total All Countries.
  - Note: explicit exclusion of wheat by country.

6. Processed Food Exports
  - Detailed product‑category breakdown of processed exports (dairy, meat products, cereal products, juices, confectionery, beverages, etc.) with the same year/6‑month/% change structure.
  - Percent change formula pattern repeated: (H−G)/G.

7. By Broad Category
  - Aggregated product‑category summary (higher level grouping). H18 (6 months Dec 02 total) is computed as =SUM(G5:G17) in this sheet and mirrors totals elsewhere; % changes calculated similarly.

8. Charts
  - Chart data area pulling values directly from 'By Sector' and 'Sector by Country' (e.g. R5 ='By Sector'!G5; S5 ='By Sector'!H5; T5 = 'By Sector'!I5).
  - Two main chart groups: exports by sector and exports by major destination. Values are fed by formulas so charts update when source data changes.

Key formula patterns & links
- Percent change: (H − G) / G used widely (columns I or J).
- Totals by category/country: SUM across component rows or SUM of referenced cells (many inter‑sheet references).
- Summary and Charts sheets pull indicators from the detailed sheets (By Sector, By Level of Processing, Sector by Country) using direct cell references.

Important notes
- Unit is $ (millions) throughout.
- Time windows: full financial years (1999‑00 to 2001‑02) and 6 months to Dec 2001 / Dec 2002.
- Data cross‑references: Summary & Charts are driven entirely by the detailed sheets; changing values in By Sector / Sector by Country / Processed Food Exports will cascade into Summary and Charts automatically.
