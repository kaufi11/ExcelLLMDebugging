Findings (short)
- Faulty cell: I21
- Error: Typo in formula — it uses a constant 116 instead of the weight cell C21.
- Effect: Oracle's weighted score is massively inflated (H21*116 = 116 instead of H21*C21 = 3).

Fix
- Replace the formula in I21 with:
  =H21*C21

Why this fixes it (short)
- The weighted-score column should multiply the vendor score (column H) by the criterion weight (column C). Using 116 is a mistyped constant, so correcting to C21 produces the correct weighted contribution (1 * 3 = 3).

Extra recommendations (short)
