   - COUNT counts the number of numeric entries (cells with numbers), not the sum of values.
   - This causes E20 to show 2 (because both C20=112 and D20=0 are numbers) instead of 112 (sum of 112+0).

2. All other sheets correctly use `=SUM(Cx:Dx)` in column E for totals.

Solution:

- Change KauCC!E20 formula from `=COUNT(C20:D20)` to `=SUM(C20:D20)`.

Explanation:

- SUM adds the values in the range, giving the correct total of full-text and PDF articles viewed.
- COUNT only counts how many numeric cells exist, not their values, so it does not provide the correct total.
