Summary — main errors found
1) M3 has a clear typo: =L3/944 (should not divide by 944).  
2) Most averages in column M (M3:M45) are calculated as Lx/10 (fixed divisor 10). Several rows have missing score cells in B:K, so dividing by 10 produces a wrong (too low) average.  
3) Some rows have missing score cells (blanks) in B:K (examples: rows 4, 11, 25, 27, 28, 42, 45). SUM(B:K) treats blanks as zero, so L is reduced and M (with /10) is incorrect.

Immediate fixes (copy down as needed)
- Fix M3 typo:
  Replace =L3/944 with =AVERAGE(B3:K3)
- Replace every Mx =Lx/10 with either:
  a) =AVERAGE(Bx:Kx)  — simplest (ignores blank cells and returns the average of the present numeric scores), or
  b) =IF(COUNT(Bx:Kx)=0,"",Lx/COUNT(Bx:Kx)) — if you prefer to keep Lx and compute average as sum/count of numeric cells.

Why this fixes the problems (short)
- =AVERAGE(Bx:Kx) automatically ignores blanks, so the average is correct even if some scores are missing.  
- Using COUNT(Bx:Kx) to divide the SUM uses the actual number of numeric inputs instead of a hard-coded 10.  
- Fixing M3 removes the obvious typo divisor 944.
