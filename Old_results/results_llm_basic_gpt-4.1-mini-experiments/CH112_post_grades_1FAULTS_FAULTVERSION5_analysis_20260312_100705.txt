Error:
In cell O46, the formula is incorrect:
`=(COUNT(B46:F46)+COUNT(G46:M46)+N46)/(COUNT(B46:F46)*100+COUNT(G46:J46)*15+COUNT(K46:M46)*30+COUNT(N46)*150)*100`
It uses COUNT instead of SUM for the numerator, which counts non-empty cells instead of summing the scores. This will give wrong overall grade calculation.

Solution:
Change the formula in O46 to use SUM instead of COUNT in the numerator, like the other rows:
`=(SUM(B46:F46)+SUM(G46:M46)+N46)/(COUNT(B46:F46)*100+COUNT(G46:J46)*15+COUNT(K46:M46)*30+COUNT(N46)*150)*100`

Explanation:
- SUM adds the actual scores.
- COUNT counts how many scores exist (used for weighting denominator).
- Using COUNT in numerator counts cells, not scores, causing wrong results.

No other errors found in the formulas for overall grades or letter grades.
