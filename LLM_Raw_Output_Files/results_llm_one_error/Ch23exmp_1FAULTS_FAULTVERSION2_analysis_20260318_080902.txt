Formulas!C30 — formula is =-B7 but should be =B7.  
Error: beginning inventory is wrongly negated, which flips Cost of Goods Available and COGS (and thus gross margin, taxes, net income).  
Fix: remove the minus sign (change to =B7). This makes beginning inventory positive and corrects downstream C32, C34, C18, C19, C25, C26, C27.
