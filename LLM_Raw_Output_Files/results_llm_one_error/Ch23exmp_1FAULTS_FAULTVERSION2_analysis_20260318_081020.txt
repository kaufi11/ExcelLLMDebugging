Cell C30: formula is =-B7 but should be =B7

Problem: Beginning inventory is entered as a negative, so Cost of Goods Available (C32), Ending Inventory (C33) and COGS (C34) are all wrong.

Fix: Replace =-B7 with =B7 (or =+B7). This makes beginning inventory add to purchases instead of subtracting, correcting downstream Gross Margin, Operating Income and Net Income.
