Faulty cell: X5

Error type: Mechanical / copy–paste typo

What's wrong: X5 contains =(COUNT(R5:V5)-MIN(R5:V5))/(COUNT(R5:V5)-1). The numerator uses COUNT instead of SUM, so it subtracts the minimum from the number of quizzes (a small integer) instead of from the sum of quiz scores. That makes the "quiz average (drop lowest)" wrong and therefore AE5 (Final Grade) wrong.

Fix (repair formula):
= (SUM(R5:V5) - MIN(R5:V5)) / (COUNT(R5:V5) - 1)

Why this fixes it: SUM(R5:V5)-MIN(R5:V5) gives the total of the quizzes after dropping the lowest score; dividing by COUNT(R5:V5)-1 yields the average of the remaining quizzes.

Tip: after fixing X5, AE5 will recalculate correctly. If you expect to copy the formula, make sure you paste the corrected version (the other rows already have the correct formula).
