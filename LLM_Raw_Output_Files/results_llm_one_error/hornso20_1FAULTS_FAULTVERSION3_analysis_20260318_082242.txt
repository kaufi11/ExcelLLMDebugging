Cell: Sheet "E20-9 (2)" — C11

Error: Formula uses =SUM(C4:C6)*C2 (multiplying total indirect costs by hours) which massively inflates the indirect hourly rate.

Solution: Change formula to =SUM(C4:C6)/C2

Why: Indirect cost hourly rate = total indirect costs ÷ total direct labor hours. Using * instead of ÷ overstates the rate and will produce incorrect allocation (and wrong job cost, mark-up, and total bid). After fixing C11, Excel will correctly update C12, C14, C16 and C17.
