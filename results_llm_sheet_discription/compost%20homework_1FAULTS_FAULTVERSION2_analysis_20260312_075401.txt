Findings (short)
- Error: Bulk compost C19 has a typo. It uses =A19+(B19/783)-1.48 instead of dividing the hour by 24.
- Effect: B19/783 = 0.01629 vs B19/24 = 0.53125, so C19 is ≈0.515 day (~12.36 hours) earlier than it should be. That point will be plotted/analysed at the wrong time.
- Possible secondary issue to check: the two sheets use different subtraction offsets (-1.48 vs -1.71). That may be intentional (different time anchors) — verify.

Fix (exact)
- Correct C19 to:
  =A19 + (B19/24) - 1.48

1. Select C19, replace formula with =A19+(B19/24)-1.48.

  This makes it easy to change/check and avoids hard-coded numbers.
