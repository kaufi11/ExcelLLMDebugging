Cell C19 — Error: formula uses COUNT(C20:C34) instead of SUM(C20:C34).

Why it’s wrong:
- COUNT returns the number of numeric cells (15) not the total hours (should be 10). This makes the column C Studio I subtotal and the overall Week 5 total incorrect.

Fix:
- Replace formula in C19 with =SUM(C20:C34)

Explanation:
- SUM adds the values (hours); COUNT only counts how many numeric entries exist. Changing to SUM gives the correct hours subtotal and fixes the Week 5 totals.
