Summary — one clear error found

- Error cell: C22 (Carrying Capacity).
- Problem: the formula multiplies by B15/2 (character weight) instead of by the character’s Strength. That produces an inflated carrying capacity (2397) and so the HTH base damage lookup returns the wrong result (1d12).
- Cause: wrong cell reference used (B15) where B16 (Strength) was intended.

Fix (one-line)
- Replace the formula in C22 with:
  =((B16/10)^3 + (G16/10)) * (B16/2)

Why this fixes it (simple)
- The formula appears intended to compute carrying capacity from Strength and Endurance. Using Strength (B16) in the final multiplier makes the result scale with the character’s Strength instead of with the character’s body weight.
- With Strength = 30 and Endurance = 12:
  - Old (wrong) result: ((30/10)^3 + 1.2) * (170/2) = 28.2 * 85 = 2397 → maps to HTH 1d12 (wrongly high).
  - New (correct) result: ((30/10)^3 + 1.2) * (30/2) = 28.2 * 15 = 423 → maps to HTH 1d6 (reasonable).
