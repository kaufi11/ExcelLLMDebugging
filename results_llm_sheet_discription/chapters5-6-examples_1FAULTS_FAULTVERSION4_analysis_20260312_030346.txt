Quick troubleshooting tips
- Use absolute references for parameter cells (Sheet1!$B$23 etc.) — you already mostly do this for Sheet1 but check the ones that were missing $.
- After copying formulas across rows/sheets, scan for any references that point to other sheets (Sheet2!, Sheet1!B24, Sheet1!B25…) — those are likely mistakes.
- To find bad references quickly: Home → Find (Ctrl+F) search for "Sheet2!" or "Sheet1!B2" style offsets.
