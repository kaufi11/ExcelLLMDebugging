Overview
- Workbook is a standard FFU (Financial Forecast Update) template with five sheets: Declaration - Annex B, Commentary - Annex C, Table 1.0 I & E - Annex D (Income & Expenditure), Table 2.0 BS - Annex E (Balance Sheet) and Table 3.0 CF - Annex F (Cash Flow).
- Layout convention: descriptive labels down column C (and nearby), numeric inputs and results in columns H (Original FFR Forecast), I (Mid‑Year Revised Outturn), J (Variance = I–H), K (Variance % = IF(H=0,"N/A",(J/H)*100)), and L for reasons where material. Monetary amounts are in £000 (stated in headers). Most numeric input cells are initialized to 0.

Sheet summaries and key formulas
1) Declaration - Annex B
- Purpose: signature/declaration page. Contains college name, address, contact, and a short declaration text.
- Cells B26/D26/J26 hold signature, title and date text inputs.

2) Commentary - Annex C
- Purpose: free‑text financial commentary linked to Table 1.0 header.
- A4 links to Table 1.0 A4 (='Table 1.0 I & E - Annex D'!A4). Prompts user to explain material changes and flags that the commentary must be completed.

3) Table 1.0 I & E - Annex D (Income & Expenditure)
- Structure: income rows (H9:H14), total income H15 = SUM(H9:H14); expenditure rows (H18:H22), total expenditure H23 = SUM(H18:H22); surplus H25 = H15-H23. Subsequent rows add gains/losses, tax, minority interest and produce final surplus (e.g. H31 = H25+H28-H29, H38 = H31-H34-H36).
- Variance and percent columns use J = I–H and K = IF(H=0,"N/A",(J/H)*100).
- Bursary/access section and transfers/ reserves included. Many row numbers use formulas like =B15+1 to auto‑increment numbering.

4) Table 2.0 BS - Annex E (Balance Sheet)
- Structure: Fixed assets (H7:H12; H13 = SUM(H7:H12)), current assets (H18:H22; H23 = SUM(H18:H22)), creditors due within one year (H26:H32; H33 = SUM(H26:H32)), net current assets H35 = H23-H33, total assets less current liabilities H37 = H13+H15+H35.
- Long‑term creditors, provisions and net assets H49 = H37-H44-H47.
- Deferred capital grants H52:H55 summed to H56; total funds H69 = H56+H59+H67. Reconciliation check H74 = H49-H69 (labelled "THESE CELLS MUST BE ZERO").
- Variance and percent logic same as Table 1.0.

5) Table 3.0 CF - Annex F (Operating Cash Flow, monthly)
- Two sections: ORIGINAL CASH FORECAST (columns H:S monthly Aug→Jul) and ACTUAL/REVISED FORECAST (same monthly layout later in sheet).
- Cash in (rows for SFEFC funding, other grants, other cash), totals per month (e.g. H10 = SUM(H7:H9)); cash out (payroll, other payments, interest) and monthly net cash inflow H17 = H10-H15+H16.
- Running balances: balance c/f H19 = SUM(H17:H18), and monthly roll‑forwards use formulas linking successive months (I18 = H19, J18 = I19, etc.).
- Financing section reconciles cash to financing; row H25..T25 set as H24-H19 etc and flagged "These cells should be zero". Cross‑sheet reconciliations: T26 = T19 - ('Table 2.0 BS - Annex E'!H20+'Table 2.0 BS - Annex E'!H21+'Table 2.0 BS - Annex E'!H22) for original, and T49 does the same for revised (referencing I20,I21,I22).

Other notable points
- Consistent formulas for variance, percentage and totals across sheets.
- Many input cells default to 0; reason columns prompt explanations when variances are material (> £50k and >10%).
- Several header cells and the A1 title are linked across sheets (e.g. several sheets use ='Table 1.0 I & E - Annex D'!A1 or A4).
- Template includes built‑in validation checks (zero checks and reconciliations) to help detect inconsistencies between I&E, Balance Sheet and Cash Flow.
