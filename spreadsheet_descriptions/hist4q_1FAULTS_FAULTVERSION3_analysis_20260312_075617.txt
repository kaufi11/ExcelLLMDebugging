Summary of workbook structure and contents

General
- Workbook contains 3 sheets:
  - Sheet "0": Title/index and notes (目次、注記). Notes state negative numbers shown in parentheses and accounting restatements. Timestamp present (B25: 2004-01-29).
  - Sheet "1": "1. 四半期実績推移(1)" — product-level sales, simplified income statement, and region sales by quarter (units: 百万円).
  - Sheet "2": "2. 四半期実績推移(2)" — business-segment information (sales, operating profit and margins) by quarter.

Layout and common structure
- Columns C–R represent four years (2000–2003) with each year split into 4 quarters (列 C–F = 2000 Q1–Q4, G–J = 2001 Q1–Q4, K–N = 2002 Q1–Q4, O–R = 2003 Q1–Q4).
- Monetary values are in millions of yen (百万円). Negative values appear as negatives (note indicates shown in parentheses).
- Many totals computed with SUM across categories/quarters; ratios use IF(...) to avoid divide-by-zero.

Key formula patterns and links
- Repeating use of SUM to aggregate category totals (e.g., SUM of product rows to form totals).
- Profit-flow calculations: Net sales → Cost of sales → Gross profit → SG&A → Operating profit → Add non-op items → Pre-tax profit → Tax/minority adjustments → Net profit.
- Ratio formulas always guard against division by zero using IF(...).
- Many cells are direct links between sections (e.g., C17 = C15).
