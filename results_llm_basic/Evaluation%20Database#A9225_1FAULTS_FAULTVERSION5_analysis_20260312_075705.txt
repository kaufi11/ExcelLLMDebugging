Summary of the error
- H19 is wrong. It uses =COUNT(H7:H17) but should sum the responses. COUNT returns how many numeric cells are in H7:H17 (9), not the total of those numbers (313).

Why this is wrong
- You want the column total (TOTAL row B19) for the "Very Good" column. COUNT only counts entries; SUM adds their values.

How to fix
1. Select H19.
2. Replace the formula with:
