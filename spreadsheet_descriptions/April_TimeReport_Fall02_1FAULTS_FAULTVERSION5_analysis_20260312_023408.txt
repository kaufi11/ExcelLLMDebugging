Sheet name: Reporting

Layout and headings
- Row 1: Week labels (B1:M1 = Week 4 … Week 15); Row 2: corresponding date ranges (B2:M2).
- Columns B–M: weekly hours by task; Column N: row-level Total (formula = sum of B:M for that row).
- Rows grouped by high-level categories: Management (rows 3–6), Models (7–10), Requirements (11–14), Elective (15–18), Studio I (19–34). Row 35 is the overall weekly/section total.

Formulas and aggregation
- Category header rows (e.g., B3, B7, B11, B15, B19) mostly use SUM of their sub-rows for each week (e.g., B3 = SUM(B4:B6), B7 = SUM(B8:B10), etc.).
- Each task row’s Column N is computed as N# = SUM(B#:M#).
- Section total row 35 sums the category header rows per week (e.g., B35 = B3+B7+B11+B15+B19).
- Final total cell N35 = N3+N7+N11+N15+N19 (grand total of all hours).
