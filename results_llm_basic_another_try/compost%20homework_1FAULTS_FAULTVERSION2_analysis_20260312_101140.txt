Error found:

In the Bulk compost sheet, cell C19 has the formula:
`=A19+(B19/783)-1.48`

This is incorrect because the divisor should be 24 (hours in a day) to convert hours to fraction of a day, but here it is 783, which is wrong.

Solution:

Change the formula in C19 to:
`=A19+(B19/24)-1.48`

Explanation:

- The formula calculates "Days from" by adding the day number (A19) plus the fraction of the day represented by hours (B19/24), then subtracting 1.48 as an offset.
- Using 783 instead of 24 incorrectly scales the hour fraction, leading to wrong date/time values.
- Correcting the divisor to 24 fixes the calculation to properly convert hours to days.
