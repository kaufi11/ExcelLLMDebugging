Cell: Balance Sheet!E7

Error: Formula uses E2 (the year/label) in =ROUND(E2+E6,5), so Total Assets is inflated by the year number (e.g. +1999).

Fix: Replace with =ROUND(E6,5) (or =ROUND(SUM(E6:otherAssetCells),5) if you have additional asset rows). Then copy the corrected formula across F7, G7, H7.

Why: E6 already contains Total Current Assets; removing E2 stops adding the year as a numeric value and produces the correct Total Assets.
