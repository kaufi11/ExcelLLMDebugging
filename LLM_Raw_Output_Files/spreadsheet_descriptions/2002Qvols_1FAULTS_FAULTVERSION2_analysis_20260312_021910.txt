Sheet name and purpose
- Sheet2 — "RSA 2002 QUARTERLY CUMULATIVE FUEL SALES IN LITERS BY MARKET CATEGORY AND PROVINCE": a detailed 2002 fuel sales register by market category and province, reporting quarterly product volumes and cumulative totals.

Overall layout
- Title rows: A1 contains the sheet title; row 4 shows quarter labels (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4) spanning groups of columns.
- Row 5 contains repeated product headings for each quarter and cumulative blocks (Diesel gas oil, Illuminating paraffin, LPG, Petrol, Quarter/ Cumulative totals).
- Columns:
  - A = Category name (market segment)
  - B = Province
  - C–G = Q1 detail (C–F = product volumes, G = Q1 total)
  - H–L = Q2 detail (H–K = product volumes, L = Q2 total)
  - M–Q = Q2 cumulative (M–P = cumulative product totals after Q2, Q = cumulative total for all products after Q2)
  - R–V = Q3 detail (R–U = product volumes, V = Q3 total)
  - W–AA = Q3 cumulative (W–Z = cumulative product totals after Q3, AA = cumulative total after Q3)
  - AI–AL = Q4 product columns (AI–AL used for Q4 product volumes / final cumulative assemblies)
  - There are auxiliary columns (AB–AD, AE–AH, AG etc.) used repeatedly for a second block of breakdowns (codes, repeated category/province and additional intermediate totals).
- Rows: groups of provinces per market category (e.g., Construction rows 6–14, then "Construction Total" row 15), repeated for many market categories (General Dealers, Government, Indep. LPG Marketers, Local marine fishing, Mining, Public Passenger Bus Transport, General Commercial & Industrial, Retail - garages, Road Freight, Transnet, Agricultural Co-ops & Farmers, Local Authorities). Final row 133 = Grand Total.

Key formula patterns
- Quarter totals: Gx = SUM(Cx:Fx), Lx = SUM(Hx:Kx), Vx = SUM(Rx:Ux), etc.
- Cumulative by product: Mx = Cx + Hx (Q1 + Q2 diesel), Nx = Dx + Ix, Ox = Ex + Jx, Px = Fx + Kx.
- Cumulative totals: Qx = Gx + Lx (total after two quarters), AA x = Qx + Vx (total after three quarters).
- Further cumulative progression: Wx = Mx + Rx (previous cumulative diesel + Q3 diesel), Xx = Nx + Sx, Yx = Ox + Tx, Zx = Px + Ux.
- Annual/category roll-ups: AI (and AJ/AK/AL) cells compute cumulative/annual product totals per row by adding the appropriate cumulative columns (e.g., AI6 = AE6 + W6 in the template). Category subtotal rows sum their province rows (example: AI15 = SUM(AI6:AI14)).
- Grand totals: row 133 aggregates all categories; AI133..AL133 are computed by summing category totals (AI15 + AI25 + ... + AI132, etc).
