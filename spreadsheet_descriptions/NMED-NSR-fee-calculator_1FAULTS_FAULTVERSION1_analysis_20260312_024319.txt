Calculations sheet — layout and purpose
- Title/metadata: A1 (“Points-Based Fee Calculator”), A2 (reg citation). A4 prompts for Permit Number & Site Name.
- Key input columns:
  - Column A: Permitting actions / descriptors (e.g., Emission Units, Fugitive Emissions, PSD, BACT, etc.).
  - Column B: user inputs — either a count (e.g., # of units or # of regs) or an “x”/symbol to indicate presence/requirement.
  - Column C: calculated Total Points for each action (formulas shown).
  - Columns E–G: list of non‑applicable regulations / citations (NMAC parts, NSPS, NESHAP text).

- Fee settings and outputs:
  - B5 label “Fee ($/point)”; C5 = 315 (dollar rate per point).
  - E5 labeled “Total Fee”. F5 contains: =IF(C43="N/A",C42,C43) — selects either C42 (full fee) or C43 (half fee / N/A logic) to display final fee.
  - A41 Total Points: C41 formula =IF(OR(C34>0,C35>0),C36,COUNT(C9:C31)) — uses TOTAL OTHER ACTIONS if any “other actions” present, otherwise counts point cells C9:C31.
  - A42 Fee: C42 = C41 * C5 (total fee = points × $/point).
  - A43 Half fee for small business: C43 = IF(B34<>"","N/A",IF(B38<>"",C42/2,"N/A")) — returns half fee if small business box (B38) is marked and B34 (general permit) is blank; otherwise “N/A”.

- Important per-line scoring rules (selected):
  - Emission Units (A9): C9 = IF(B9<>"",IF(B9<5,5,IF(B9>15,15,B9)),0) — minimum 5, maximum 15, equals B9 if between 5–15.
  - Fugitive Emissions (A11): C11 = IF(B11<>"",5,0).
  - Non-attainment Area (A12): C12 = IF(B12<>"",75,0).
  - Modeling Review (A13): C13 = IF(B13<>"",15,0).
  - Air Toxics / Health Assessment (A19): C19 = IF(B19<>"",100,0).
  - Level 1 (A16): C16 = IF(B16<>"",8,0).
  - BACT (A18): C18 = IF(B18<>"",60,0).
  - Applicable regulations (A22–A24):
    - 20 NMAC 2.X: C22 = IF(B22<>"",(3*B22),0) — 3 points per reg.
    - NSPS: C23 = IF(B23<>"",(5*B23),0) — 5 points per NSPS.
    - NESHAPs: C24 = IF(B24<>"",(5*B24),0) — 5 points per NESHAP.
  - Case-by-case MACT (A27): C27 = IF(B27<>"",100,0).
  - PSD / PSD netting:
    - PSD Netting (no other analysis) A30: C30 = IF(B31="",(IF(B30<>"",20,0)),"") — gives 20 if B30 indicated and B31 blank.
    - PSD review including netting A31: C31 = IF(B31<>"",75,0).
  - Other actions:
    - General Permit A34: C34 = IF(B35="",(IF(B34<>"",10,0)),"") — returns 10 when B34 indicated and B35 blank.
    - Streamlined sites A35: C35 = IF(B35<>"",(B35*10),0) — 10 points per site.
    - Total Other Actions A36 = SUM(C34:C35).

- User instructions and notes:
  - A47–A48: prompts to insert numbers of units/regs and mark “X” for applicable fee points.
  - A38–A39: small business indicator — put “x” in B38 to qualify for half fee (subject to B34 general permit exclusion).
