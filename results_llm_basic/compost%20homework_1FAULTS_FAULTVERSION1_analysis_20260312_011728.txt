Findings
- On sheet "Bulk compost" only C5 is wrong. Its formula uses +1.48 instead of -1.48: =A5+(B5/24)+1.48. Every other row in that column uses -1.48.
- Effect: C5 is shifted by 2.96 days (≈71 hours). Example: with A5=2 and B5=10 the wrong formula gives 3.8967, the correct one should give 0.9367 — a large, incorrect time shift.

Quick fix
- Replace the formula in C5 with the correct one: =A5+(B5/24)-1.48
- Or copy a correctly working cell (e.g. C4) and paste/formula-fill down to C5.

How to avoid this in future (recommended)
- Put the offset (1.48) in one cell (e.g. Z1 = 1.48) and use a single reference:
  =A5 + (B5/24) - $Z$1
  That prevents sign/typing mistakes and makes global changes easy.
- Optionally use Conditional Formatting or a quick check like =ABS(Cn - (An + Bn/24 - 1.48))>1 to flag outliers.
