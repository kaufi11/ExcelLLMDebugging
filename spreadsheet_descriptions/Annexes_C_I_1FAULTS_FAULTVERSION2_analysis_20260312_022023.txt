Summary of workbook structure and contents — concise

Overall layout / common conventions
- Workbook is a Financial Forecast Update pack for 2002/03 using standard Annex tabs (C–I).
- Many sheets use the same column structure: H = Original FFR forecast, I = Mid‑year revised outturn, J = Variance (I–H), K = Variance % (IF(H=0,"N/A",(J/H)*100)), L = Reason for material variances.
- College name is stored in Declaration - AnnexC!C3 and is pulled into other sheets by formula (e.g. 'Declaration - AnnexC'!C3).
- Line numbers in column B are often auto‑generated by formulas like =previous+1.
- Most numeric cells are initialized to 0; many totals and variance cells use SUM and simple subtraction formulas.

Sheet-by-sheet summary

1) Declaration - AnnexC
- Title and return deadline. Fields for College Name (C3), Address (C5:C9), Contact (C11), Telephone (C13), e‑mail (C15).
- Declaration text and signature/date area (A19, A26:B26, D26, H26, J26).
- Serves as the central source of the college name used elsewhere ('Declaration - AnnexC'!C3).

2) Commentary - Annex D
- Pulls college name from Declaration (A4 = 'Declaration - AnnexC'!C3).
- Box for financial commentary instructions and requirement notes (A6–A11).

3) annex e — Income and Expenditure Account (Consolidated)
- Rows of income (C9:C15) and expenditure (C18:C23) line items with H/I amounts and J = I–H.
- Totals: H15/I15 = SUM(H9:H14) etc.; surplus lines: H25 = H15–H23; subsequent adjustments for gains/losses to arrive at post‑tax surplus (H31, H38).
- Variance % formula pattern: Kx = IF(Hx=0,"N/A",(Jx/Hx)*100).
- Bursary/hardship section (C56:C61) with over/under calculations.
- Many intermediate totals use SUM ranges and direct references to replicate standard I&E format.

4) annex f — Consolidated Balance Sheet
- Fixed assets (C7:C13), current assets (C18:C22), creditors short/long term, provisions, total net assets and funds.
- Key totals: H13 = SUM(H7:H12); H22 = SUM(H18:H21); H32 = SUM(H25:H31); H34 = H22–H32 (Net current assets); H36 = H13+H15+H34; H48 = H36–H43–H46 (Total Net Assets) etc.
- Deferred capital grants (H51:H55 = SUM); Endowments H58 = H15; Total Funds H68 = H55+H58+H66.
- Reconciliation check: H73 = H48–H68 (labelled "THESE CELLS MUST BE ZERO") and equivalent for revised outturns; K73 pulls K48.
- Uses same variance and IF formulas for percentages.

5) annex g — Operating Cash Flow (Consolidated)
- Two parts: ORIGINAL CASH FORECAST (columns H:S, monthly Aug–Jul, T = total) and ACTUAL/REVISED FORECAST (rows H28:S...).
- Income rows (SFEFC funding, other grants, other cash) with monthly entries, totals by month and overall (T = SUM(H:S)).
- Expenditure rows (Payroll, Other payments, Interest) and totals (Total cash out).
- Net cash inflow per month = cash in − cash out + deposits (row 17). Cumulative balances: balance b/f (row 18) and balance c/f (row 19) built month to month (I18 = H19 etc.).
- Financing section reconciles cash to balance sheet cash lines: T24 = SUM financing; row 25/48 are "These cells should be zero" (H25 = H24–H19 etc.).
- Cross‑sheet reconciliation formulas: T26 = T19 - ('annex f'!H20+'annex f'!H21) (reconciles total cash c/f to annex f cash/investments lines). Similar T49 for revised figures referencing annex f I20/I21.
- Summary rows compute month‑by‑month differences between revised and original closing balances and percentage variances (row 51/53).

6) Annex H and Annex H(1)
- Annex H: template for reporting impact of a specific grant (FE/54/02) with fields to allocate grant across Annex D (income & expenditure) and Annex E (balance sheet).
- Annex H(1): worked example populated with sample amounts (Total funding I17 = 90,000; distribution: Funding Council grants I21=60,000; Exceptional restructuring costs I23=40,000; Other operating expenses I25=20,000; Balance sheet entries I31 cash at bank=30,000 and I33 deferred capital grants=30,000).
- Purpose: show how to map grant to I&E and balance sheet.

7) Annex I — Underlying operating surplus/deficit statement
- Pulls Surplus/(Deficit) after depreciation from annex e: H11 = 'annex e'!I25 (mid‑year revised outturn surplus).
- Adds back exceptional items: H14 = 'annex e'!I19, plus fields for SSAP 24 and other one‑off items.
- Underlying operating surplus/deficit H27 = SUM(H10:H26).

Key cross‑sheet links and checks
- 'Declaration - AnnexC'!C3 used as college identifier in multiple sheets.
- annex e ↔ annex f & annex g are linked by totals: cash in annex g reconciles to annex f cash/investments (annex g T26/T49 referencing annex f H20/H21 and I20/I21).
- Reconciliation cells explicitly flag mismatches ("THESE CELLS MUST BE ZERO" and "These cells should be zero").
- Consistent defensive formulas for percent variance to avoid divide‑by‑zero (IF(H=0,"N/A",...)).

Notes on current contents
- Most numeric inputs are 0; many totals and variances will therefore show 0 or "N/A".
- The only non‑zero example data is in Annex H(1) (example values totalling 90,000).
