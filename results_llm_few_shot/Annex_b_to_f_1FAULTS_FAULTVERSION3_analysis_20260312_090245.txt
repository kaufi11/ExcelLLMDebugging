2) Cash-flow / balance-sheet reconciliation references (Table 3.0 CF)

- Fault: Several column-T cells (examples: T18, T41 in Table 3.0) are set to =H18 or =H41 instead of summing across the months.

  - e.g. T41 -> =SUM(H41:S41)
