Cell A7 — Error: formula =A6-1 (subtracts 1) so the paragraph numbering resets/duplicates (1,2,1,2,3...) instead of incrementing.  
Solution: change A7 to =A6+1 and fill down the column (or enter correct sequence values).  
Why: the -1 produces wrong numbers and will break any lookups or references that rely on a continuous ascending sequence.
