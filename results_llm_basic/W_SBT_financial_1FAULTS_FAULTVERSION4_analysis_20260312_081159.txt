1) Sheet "In-Kind Contributions" — Personnel costs row (row 23)
- Errors:
  - R23 =Q23 (wrong) — Q23 is not the previous month value, so column R (month 13) becomes blank/ wrong.
  - AF23 =AE23 (wrong) — AF (month 25) should copy month 24, not the year-total AE.

  - Change R23 to =O23
  - Change AF23 to =AC23
- After change the forward-fill chain (S23=T23… and AG23=AF23…) will produce correct month values and the year totals will recalculate correctly.
