D11 — #DIV/0!  
Error: D11 =B11/C11 divides by zero because C11 = A11^$B$6 = 0^CE = 0 (A11 is 0). Also causes H11 (LOG(A11,2)) to be invalid.  
Fix (recommended): change A11 from 0 to 1 (start n ≥ 1).  
Alternate fix: guard formulas, e.g. D11 =IF(C11=0,"",B11/C11) and H11 =IF(A11>0,LOG(A11,2),""). Explanation: many log and division operations are undefined at n=0; Master method checks assume n≥1.
