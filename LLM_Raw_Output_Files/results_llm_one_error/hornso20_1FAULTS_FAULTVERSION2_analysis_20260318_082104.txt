E20-9 (2) — C11: Formula uses C3 instead of C2 (FORMULA =SUM(C4:C6)/C3).  
Error: dividing total indirect costs by direct labor COSTS (C3) instead of by direct labor HOURS (C2).  
Fix: change formula to =SUM(C4:C6)/C2.  
Why: indirect cost hourly rate must be total indirect costs ÷ total direct labor hours (units $/hour). Using C3 gives a wrong rate and all downstream calculations (allocation, job cost, bid) will be incorrect.
