Sheet name: Syracuse, NY

Purpose: Market-share table showing institution-level deposit totals, weighted deposits (banks = full weight; thrifts = 50% weight, with one special multiplier), market share percentages, and HHI contributions for the Syracuse, NY market.

Layout and key ranges
- Title/metadata: B1 = "MARKET SHARE TABLE"; B2 = "Syracuse, NY *"; B4 = "Total Bank and 50% Thrift Deposits"; B5 = date/notes line (January 30, 2004). Notes appear in B39:B47 describing geographic scope and data dates (deposit data as of June 30, 2002; ownership as of Jan 30, 2004).
- Column headers (row 9):
  - A: rank (A10:A33)
  - B: Institution name (B10:B33)
  - C: Type (Bank or Thrift) (C10:C33)
  - D: Number of offices (D10:D33)
  - E: Deposits ($000s) (E10:E33)
  - F: Weighted deposits (F10:F33)
  - G: Market share (%) (G10:G33)
  - H: HHI contribution (H10:H33)
- Totals row: label in B36 = "TOTALS"; D36/E36/F36/G36/H36 contain SUM formulas.

Formulas and logic
- F (weighted deposits): generally =1 * $E(row) for Banks; =0.5 * $E(row) for Thrifts. Exception: F25 (BEACON FEDERAL) uses multiplier 1.21674489270828 * $E25.
- G (market share %): = $F(row) / $F$36 * 100 — each institution’s weighted deposits divided by total weighted deposits (F36) expressed as percent.
- H (HHI contribution): = $G(row)^2 — squares the market-share percentage for each firm (consistent with an HHI computed on percent shares).
- Totals:
  - D36 = SUM(D10:D33) (total offices)
  - E36 = SUM(E10:E33) (sum of deposit dollars in $000s)
  - F36 = SUM(F10:F33) (sum of weighted deposits — denominator for market shares)
  - G36 = SUM(G10:G33) (sum of market shares — should be ~100%)
  - H36 = SUM(H10:H33) (sum of squared percent shares = HHI)

Key values / notable rows
- Top deposit holders (E col, $000s): ALLIED IRISH BANKS 1,615,351; HSBC 1,037,304; KEYCORP 969,798; FLEETBOSTON 903,344; J.P. MORGAN CHASE 644,391.
- Thrift handling: Thrifts are half-weighted in F and include institutions such as FIRST NIAGARA (Thrift, E18 = 571,418 but F18 = 0.5*E18) and several small thrifts.
- Special multiplier: BEACON FEDERAL (row 25) uses 1.21674489270828 * E25 in F25.

Overall structure: a ranked list (rows 10–33) of 24 institutions with deposit figures, automated weighted deposit calculation, market-share percent based on total weighted deposits, and HHI contributions; summary totals in row 36 and explanatory notes below.
