Summary of main errors:

- Wrong formula in G3 for cups produced (uses 45 + growth rate instead of 1 + growth rate).
- Sugar price growth linked to lemon price growth instead of sugar price growth rate.
- Total revenue sums only online sales, missing street sales.

**Solutions:**

1. Fix Income!G3 formula:  
   Change from `=F3*(45+$D$3)` to `=F3*(1+$D$3)` to apply consistent growth rate.

2. Fix sugar price growth formulas (Income!F8 onward):  
   Change references from `$D$7` to `$D$8` so sugar price grows by sugar price growth rate.

3. Fix total revenue formulas (Income!E12 to P12):  
   Change from `=SUM(E10:E10)` to `=E10+E11` (or `=SUM(E10:E11)`) to include both online and street sales.

**Explanation:**

- The cups produced should grow by a percentage, so multiplying by `(1 + growth rate)` is correct. Multiplying by `(45 + growth rate)` inflates values wrongly.  
- Sugar price growth should be based on sugar price growth rate, not lemon price growth rate, to reflect correct cost changes.  
- Total revenue must include all sales channels (online + street) to accurately reflect income.

Fixing these will correct the model's calculations and provide realistic projections.
