Summary of the error
- On the Fall 2002 sheet cell H14 uses COUNT(H10:H13) instead of SUM(H10:H13).
- COUNT returns the number of numeric cells (4), not the subtotal of the amounts, so H14 = 4 and every downstream total in column H (H21, H25) is wrong.

Why this is wrong (short)
- You want the subtotal (sum of Matriculation + Building + Capital Improvement + Student Financial Aid). COUNT only tells you how many numeric cells there are.

Correct formula and how to fix
- Replace H14 with:
  =SUM(H10:H13)
- You can fix quickly by copying the correct formula from G14 (or B14/C14/D14) into H14.
