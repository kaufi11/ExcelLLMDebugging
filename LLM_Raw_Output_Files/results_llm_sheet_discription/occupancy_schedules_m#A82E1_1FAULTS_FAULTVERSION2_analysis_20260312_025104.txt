Summary — I found two types of errors. Fix both to get correct latent-per-person numbers and correct hourly schedules.

1) Wrong formula used for "Latent load per person" (many sheets)
- Problem: the latent-per-person formulas multiply the latent gains (Occupancy!F22,F23) by the sensible-gain cells (Occupancy!F19,F20) and divide by (F19+F20). That is incorrect — the weights must be the summed schedule totals (Occupancy!F38 and Occupancy!F39), same approach as the sensible-per-person calculation.
- Where: appears in the header formulas on multiple sheets (examples: Occ L0 F10, Occ L1-WD F10, Occ L1-DU F10, Occ L2-WD-DU F11, Occ L3 top, etc.). You can search for the pattern (Occupancy!F22*Occupancy!F19 or denominator Occupancy!F19+Occupancy!F20) to find all occurrences.
- Why it’s wrong: it effectively weights latent gains by the sensible-gain numbers instead of by the relative hours each area (living vs bedroom) contributes — so the computed latent BTU/person/hr will be wrong.
- Fix (exact corrected formula):
  Replace any formula of the form:
    =(Occupancy!F22*Occupancy!F19 + Occupancy!F23*Occupancy!F20) / (Occupancy!F19 + Occupancy!F20)
  with:
    =(Occupancy!F22*Occupancy!F38 + Occupancy!F23*Occupancy!F39) / (Occupancy!F38 + Occupancy!F39)
- How to apply: use Excel Find (search for "Occupancy!F22*Occupancy!F19" or "Occupancy!F19+Occupancy!F20") and edit each cell; or use Find/Replace to swap F19→F38 and F20→F39 in those latent formulas only (do not change sensible formulas).

2) Accidental negative signs in one hourly aggregation cell
- Problem: Occ L2-WD-DU, cell F27 has an unintended leading minus:
    =-'Occ L3-WD-DU-LR'!J27-'Occ L3-WD-DU-LR'!K27
  All sibling cells sum the two L3 columns with +, so this one negative makes that hour negative (and breaks totals).
- Why it’s wrong: it subtracts the source columns instead of summing them; occupancy fractions become negative.
- Fix (exact corrected formula):
    ='Occ L3-WD-DU-LR'!J27 + 'Occ L3-WD-DU-LR'!K27
- How to apply: edit Occ L2-WD-DU!F27 and change the minus signs to plus. Also quick check: scan other Occ L2 / L1 / L0 hour formulas for any leading "-" that should be "+".
