Cell: 2002 Report!C13

Error: formula uses =G13*1000000 (multiplies) instead of dividing, producing a huge, incorrect value that breaks totals and downstream calculations.

Fix: change formula to =G13/1000000 (or =G13/1e6). Edit C13, replace * with / and press Enter. This correctly converts pounds to millions of pounds and will restore correct sums and ratios.
