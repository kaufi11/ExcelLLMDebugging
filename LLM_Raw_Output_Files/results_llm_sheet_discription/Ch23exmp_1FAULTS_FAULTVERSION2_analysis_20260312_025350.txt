1) Error: beginning inventory turned negative (wrong sign)
- Problem: Formulas sheet C30 = -B7 (negative). Beginning inventory should be the B7 balance (positive).
- Effect: available-for-sale, COGS, gross margin, taxes and net income get wrong (too small / negative COGS).

- Explanation: beginning inventory is an asset balance carried forward; it should be added, not subtracted, when computing cost of goods available for sale.
