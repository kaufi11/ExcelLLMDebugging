Here are the clear errors I found and simple fixes.

1) Faulty cell: F69
- Error type: Hard-coded / data-entry error
- Problem: F69 contains the constant 2 instead of calculating the long-term debt total like the other liability rows.
- Fix (repair formula): =B69+C69-D69+E69
- Why: All other liability/equity totals in column F use the same structure (B + C - D + E). Replacing the 2 with this formula keeps F69 consistent and makes it update automatically.

2) Faulty cell: C95
- Error type: Range / omission error
- Problem: C95 =SUM(C90:C94) includes C94 (which is the subsidiary credit amount recorded in column D, not column C); that either adds a blank or the wrong cell. The dr total should sum only the debit lines in column C.
- Fix (repair formula): =SUM(C90:C93)
- Why: The basic elimination entry debits are in C90:C93; summing only those cells yields the correct dr total to compare to the credit total in D95.
