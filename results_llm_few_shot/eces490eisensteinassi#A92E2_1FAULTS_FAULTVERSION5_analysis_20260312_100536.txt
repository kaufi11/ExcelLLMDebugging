Faulty cell
- G19

Error type
- Typo / mechanical error

What’s wrong (short)
- G19 contains 468*F19 instead of E19*F19. That single bad entry massively inflates the second-distribution entropy (G46).

Fix (exact)
- Replace G19 with: =E19*F19
- Then fill/copy that corrected formula down G19:G44 (or verify all G20:G44 are E#*F#).

Why this fixes it (short)
- Each entropy term should be p * (-log2 p). G19 must multiply the probability in E19 by its information content in F19. The 468 constant was a typo, producing a huge wrong term and a wrong total H(A).

Optional sanity checks
- After fix, G46 = SUM(G19:G44) should be ~4.26 bits (for the given E distribution). Max H (G47) = -LOG(1/26,2) ≈ 4.700 bits.
