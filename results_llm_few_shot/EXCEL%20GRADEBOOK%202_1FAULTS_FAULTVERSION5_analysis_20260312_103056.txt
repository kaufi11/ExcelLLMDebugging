Findings
- Faulty cell: Q5
- Error type: Formula typo (mechanical)
- What’s wrong: Q5 uses COUNT instead of SUM in the numerator:
  (COUNT(J5:O5)-MIN(J5:O5))/(COUNT(J5:O5)-1)
  That yields a wrong value (it subtracts the minimum from the count).

Fix
- Correct formula for Q5:
  =(SUM(J5:O5)-MIN(J5:O5))/(COUNT(J5:O5)-1)

Why this fixes it (short)
- The intention is "drop the lowest classwork score then average the rest." You must sum the scores, subtract the lowest, then divide by (number of scores − 1). Replacing COUNT with SUM in the numerator implements that logic.

Optional small improvement
- Make the weight references fully absolute in AE formulas (e.g., $C$1, $K$1, $S$1, $Z$1) so copying formulas won’t accidentally change those references.
