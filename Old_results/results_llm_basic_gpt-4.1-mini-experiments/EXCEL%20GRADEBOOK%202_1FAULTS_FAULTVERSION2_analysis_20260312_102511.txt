Error:
In cell Q5, the formula is incorrect:
`= (SUM(J5:O5) + MIN(J5:O5)) / (COUNT(J5:O5) + 1)`
This formula adds the minimum score and increases the count by 1, which is inconsistent with the other rows.

Explanation:
The other rows calculate the average excluding the lowest score by:
`(SUM(range) - MIN(range)) / (COUNT(range) - 1)`
This removes the lowest score before averaging.

Solution:
Change Q5 formula to:
`= (SUM(J5:O5) - MIN(J5:O5)) / (COUNT(J5:O5) - 1)`

Explanation of solution:
This formula correctly calculates the average of the classwork scores excluding the lowest score, consistent with other students' formulas. It sums all scores, subtracts the lowest, and divides by one less than the total count.
