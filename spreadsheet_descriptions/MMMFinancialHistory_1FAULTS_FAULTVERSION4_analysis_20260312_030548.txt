Overview
- Workbook contains 3 sheets: "Select Financial Data", "Statements of Earnings", and "Cash Flow".
- Common layout: years across the top (columns B:K or B:F) and financial line items down the rows. Units: "(add 000, except per share)" (i.e., amounts in thousands; EPS in dollars).

Sheet: Select Financial Data
- Sections:
  - Consolidated Operating Results (Net Sales, Freight, Total revenues, Cost of sales, Cost of operations, Other operating income/expense, Earnings from operations, Interest, Nonoperating items, Earnings before taxes, Taxes, Net earnings).
  - Basic and Diluted EPS (Earnings before extraordinary items, extraordinary items, cumulative effect, net EPS).
  - Cash Dividends per share.
  - Condensed Consolidated Balance Sheet Data (selected current / noncurrent assets, liabilities, shareholders’ equity and totals).
- Key formulas:
  - Totals and subtotals implemented with SUM, e.g., Total revenues B8 = SUM(B6:B7); Cost of operations B11 = SUM(B9:B10).
  - Earnings lines are computed as differences, e.g., B12 = B8 - B11; B14 = B12 - B13.
  - EPS net lines use SUM of component EPS rows.
  - Balance-sheet total B50 = SUM(B44:B49); liabilities + equity B59 = SUM(B51:B58).
