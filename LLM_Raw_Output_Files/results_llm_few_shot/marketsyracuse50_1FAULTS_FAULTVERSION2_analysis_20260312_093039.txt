Faulty cell
- F29

Error type
- Typo / mechanical (wrong multiplier)

What’s wrong (short)
- F29 is =891*$E29 but should be =1*$E29 (or simply =E29). That single "891" inflates the weighted deposits total (F36), so every market share (G10:G33) and the HHI (H36) are wrong.

Fix (quick)
- Change F29 to =E29

Better / safer fix (prevents similar errors)
- Replace the whole F column formula with a rule that uses the Institution Type in column C, for example in F10 and copy down:
  =IF($C10="Thrift",0.5*$E10,$E10)

Why this fixes it (short)
- F holds the weighted deposits (thrift = half of E, bank = full). Fixing F29 restores the correct weighted total (F36). That makes G = F/F36*100 produce correct percentage shares (G36 ≈ 100) and H (=G^2) produce the correct HHI.

How to verify
- After fix: F36 = SUM(F10:F33) will drop to the correct value; SUM(G10:G33) should be ~100 (allowing rounding); H36 will show the corrected HHI.
