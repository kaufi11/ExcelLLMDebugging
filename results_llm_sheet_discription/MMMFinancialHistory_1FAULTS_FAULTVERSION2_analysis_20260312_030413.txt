Found two clear formula errors on the "Select Financial Data" sheet. Short summary and fixes:

1) Wrong sign in Earnings from Operations (1997)
- Location: Select Financial Data, cell H14 (year 1997).

- Why it’s wrong: Row H13 holds "Other operating (income) and expenses, net" (a negative number for income). All other years subtract that row (B14 = B12 - B13 etc). Using + instead of - gives the wrong earnings-from-operations for 1997 and therefore makes H18, H21, H25 and subsequent downstream numbers incorrect.
- Fix: Replace H14 with =H12-H13 and recalc. Check H18/H21/H25 in 1997 will update automatically.

2) Net Earnings references wrong row range (off-by-one)
- Location: Select Financial Data, Net Earnings row (row 25): cells B25 through K25.

- Fix: Change each Net Earnings formula to SUM(<earnings before extraordinary row>:<last adjustment row>) — e.g. B25 = SUM(B21:B24). Recalculate.
