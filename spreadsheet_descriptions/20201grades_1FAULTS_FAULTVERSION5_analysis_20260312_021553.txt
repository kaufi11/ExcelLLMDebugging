Sheet1 (detailed gradebook for SECTION 01 (MW 11-11:50))
- Layout:

  - Column T: Sum of the D:S activity cells for each student (formula in Tn: =SUM(Dn:Sn) shown as T#: FORMULA =SUM(D#:S#)).
  - Column U: Assignment percentage (U# = T#/150*100 for Sheet1 — max 150 shown by formula pattern).
  - Columns V–AO: First research project and the Second research project itemized components:
    - Y:AP (and AF) contain component scores for First Research Project; AF1 header = SUM(Y2:AF2) (per‑row AF values shown).
    - AG:AO components and AO1 = SUM(AG2:AO2) (Second Research Project components aggregate).
    - AQ per student: =SUM(Y#:AP#) (project total); AR = AQ/350*100 (project percent).
  - Columns AV:AZ and BA: Lab / small-group work: AV:AZ are lab subcomponents; BA = SUM(AV:AZ) (lab total).
  - Columns BB:BE and BF: Exams subtotal: BB-BE are exam component columns; BF = SUM(BB:BE).
  - Columns BG:BL, BM: another grouped subtotal (FORMULA =SUM(BG:BL)) — used for another assessment block.
  - Columns BN:BR, BS: another block and subtotal (BS = SUM(BN:BR)).
  - Column BT: Overall points (BT = BA + BF + BM + BS). BU = BT/400*100 (overall percent scaled to 400).
  - Columns BZ, CA, CB, CE, CF, CG: aggregated reporting:
    - BZ = copy of AQ (project total), CA = copy of BT (overall points), CB = copy of T (assignment sum).
    - CE = SUM(BZ:CD) (aggregate current points across categories), CF = CE/830*100 (percent of total course points = 830), CG = letter grade (manually entered per row).
- Key formulas and weights:
  - Per‑student homework sum: Tn = SUM(Dn:Sn); U = T/150*100 (homework maximum = 150 in this sheet).
  - Project totals: AF / AQ sums; AR = AQ/350*100 (project max 350).
  - Overall composite: BT (sum of category subtotals), BU = BT/400*100 (400 = subtotal max used for that percent), CE and CF compute combined total and percent out of 830 total course points.
- Contents: Student rows (A5:A19) include names, IDs, scores for each assignment, project, labs, exams. Many student rows include precomputed subtotals (T, AF/AQ, BA, BF, BM, BS, BT, BU, CE, CF) and letter grade in CG.
