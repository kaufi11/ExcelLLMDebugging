Summary — two kinds of errors found:

1) Wrong formula used to compute the average latent load per person (copy/paste reference error)  
2) A stray negative sign in one hourly aggregation formula

Faulty formulas and fixes

A. Latent load per person — wrong references
- Problem: several sheets compute "Latent load per person" using Occupancy!F19 and Occupancy!F20 (the sensible gains) instead of using the schedule totals (Occupancy!F38 and Occupancy!F39). That produces a meaningless weighted average.
- Where I found this pattern (examples): 
  - Sheet "Occ L0" cell F10
  - Sheet "Occ L1-WD" cell C10
  - Sheet "Occ L1-DU" cell C10
  - Sheet "Occ L2-WD-DU" cell F11 (and similar top-of-sheet latent formulas in other detail-level sheets)
- Why it's wrong: you must weight latent gains (Occupancy!F22, F23) by the schedule totals (how many hours/people are in living vs bedroom), exactly like the sensible calculation did — not by the sensible-gain cells F19/F20.
- Correct formula (replace the bad one with this):
  = (Occupancy!F22 * Occupancy!F38 + Occupancy!F23 * Occupancy!F39) / (Occupancy!F38 + Occupancy!F39)
- Short explanation: this averages latent BTU/person/hr across living and bedroom areas using the same schedule-weighting used for the sensible average.

B. Typo with negative signs
- Faulty cell: Sheet "Occ L2-WD-DU", cell D25 shows:
  =-'Occ L3-WD-DU-LR'!E25-'Occ L3-WD-DU-LR'!F25
- Problem: the formula subtracts both source cells (turning positive occupancy fractions into negative values).
- Fix (make it a sum as intended):
  ='Occ L3-WD-DU-LR'!E25 + 'Occ L3-WD-DU-LR'!F25
- Short explanation: looks like a stray minus sign from a copy/paste. This should combine two fractional schedules (add them), not subtract.
