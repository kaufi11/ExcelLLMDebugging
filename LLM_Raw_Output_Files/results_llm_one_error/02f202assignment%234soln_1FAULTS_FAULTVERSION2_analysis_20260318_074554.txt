J105 — formula uses COUNT(H103:H104) instead of summing the dollar amounts.

Error: COUNT returns 2 (number of cells), not the sum of the ending-inventory dollar amounts, so the LIFO COGS (J106 = J102 - J105) is wrong.

Fix: replace J105 with =SUM(H103:H104). Explanation: you need the total dollar amount of the EI items (sum of H103:H104), not the count of cells; after changing J105 the LIFO COGS (J106) will calculate correctly.
