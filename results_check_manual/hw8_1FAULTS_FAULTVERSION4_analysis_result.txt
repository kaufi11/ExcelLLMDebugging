5) C175 (critical/incident angle)
- Problem: C175 = DEGREES( ATAN((C171/C172)) ). Using ATAN(n_glass / n_water) is incorrect for Snell's-law / critical angle. For the critical angle (glass→water) you need arcsin(n2 / n1) with n2 ≤ n1.
