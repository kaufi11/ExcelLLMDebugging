D52 — Unrestricted: formula incorrectly includes "-D273". Error: D273 (permanently restricted fund) is part of D275 (Total Appropriated), so subtracting D273 here wrongly reduces Unrestricted and causes the balance-sheet mismatch. Fix: change D52 to =D275+D277 (and E52 to =E275+E277). Explanation: Unrestricted net assets should be the appropriated total plus unappropriated amounts — do not subtract the permanently restricted fund.
