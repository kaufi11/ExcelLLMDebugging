Faulty cell(s)
- F24

What’s wrong (simple)
- F24 has =1/$E24 (division). It should copy the deposit amount like the other banks (multiply by 1 or just reference E24). Because F36 (total weighted deposits) sums F10:F33, that wrong value makes F36, all market shares (G10:G33) and the HHI (H10:H33 and H36) incorrect.

Fix(es)
1. Correct the single-cell typo:
   - Change F24 to = $E24  (or =1 * $E24).

2. (Optional but recommended) Make the weighted deposit formula automatic based on Type (so you don't need different constants manually). Put this in F10 and copy down:
   - =IF($C10="Thrift",0.5*$E10,$E10)

3. (Optional formatting) If you prefer G to show true % formatting, use:
   - G10: = $F10 / $F$36 and format as % (instead of multiplying by 100).

Why this fixes it (short)
- The / in F24 produced a tiny number instead of the deposit amount, so the total weighted deposit (F36) was too small and every market share (G) and squared-share (H) derived from it were wrong. Correcting F24 (or using the IF rule) restores the correct totals and market-share calculations.
