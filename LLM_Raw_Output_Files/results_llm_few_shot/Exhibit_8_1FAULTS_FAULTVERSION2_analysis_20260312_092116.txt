Quick checklist to avoid similar problems
- Never hard-code constants into growth formulas — use previous-year cell references.
- Use the percent cells (D16..D18) rather than repeating the number in the formula.
- Make sure formulas point to the same-year totals (E7/E8/E9) rather than to other columns (C7/C8/C9) unless that column actually contains the totals.
- When copying formulas down, check that relative references are correct (or use $ for absolute refs where appropriate).
