Summary of workbook: 2 sheets — "STEP 1 OF 2" (Personal Financial Profile) and "STEP 2 OF 2" (Monthly Financial Plan / Budget). Sheet 2 pulls key totals from Sheet 1 and builds a budget with recommended % ranges.

STEP 1 OF 2 — Personal Financial Profile
- Purpose: collect household income, assets, giving, savings and debts.
- Main sections (vertical layout):
  - Header / title (row 4).
  - WHAT I EARN (columns C–G, rows 9–20): input rows for Job #1, Job #2, Other incomes; tax withheld column G. Key total: E22 = SUM(E11:E20) (gross income total), G22 = SUM(G11:G20) (taxes withheld total).
  - WHAT I OWN (columns I–M, rows 9–20): asset categories (checking, savings, MMKT, CDs, stocks, insurance cash value, home, cars, other property, IRAs, business, etc.). Asset total: M22 = SUM(M9:M20).
  - WHAT I GIVE (rows 25–35): tithes, offerings and other giving lines. Subtotals: G32 = SUM(G29:G31), I32 = SUM(I29:I31), K32 = SUM(K29:K31), M32 = SUM(M29:M31). Totals: E33 = SUM(E29:E30) (sheet shows), I33 = SUM(G32+I32), M33 = SUM(K32+M32), M35 = SUM(E33:M33) — overall giving total in M35.
  - WHAT I SAVE / FUTURE NEEDS (rows 38–45): emergency, future plans, totals. E43 = SUM(E42) and I43 = SUM(I41:I42). M43 = M42 (given) and M45 = SUM(E43:M43) — total saving in M45.
  - WHAT I OWE (rows 48–65): credit/unsecured debts, interest %, monthly payment, total debt section. Debt total: G65 = SUM(G53:G63), M65 = SUM(K53:K63) (total monthly), and C65/F65 show totals; the sheet uses SUM ranges to aggregate debts and monthly payments.
- Common formula type: SUM over contiguous ranges. Many cells are blank input placeholders for amounts, descriptions and dates.

STEP 2 OF 2 — Monthly Financial Plan (Budget)
- Purpose: allocate monthly income into budget categories using recommended % ranges and values pulled from STEP 1.
- Key input / link cells:
  - H6 = 'STEP 1 OF 2'!E22 — total earnings (basis for % calculations).
  - H8 = 'STEP 1 OF 2'!M35 — total giving (GIVING category).
  - H10 = 'STEP 1 OF 2'!M45 — total saving (SAVING category).
  - H12 = 'STEP 1 OF 2'!M65 — total repaying/debt (REPAYING category).
- Structure & categories:
  - Left columns list budget categories (Giving, Saving, Repaying, Taxes, Enjoying, Housing, Transportation, Insurance, etc.).
  - Column F shows percent formulas: e.g., F8 = H8/H6, F10 = H10/H6, F12 = H12/H6, F14 = H14/H6, etc. (these compute the % of earnings each category consumes).
  - Subtotals: housing subtotal H32 = SUM(H19:H31); transportation subtotal H43 = SUM(H35:H42); insurance subtotal H50 = SUM(H46:H49); other subtotals like M18 = SUM(M7:M17) (ENJOYING total), M28 = SUM(M21:M27) (PROF. SERVICES/child care subtotal), M40 = SUM(M31:M39) (ENTERTAINMENT subtotal).
  - Budget totals: M43 = H32+H43+H50+M18+M28+M40 (TOTAL EXPENSES), M45 = H16 (NET INCOME/ENJOYING carried through), M47 = M45 - M43 (Difference / surplus or shortfall).
  - Several K-column formulas compute proportional shares (e.g., K6 = M18/H6, K20 = M28/H6, K30 = M40/H6) to assess category ratios vs income.
- Common formula types: links to STEP 1 totals, SUM of category ranges, ratio calculations Hx/H6 to get percent of earnings.

Overall flow and key values
- Data-entry oriented: STEP 1 collects raw financial picture (income, assets, giving, saving, debt).
- STEP 2 consumes STEP 1 totals to build a monthly budget, computes category percentages and subtotals and returns a net difference (M47) to show surplus/shortfall.
- Primary cross-sheet links to note: H6 ← E22 (Step1 earnings), H8 ← M35 (giving), H10 ← M45 (saving), H12 ← M65 (debt).
- Primary aggregation formulas: many SUM(range) formulas to total rows/columns; percent formulas =Hx/H6; final expense aggregation M43 sums major category subtotals.
