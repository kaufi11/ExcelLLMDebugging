- E: numeric subscription rates (Euros). Several rates show long decimal fractions (likely from conversions).
- F: numeric subscription counts — in the provided extract these appear mostly empty (not shown), so most G cells will evaluate to zero unless F is populated.
- G: numeric totals (calculated). In the file these are formulas; displayed here as textual "FORMULA =E*F".
- H: numeric postage charges or text ("incl."), varies by journal.

- Most G totals depend on column F (Subs.), but F values are not included in the provided snippet — so G and the grand total G407 will be zero/uncomputed until F is populated.

- Decimal precision: many E values include long decimals (may be rounding artifacts); consider formatting E and G to 2 decimal places (currency).
