I checked the three sheets and found a few real errors and a couple of risky/misleading formulas. Short summary, explanation, and exact fixes below.

1) Wrong formula in Seq Production — "Other Activities" grand total (big error)
- Problem: G21 is set to =COUNT(G19:G20). That returns 0/1/2 (a count), not the dollar sum of "Other Activities". This makes B23 (one-year total = B21 + D21 + G21) incorrect.
- Fix: change G21 to sum the dollar cells:
  - Replace with: =SUM(G19:G20)
- Why: G19 and G20 hold the Other Activities direct and indirect dollar amounts; you must SUM them to get the grand $ total for Other Activities.

2) Missing / inconsistent "Total Direct Costs" in Tech Dev & Bioinformatics Infrastructure (layout/formula mismatch)
- Problem: On Tech Dev sheet A15 is labeled "Total Direct Costs" but B15 has no formula. Instead B17 = SUM(B9:B16) is being used as the "Total Tech Dev Costs". This is confusing and can double-count if B15 is later filled manually. Same pattern exists for the Infrastructure section on the Bioinformatics sheet: total/direct rows are inconsistent.
- Fix (clear, standard approach):
  - For Tech Dev:
    - Put in B15 (Total Direct Costs): =SUM(B9:B14)
    - Put in B17 (Total Tech Dev Costs): =B15 + B16
  - For Bioinformatics — Infrastructure section:
    - Put in B15 (Total Direct Costs for Infrastructure): =SUM(B9:B14)
    - Put in B17 (Infrastructure Cost Totals): =B15 + B16
- Why: keep direct and indirect separated and then add them for the section total. This avoids ambiguity and accidental double-counting.

3) Division-by-zero risk in percent formulas (Tech Dev, Bioinformatics, possibly Seq Production)
- Problem: Several percent formulas divide by a total cell that can be zero (B6 on Tech Dev & Bioinformatics is initialized to 0; B21/D21 may be 0). That produces #DIV/0! or confusing output.
- Fix: wrap these % formulas with an IF to avoid divide-by-zero and show blank (or 0). Example replacements:
  - Tech Dev B18: =IF($B$6=0,"",B17/$B$6)
  - Bioinformatics B18: =IF($B$6=0,"",B17/$B$6)
  - Bioinformatics B31: =IF($B$6=0,"",B30/$B$6)
  - Bioinformatics B44: =IF($B$6=0,"",B43/$B$6)
  - Seq Production percent cells that divide by B21/D21 (e.g., C13, E13, H13, C19 etc.): =IF($B$21=0,"",B13/$B$21) (adjust absolute ref to the correct total column as already used)
- Why: prevents #DIV/0! and makes the sheet cleaner until totals are filled.

4) Hard-coded 1’s for percent total cells (minor/optional)
- Observation: C21, E21, H21 are hard-coded to 1. That is OK (shows 100%) but better practice is to compute them (e.g., =B21/B21) so they always reflect the actual totals. Optional cleanup.
