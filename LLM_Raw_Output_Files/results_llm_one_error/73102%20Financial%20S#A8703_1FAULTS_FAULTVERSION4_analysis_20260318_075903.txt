D34 — Error: formula =D30+D32 references the wrong property total (D30 sums empty/unused rows).  
Fix: replace with =D162+D170 (and update E34 to =E162+E170).  
Why: the detailed property totals are in D162 and accumulated depreciation is in D170; using D30 (blank) omits the property assets and misstates net property/equipment, which can make totals and the balance sheet wrong.
